# X-ray

X-ray; **x-ray**. Either form is correct, although the first has predominated in both AmE and BrE in print sources since the late 1800s—the margin between the two being much less in AmE. Although *W11* suggests that the term is hyphenated as an adjective and verb \(*X-ray*\) but not as a noun \(*X ray*\), most other dictionaries hyphenate the term in all parts of speech. That makes good sense, and it's an easy rule to follow.

 - Current ratio in print \(*X-ray* vs. *x-ray*\): 3:1
