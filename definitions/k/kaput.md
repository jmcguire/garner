# kaput

kaput /kә-**pʊt**/ \(= broken to the point of either malfunctioning or not functioning at all\), a German loanword dating in English from the late 1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled. The spelling of the German etymon, *\*kaputt*, is a variant in English.

 - Current ratio in print \(*kaput* vs. \**kaputt*\): 6:1
