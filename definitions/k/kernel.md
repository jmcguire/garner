# kernel

kernel. As a verb dating from the 1400s, this word means either "to envelop somewhat as a nut or seed is enveloped" or "to ripen as a nut or seed does inside the shell." In BrE, it has always been inflected *kernelled* and *kernelling*. In AmE, the predominant inflections have been *kerneled* and *kerneling* since about 1900. See spelling \(c\).
