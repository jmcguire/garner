# know > knew > known

know > knew > known. So inflected. See irregular verbs.

Through careless error, *know* is sometimes written *now*—e.g.:

 - "Gempler said he didn't *now* \[read *know*\] why the union produced the report." Hannelore Sudermann, "Teamsters Attack Apple Industry over Core Issues," *Spokesman-Rev.* \(Spokane\), 23 July 1997, at A10.

 - "Did you *now* \[read *know*\] that in the 1950s Vancouver had more neon signs than Las Vegas?" Cheryl Chan, "Family Day 2022," *Postmedia News* \(Can.\), 17 Feb. 2022, Local News §.
