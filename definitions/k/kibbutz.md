# kibbutz

kibbutz /ki-**bʊtz**/ \(= a communal farm in Israel where many people live and work together\) forms the Hebrew plural *kibbutzim* across World Englishes—not *\*kibbutzes*. A member of a *kibbutz* is known as a *kibbutznik*.

 - Current ratio in print \(*kibbutzim* vs. *\*kibbutzes*\): 55:1
