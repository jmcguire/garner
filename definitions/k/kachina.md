# kachina

kachina \(= \[1\] one of the many deified ancestral spirits in the mythology of the Hopi people; \[2\] a dancer who depicts such a deity or spirit; \[3\] the mask worn by such a dancer; or \[4\] a doll representing the deity or spirit\), a term dating in English from the late 1800s, has been predominantly so spelled since about 1920. \**Katcina* and \**katchina* are variants.

 - Current ratio in print \(*kachina* vs. \**katcina* vs. \**katchina*\): 45:1:1
