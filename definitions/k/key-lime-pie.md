# key lime \(pie\)

key lime \(pie\). So written—predominantly lowercase since about 2000. The small, highly acidic limes draw their name from the Florida Keys, where they were naturalized. But the word *key*, in reference to limes, has lost its proper-name character.

 - Current ratio in print \(*of key lime pie* vs. *of Key lime pie* vs. *of Key Lime pie*\): 14:4:1
