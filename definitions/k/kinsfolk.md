# kinsfolk

kinsfolk \(= relatives\), a compound dating from the 1400s, is the traditional and still the predominant form. Although the variant plurals *kinfolk* and \**kinfolks* surged for many decades after 1940 and now rival *kinsfolk*, they have not enjoyed sustained predominance.

Current ratio in print \(*his kinsfolk* vs. *his kinfolk* vs. \**his kinfolks*\): 13:5:1
Current ratio in print \(*all the kinsfolk* vs. *all the kinfolk* vs. \**all the kinfolks*\): 2.2:1.9:1
