# karaoke

karaoke, a Japanese loanword literally meaning "empty orchestra" and dating in English from the 1970s, denotes a form of entertainment involving singing along to a prerecorded backing tape. Although the Japanese pronunciation of the word is close to /kah-rah-**oh**-kay/, the established pronunciation in English is /ka-ree-**oh**-kee/ or /kә-**roh**-kee/.
