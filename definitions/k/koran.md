# Koran

Koran; **Qur'an**; **Quran**. These English translations of the Arabic name for Islam's holy book are phonetic. Although *Koran* long predominated in AmE and BrE, *Qur'an* and *Quran* are now predominant in World English print sources. The second syllable is stressed: /kә-**rahn**/ or /kә-**ran**/.
