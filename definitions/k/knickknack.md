# knickknack

knickknack is so spelled, with a *-kk-* in the middle. Some writers mistakenly omit one of them—e.g.: "Eddy's daffy, disintegrating mother gets caught climbing in windows or pinching *knicknacks* \[read *knickknacks*\]." Arion Berger, "Absolutely Frightening," *Village Voice*, 13 June 1995, at 45. *\*Nicknack* is a variant spelling.
