# kebab

kebab; **\*kabob**; **\*kabab**; **\*kebob**; **\*cabob**. The first of these spellings—the one closest to the Turkish etymon—had become standard by the early 1940s. The others are variants.

Current ratio in print \(*kebab* vs. \**kabob* vs. \**kabab* vs. \**kebob* vs. \**cabob*\): 458:38:14:6:1
