# kalamata

kalamata /kah-lә-**maht**-ә/ \(= a Greek-style black olive cured in brine\), from a Greek place name used as a word dating in English from the 1970s, has been predominantly so spelled since the early 1990s. Before that, the variant \**calamata* was common.

Current ratio in print \(*kalamata* vs. \**calamata*\): 108:1
