# kiltie

kiltie \(= \[1\] someone who wears a kilt—esp. the thick tartan skirt-like garment traditionally worn by Scotsmen; or \[2\] a decorative tuft of leather or leatherlike material with serrated ends, traditionally worn atop golf shoes\) is predominantly so spelled. In sense 1, *kilty* is a variant spelling that appears almost as frequently as *kiltie*.

Current ratio in print \(*kiltie* vs. *kilty*\): 1.6:1
