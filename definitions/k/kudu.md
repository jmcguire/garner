# kudu

kudu \(the large grayish-brown African antelope with long, twisted horns\), an Afrikaans loanword dating in English from the late 1700s, has been predominantly so spelled since about 1910. Before that, the variant \**koodoo* was the usual spelling. The word has a zero plural <several kudu>.

 - Current ratio in print \(*kudu* vs. \**koodoo*\): 8:1
