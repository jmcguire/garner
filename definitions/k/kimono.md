# kimono

kimono \(= a long, loose Japanese robe\) is frequently misspelled *\*kimona*—e.g.: "Over her fireplace in Maryland hangs a portrait of her in a *kimona* \[read *kimono*\] from the year she lived in Japan." Patricia Meisol, "Migration Pattern," *Baltimore Sun*, 28 Oct. 1997, at E1. The proper pronunciation is /ki-**moh**-noh/; the common but inferior pronunciation /ki-**moh**-nә/ probably accounts for the misspelling.

Language-Change Index

*kimono* misspelled *\*kimona*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*kimono* vs. \**kimona*\): 99:1
