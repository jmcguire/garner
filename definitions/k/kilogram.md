# kilogram

kilogram \(the metric unit for measuring weight, equal to about 2.205 pounds\) has been predominantly so spelled for a long time—since about 1875 in AmE and about 1920 in BrE. The earlier spelling, \**kilogramme*, has been in a steady decline since 1900.

Current ratio in print \(*kilogram* vs. \**kilogramme*\): 24:1
