# Parcheesi

Parcheesi /pahr-**cheez**-ee/ is the trademarked name of a mostly children's board game played with dice and counters—an American version of the East Indian game *pachisi*, which dates at least as far back as the 1500s. Only in the late 1960s did the trademarked *Parcheesi* overtake *pachisi* in frequency of appearance in English-language print sources. Among the variant spellings are \**parchesi* and \**parchisi*.

 - Current ratio in print (*Parcheesi* vs. *pachisi* vs. \**parchesi* vs. \**parchisi*): 96:40:11:1
