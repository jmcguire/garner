# pannier

pannier /**pan**-yәr/ \(= \[1\] a saddlebag; \[2\] a basket carried on one's back; or \[3\] a hoop or other device formerly used to expand a woman's skirt laterally\) is a French loanword dating in English from the 1200s. Since the early 1600s, it has been predominantly spelled with the -*nn*-. The spelling \**panier* is a variant. See gallicisms.

 - Current ratio in print \(*pannier* vs. \**panier*\): 4:1
