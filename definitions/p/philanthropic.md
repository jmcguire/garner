# philanthropic

philanthropic /fil-әn-**throp**-ik/ (= having the habit or purpose of giving money and help to those in need), a Greek loanword dating in English from the mid-1600s, has always been the standard adjective. **Philanthropical* is a needless variant. See -ic/-ical.

 - Current ratio in print (*philanthropic* vs. \**philanthropical*): 127:1
