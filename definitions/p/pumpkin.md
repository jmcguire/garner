# pumpkin

pumpkin is often misspelled *\*pumkin*—e.g.: "It will begin with ravioli stuffed with pureed *pumkin* \[read *pumpkin*\] in a nutmeg cream." Chris Sherman, "Gala Night to Have Grand Menu Series," *St. Petersburg Times* (Fla.), 5 Nov. 1996, at B2. The word is pronounced /**pәmp**-kin/, not /**pәng**-kin/—unless, as Elster says, "you're joking or using it as a term of endearment for a child" (*BBBM* at 399).
