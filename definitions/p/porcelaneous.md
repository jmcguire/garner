# porcelaneous

porcelaneous \(= of, relating to, or involving porcelain\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the late 1700s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since about 1937 and in BrE since 2015. Among the variant forms are \**porcellaneous*, \**porcelainous*, \**porcelanic*, \**porcellanic*, \**porcelaineous*, and \**porcelainic*.
