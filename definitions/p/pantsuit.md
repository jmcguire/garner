# pantsuit

pantsuit \(= a woman's suit consisting of a jacket and matching slacks\), a compound dating from the early 1960s, has been predominantly so written since the late 1970s. Before that, the two-word form \**pants suit* was prevalent. In BrE, the two-word *pant suit* prevails.

 - Current ratio in print \(*pantsuit* vs. \**pants suit* vs. \**pant suit*\): 12:1.9:1
