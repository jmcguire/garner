# predestine

predestine, vb.; **\*predestinate**, vb. Although Merriam-Webster dictionaries might lead you to believe otherwise, *predestine* has been the standard form since the mid-1800s. *\*Predestinate*, as a verb, is a needless variant. But the adjective by the same spelling is a synonym of *predestined*: it is pronounced /pri-**des**-ti-nәt/. It might be considered a needless variant as well. But none of these pronouncements were foreordained.

 - Current ratio in print \(*predestined* vs. *\*predestinated*\): 10:1
