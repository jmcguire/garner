# prolifically

prolifically is the adverb corresponding to *prolific*, adj. But some writers misspell it *\*prolificly*, maybe on the analogy of *publicly*—e.g.: "No wonder I wrote so easily, and *prolificly* \[read *prolifically*\]." Jeanne Crownover, "She'd Be a Writer . . . Except She Has Too Much Tranquility," *Sacramento Bee*, 1 Jan. 1995, Scene §, at 6. See -ic/-ical.

## Language-Change Index

 - *prolifically* misspelled *\*prolificly*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*prolifically* vs. \**prolificly*): 960:1
