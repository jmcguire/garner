# paralipsis

paralipsis; **\*paralepsis**; **\*paraleipsis**. Although the more classical spelling *\*paraleipsis* predominated until about 1960, *paralipsis* has become standard among rhetoricians since that time. *\*Paralepsis* is the second most common spelling. Let's not fully discuss the degree to which *\*paraleipsis* has fallen into disfavor. For the meaning of this rare term, see p. 1019.

Current ratio in print \(*paralipsis* vs. \**paralepsis* vs. \**paraleipsis*\): 5:2.2:1
