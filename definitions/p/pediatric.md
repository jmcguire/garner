# pediatric

pediatric; **podiatric**. These words both emerged about 1850. *Pediatric* /pee-dee-**a**-trik/ = of, relating to, or involving the branch of medicine dealing with children. \(A common BrE spelling is *paediatric*.\) *Podiatric* /poh-dee-**a**-trik/ = of, relating to, or involving the branch of medicine dealing with the human foot.
