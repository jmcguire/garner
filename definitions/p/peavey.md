# peavey

peavey \(= a lumber worker's heavy lever with a pointed metal tip and a hinged hook at the end\), an Americanism dating from about 1870, derives from the name of the blacksmith who invented it: Joseph Peavey. The variant spelling \**peavy* is essentially a misspelling of the eponymous source of the word.

Current ratio in print \(*peavey* vs. \**peavy*\): 2:1
