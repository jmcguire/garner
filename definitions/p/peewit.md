# peewit

peewit \(= any of various birds, esp. the lapwing or the tyrant flycatcher\), an onomatopoeic word after the bird's call, dates from the 1500s. Until about 1840, the predominant spelling was \**pewit*, but since that time it has been *peewit*. Even so, *lapwing* is much more common.

 - Current ratio in print \(*peewit* vs. \**pewit*\): 8:1
