# parti-colored

parti-colored (= many-colored, multihued) is the correct form. But *\*party-colored* is a fairly common misspelling, perhaps from the festiveness suggested by the word's meaning—e.g.: "The lawn grows lush and is bordered by . . . groupings of annuals such as *party-colored* \[read *parti-colored*\] seed dahlias." Kym Pokorny, "Presidential Bouquet," *Oregonian* (Portland), 18 Aug. 1996, at L11. (See etymology (d).) Increasingly the word is made solid: *particolored*.

*\*Parti-color*, adj., is a needless variant.

## Language-Change Index

 - *parti-colored* misspelled *\*party-colored*: Stage 3
 - Current ratio in print (*parti-colored* vs. \**party-colored*): 4:1
 - Current ratio in print (*parti-colored* vs. *particolored*): 2:1
