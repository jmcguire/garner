# philtre

philtre (= a love potion or love charm), a French loanword dating in English from the 1500s, is predominantly so spelled in all varieties of English. **Philter* is a variant often mistakenly recorded in American dictionaries as the primary AmE spelling.

 - Current ratio in print (*philtre* vs. \**philter* in BrE): 2.3:1
 - Current ratio in print (\**philter* vs. *philtre* in AmE): 1.9:1
