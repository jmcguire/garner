# piscatorial

piscatorial \(= of, relating to, or involving fish or fishing\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the early 1600s, overtook the slightly older word **piscatory* in frequency of appearance in the 1860s. Today the latter word might be classed as a needless variant.

Current ratio in print \(*piscatorial* vs. \**piscatory*\): 1.8:1
