# petit four

petit four /**pet**-ee **for**/ \[Fr. "small oven"\] \(= a small sweet cake or biscuit cut from pound, sponge, or some similar cake and then frosted\), a French loan dating in English from the mid-1800s, predominantly keeps its French plural \(though it is not italicized\): *petits fours* /**pet**-ee **forz**/, not *petit* *fours*. See plurals \(b\); see also loanwords \(a\).

Current ratio in print \(*petits fours* vs. *petit fours*\): 1.3:1
