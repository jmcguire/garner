# parenthesis

parenthesis /pә-**ren**-thә-sis/, the singular noun, forms the plural *parentheses* /pә-**ren**-thә-seez/. But because the plural is more common than the singular, some speakers use the mistaken back-formation *\*parenthese* \(wrongly pronounced /pә-**ren**-thә-see/\). The error occurs occasionally in writing as well as in speech.

## Language-Change Index

*\*parenthese* as false singular in place of *parenthesis*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*parenthesis* vs. *\*parenthese*\): 291:1
