# pointillism

pointillism \(= a style of painting that uses small spots of color all over the canvas as opposed to brushstrokes\) is pronounced mostly in an anglicized way in AmE: /**poyn**-ti-liz-әm/—although /**pwan**-ti-liz-әm/ is also current and predominates in BrE. The fully Frenchified /**pwan**-tee-yiz-әm/ is an affectation in English-speaking contexts.
