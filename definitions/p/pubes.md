# pubes

pubes \(= \[1\] a person's external genitals; or \[2\] pubic hair\) traditionally has two syllables /**pyoo**-beez/. It's often pronounced /pyoobz/, and many people declare that they've never even heard the two-syllable pronunciation. Today in common speech it is usually /pyoobz/.

## Language-Change Index

 - *pubes* /**pyoo**-beez/ mispronounced /pyoobz/: Stage 4
