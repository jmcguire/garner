# partisan

partisan; **\*partizan**. The first is the standard spelling in both AmE and BrE. Although the term denotes "someone who takes part or sides with another," it has connotations of a prejudiced, unreasoning, or blindly fanatical adherent.

*Partisan* is pronounced /**pahr**-ti-zәn/ in AmE and /pah-ti-**zan**/ in BrE.

Current ratio in print \(*partisan* vs. \**partizan*\): 134:1
