# peddler

peddler \(= an itinerant seller\) has been the standard AmE spelling since about 1860. *Pedlar* is standard BrE, *\*pedler* being a chiefly BrE variant.

The occupation of a *peddler* is known as *pedlary* in both AmE and BrE \(\**peddlery* and \**pedlery* being variants\).

Current ratio in print \(*peddler* vs. *pedlar* vs. \**pedler* in AmE\): 27:4:1   
Current ratio in print \(*pedlar* vs. *peddler* vs. \**pedler* in BrE\): 45:40:1   
Current ratio in print \(*pedlary* vs. \**peddlery* vs. \**pedlery* in AmE\): 12:1.8:1   
Current ratio in print \(*pedlary* vs. \**peddlery* vs. \**pedlery* in BrE\): 5:1.7:1
