# pierogi

pierogi /pә-**roh**-jee/ \(= a small pastry turnover with a savory filling of meat, cheese, vegetables, etc.\), a Russian/Polish loanword dating in English from the early 1800s, has been predominantly so spelled since about 1990. Before that, the variants *pirogi* and *pirozhki* were equally common.

Current ratio in print \(*pierogi* vs. *pirozhki* vs. *pirogi*\): 12:3:1
