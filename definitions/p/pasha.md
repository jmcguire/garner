# pasha

pasha /**pah**-shә/ or, in BrE, /**pash**-ә/ \(= \[1\] a high-ranking man in Turkey; or \[2\] such a man's office\), a Turkish loanword dating in English from the 1600s, has been predominantly so spelled since about 1830. \**Bashaw* and \**basha* are variants.

2018 ratio in print \(*Turkish pasha* vs. *Turkish \*bashaw* vs. *Turkish \*basha*\): 424:44:1
