# papilloma

papilloma \(= a benign tumor of the skin, such as a wart or corn, or of a mucous membrane\), a Latin/Greek hybrid dating in English from the mid-1800s, predominantly forms the anglicized plural *papillomas*. Up to the 1930s, the classical plural \**papillomata* was standard.

 - Current ratio in print \(*papillomas* vs. \**papillomata*\): 12:1
