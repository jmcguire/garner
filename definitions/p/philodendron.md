# philodendron

philodendron \(= any of various tropical American vines of the arum family, usu. having showy, heart-shaped leaves and commonly used as houseplants\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the 1840s, has always predominantly made the anglicized plural *philodendrons*. The classical plural \**philodendra* is a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*philodendrons* vs. \**philodendra*\): 24:1
