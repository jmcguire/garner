# pledgor

pledgor; **pledger**; **\*pledgeor**. The most logical spelling is *pledger*, not *pledgor* or *\*pledgeor*—and *pledger* is 20 times as common as *pledgor* in AmE journalistic writing. But in legal contexts, *pledgor* is 50 times as common as *pledger*, largely because it is the regular correlative of *pledgee*. That spelling also predominates in books printed throughout the world. See spelling \(d\).

Current ratio in print \(*pledgor* vs. *pledger* vs. *\*pledgeor* in World Englishes\): 46:21:1
