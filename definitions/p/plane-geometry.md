# plane geometry

plane geometry —so written—is sometimes mistakenly made *\*plain geometry*. E.g.: "Unlike most suckers, I never had to take *plain* \[read *plane*\] geometry or trig." Gary Dunford, "Blame It on X-Factor, Eh," *Toronto Sun*, 15 May 1997, at 6.

## Language-Change Index

 - *\*plain geometry* for *plane geometry*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print \(*plane geometry* vs. *\*plain geometry*\): 35:1
