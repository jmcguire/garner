# participatory

participatory \(= involving participation; allowing concerned parties to take part\), an adjective dating from the late 1800s, didn't oust its older rival, *\*participative*, until the 1960s. Now *\*participative*, along with *\*participational*, is a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*participatory* vs. \**participative* vs. \**participational*\): 953:110:1
