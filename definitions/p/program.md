# program

program; **programme**. The first is the established AmE spelling; the second is BrE (though a close rivalry has developed since the early 1960s). *Program* is standard BrE in reference to computer programs.

The word is best pronounced /**proh**-gram/ in AmE and BrE alike—but /**proh**-grәm/, with an anomalous schwa sound, is widespread and acceptable today.

 - Current ratio in print (*training program* vs. *training programme* in AmE): 18:1
 - Current ratio in print (*training programme* vs. *training program* in BrE): 1.5:1
 - Current ratio in print (*computer program* vs. *computer programme* in World Englishes): 27:1
