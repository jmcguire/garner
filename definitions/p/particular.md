# particular

particular \(= of, relating to, or involving a single person or thing\) is pronounced /pәr-**tik**-yә-lәr/—not /pә-**tik**-lәr/. The adverb *particularly* has five distinct syllables: /pәr-**tik**-yә-lәr-lee/—though in dialect it can be shortened to three /pә-**tik**-lee/. For more on the adverb, see the next entry.
