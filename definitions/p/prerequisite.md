# prerequisite

prerequisite.

**A. And *requisite*.** Rarely is *prerequisite* used with the degree of punctilio that Eric Partridge prescribed: "Properly, a *prerequisite* has to be obtained or fulfilled before a *requisite* can be attended to. In short, *prerequisite* is rarely permissible." "Vigilans" \[Eric Partridge\], *Chamber of Horrors* 114 \(1952\). Probably it is more accurate to say that *prerequisite* includes a built-in time element, whereas *requisite* does not.
