# pedestal

pedestal \(= a base or foundation\) is sometimes, in a wild error, misused for *pestle* \(= a tool for pounding or grinding substances in a mortar\)—e.g.: "Not at all pretty is the way Oklahoma State plays, all stumble and grunt, a mortar-and-*pedestal* \[read *pestle*\] kind of basketball." Bernie Lincicome, "These Cowboys Don't Look Capable of Roping a Mule," *Austin Am.-Statesman*, 28 Mar. 1995, at E5.
