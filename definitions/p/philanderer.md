# philanderer

philanderer /fi-**land**-ә-rәr/ (= a man who engages in brief love affairs) is the standard form. *\*Philander*, n., is a needless variant. Of course, *philander* is the invariable verb <after a life of philandering, he had little to show for it but nine illegitimate children whom he hardly knew>.

 - Current ratio in print (*a philanderer* vs. *\*a philander*): 115:1
