# progress

progress, n. & vb. The noun *progress* (= \[1\] slow or steady movement in a determinate direction; \[2\] advancement toward an objective; or \[3\] gradual betterment) is pronounced /**prah**-gres/ in AmE and /**proh**-gres/ in BrE. The verb *progress* (= \[1\] to move forward; \[2\] to move to a better or more advanced phase; or \[3\] to continue) is pronounced /prә-**gres**/ in AmE and BrE alike.
