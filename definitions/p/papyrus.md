# papyrus

papyrus /pa-**pī**-rәs/ \(= \[1\] a tall sedge native to northern Africa; \[2\] the pith of this plant made into paper-like writing material; or \[3\] a manuscript written on this material\) overwhelmingly makes the plural *papyri* /pa-**pī**-rī/—not *papyruses*.

 - Current ratio in print \(*papyri* vs. *papyruses*\): 104:1
