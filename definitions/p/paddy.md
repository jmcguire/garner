# paddy

paddy \(= a watery field in which rice is grown\) is the standard spelling. \**Padi* and \**paddie* are variant forms. The term derives from the Malay word *padi* "rice," so a *paddy field* \(either not a redundancy or an idiomatically acceptable one\) is just a field of rice.

Current ratio in print \(*rice paddy* vs. \**rice padi* vs. \**rice paddie*\): 1,310:3:1
