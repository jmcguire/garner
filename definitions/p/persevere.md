# persevere

persevere is pronounced /pәr-sә-**veer**/. Because the word is frequently a victim of the intrusive *r* \(/pәr-sәr-**veer**/\), it is often misspelled *\*perservere*. The corresponding noun, *perseverance* /pәr-sә-**veer**-әn\[t\]s/, has been similarly victimized. See pronunciation \(b\), \(c\) & spelling \(a\).
