# polygraph

polygraph. Although *polygraph* is pronounced /**pol**-ee-graf/, the related forms are pronounced quite differently: *polygraphy* /pә-**lig**-rә-fee/, *polygrapher* /pә-**lig**-rә-fәr/, *polygraphic* /pol-ee-**graf**-ik/. The pronunciations are analogous to those involving *photograph* and its cognates.
