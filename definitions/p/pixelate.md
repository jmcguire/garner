# pixelate

pixelate; **\*pixilate**; **\*pixellate**; **\*pixillate**. The first is the standard verb meaning "to divide \(an image\) into pixels \[minute areas of illumination on a display screen\], esp. for display or storage in a digital format." The other spellings are variants. The single-*l* *pixelated* predominates in AmE and BrE alike.

 - Current ratio in print \(*pixelate* vs. \**pixilate* vs. \**pixellate* vs. \**pixillate*\): 83:15:5:1
