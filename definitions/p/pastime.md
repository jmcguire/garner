# pastime

pastime is sometimes misspelled *\*pasttime.* The misspelling derives from a misunderstanding of the word's origin: *pastime* derives from *pass* (v.t.) \+ *time*, not *past* \+ *time*.

## Language-Change Index

 - *pastime* misspelled *\*pasttime*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*pastime* vs. \**pasttime*): 332:1
