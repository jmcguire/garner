# pinochle

pinochle; \***pinocle**; **\*penuchle**; **\*penuckle**. The first has been the standard spelling for the name of the card game since it began in the mid-1800s. The others are variants.

 - Current ratio in print (*pinochle* vs. \**pinocle* vs. \**penuchle* vs. \**penuckle*): 360:9:1.9:1
