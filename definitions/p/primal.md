# primal

primal; **primordial**; **primeval**; **\*primaeval**. All these words essentially mean "first in time; original." But in the jargon of scientists, each is usually confined to a distinct scientific context: *primal* to psychology <primal therapy>, *primordial* to biology <primordial soup>, and *primeval* to geology or paleontology <primeval forests>. *\*Primaeval* is a chiefly BrE variant of *primeval*; even the *OED* gives priority to the latter now.

Current ratio in print \(*primeval* vs. *\*primaeval*\): 31:1
