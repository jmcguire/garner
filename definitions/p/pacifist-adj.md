# pacifist, adj

pacifist, adj.; **pacifistic**. *Pacifist* \(= believing that wars are wrong and refusing to use violence\) is the usual adjective when a noun follows. *Pacifistic* is a variant used especially as a predicate adjective <those people are pacifistic>. Of course, *pacifist* is also a noun denoting someone with pacifist attitudes or views.
