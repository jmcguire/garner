# paterfamilias

paterfamilias \(= \[1\] the male head of a household; \[2\] a patriarch; or \[3\] a male leader of a movement, campaign, discipline, etc.\) is pronounced /pat-әr-fә-**mil**-ee-әs/. Pl. *patresfamilias*—the anglicized variant *\*paterfamiliases* being extremely uncommon. See plurals \(a\), \(b\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*patresfamilias* vs. \**paterfamiliases*\): 34:1
