# past

past. This word occurs in many redundant phrases, such as *\*past history*, *\*past track record*, and *\*past experience*, in which the noun denotes something that by its very nature is rooted in the past. See redundancy. For a misuse, see **passed**.

## Language-Change Index

 - *past* in redundancies such as *\*past history*, *\*past experience*: Stage 3
