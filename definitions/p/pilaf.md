# pilaf

pilaf; **pilau**; **\*pilaff**; **\*pilaw**. What do we call the dish consisting of rice, vegetables, and sometimes meat? In AmE, it's *pilaf* \(/**pee**-lahf/—not /pi-**lahf**/\). In BrE, it's traditionally *pilau* /**pee**-low/—though since the 1960s *pilaf* has come into currency in BrE and since the mid-1990s seems to have been steadily ousting *pilau*. *\*Pilaw* and *\*pilaff* are variant spellings.

 - Current ratio in print \(*pilaf* vs. *pilau* vs. *\*pilaff* vs. *\*pilaw*\): 55:14:4:1
