# pixie

pixie (= a fairy or elf) has been the standard spelling since the 1920s. *\*Pixy* is a variant.

The predominant spelling of the corresponding adjective has been *pixieish*—from 1985 in BrE and from 1974 in AmE. The older form \**pixyish* is now just a variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*little pixie* vs. *\*little pixy*): 27:1
 - Current ratio in print (*pixieish* vs. \**pixyish*): 1.1:1
