# proscenium

proscenium \(= the front part of a stage, esp. in front of the curtain\) has predominantly formed the plural *prosceniums* across World Englishes since about 1920. Before that, the Latinate plural *proscenia* \(for this ultimately Greek term\) was standard. See plurals \(b\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*prosceniums* vs. *proscenia*\): 1.1:1
