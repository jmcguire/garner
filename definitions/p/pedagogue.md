# pedagogue

pedagogue \(= a teacher, often a pedantic one\) is the standard spelling. *\*Pedagog* is a variant. \(Cf. **analogue**, **catalogue** & **dialogue**.\) For a comment on the potential decline of the *-ue* form, see -agog\(ue\).

Current ratio in print \(*pedagogue* vs. *\*pedagog*\): 53:1
