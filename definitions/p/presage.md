# presage

presage, n. & vb. A *presage* /**pres**-ij/ is either an omen—something that portends a future occurrence—or an intuition that something is going to happen, especially something bad. To *presage* (/**pres**-ij/ or /pri-**sayj**/) is to foreshadow, foretell, or predict. Elster recommends distinguishing the pronunciation of the verb from that of the noun (*BBBM* at 389).
