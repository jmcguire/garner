# panatela

panatela \(= a long thin cigar\) has been predominantly so spelled since the early 20th century. But from the time the term was first introduced into English in the 1840s until about 1900, \**panatella* was the predominant spelling. Now it's just a variant form, along with \**panetella* and \**panetela*.

Current ratio in print \(*panatela* vs. \**panatella* vs. \**panetela* vs. \**panetella*\): 8:5:1.02:1
