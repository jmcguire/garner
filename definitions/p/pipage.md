# pipage

pipage \(= \[1\] a system of pipes for transmitting gas, water, etc.; \[2\] the transmission of oil, gas, water, etc. through such a system; or \[3\] the financial charge for transmission of this kind\), a noun dating from the 1600s, is predominantly so spelled in AmE. In BrE and across World Englishes generally, *pipeage* is the more usual spelling. Cf. **mileage**.

Current ratio in print \(*pipage* vs. *pipeage* in AmE\): 1.2:1 2016 ratio in print \(*pipeage* vs. *pipage* in BrE\): 12:1
