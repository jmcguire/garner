# patty

patty \(= \[1\] a small flat rounded piece of chopped meat or other food for cooking; or \[2\] a small flat candy or pastry\), a term dating from the mid-1600s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Pattie* is an infrequent variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*hamburger patty* vs. \**hamburger pattie*\): 174:1
