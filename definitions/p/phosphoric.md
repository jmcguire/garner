# phosphoric

phosphoric; **phosphorous**; **phosphorescent**. The usual adjective corresponding to the noun *phosphorus* is *phosphoric* \(= of, relating to, involving, or containing phosphorus\). *Phosphorous* is similar in sense but usually describes something having only trace amounts of phosphorus in it <phosphorous rain>. *Phosphorescent* accents the glow-in-the-dark aspect of phosphorus-containing materials <phosphorescent rocks that glow after sunset>.
