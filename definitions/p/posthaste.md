# posthaste

posthaste is obsolete in all but its adverbial sense (= quickly). And even that is archaic-sounding and fairly rare—e.g.:

 - "Drink it *posthaste*; fruit shakes separate if they sit around." Cathy Thomas, "Go Absolutely Bananas," *Buffalo News*, 30 Apr. 1997, at C2.

 - "After listening to their stories, I asked Agent Findley to initiate a case report with Officer Stoinski's report attached and get it to me *posthaste*." Terry Grosz, *The Thin Green Line* 295 (2004).

 - "You may see that as a sign you've come home again and submit an offer *posthaste*." Leslie Sargent Eskildsen, "Read Telltale Signs Home's Right for You," *Orange County Register*, 25 July 2021, at 5.
