# psaltery

psaltery (= a stringed musical instrument \[of the zither family\] invented by the ancient Hebrews and popular esp. during the Middle Ages), a French/Latin loanword dating in English from the 1300s, has been so spelled throughout the period of Modern English. **Psaltry* is a variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*psaltery* vs. \**psaltry*): 154:1
