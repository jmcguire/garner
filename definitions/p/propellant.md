# propellant

propellant, n. & adj., is the usual form in all varieties of English for both parts of speech <gas propellants> <propellant powder>. In the first half of the 20th century, it was common in AmE to make \**propellent* the adjective and *propellant* the noun, but this distinction had disappeared by 1970.

Current ratio in print \(*propellants* vs. \**propellents*\): 205:1
Current ratio in print \(*propellant charge* vs. \**propellent charge*\): 58:1
