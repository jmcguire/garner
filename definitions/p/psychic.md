# psychic

psychic; **psychical**; **\*psychal**; **psychological**. *Psychic* = \(1\) of, relating to, or involving the psyche; \(2\) spiritual; or \(3\) paranormal. *Psychical* \(= of, relating to, or involving the mind\) is contrasted with *physical*. *\*Psychal* is a needless variant. *Psychological* = \(1\) of, relating to, or involving psychology; or \(2\) of, relating to, or involving the mind, awareness, feeling, or motivation. The *OED* formerly suggested that sense 2 of *psychological* is a loose usage, but it has long been firmly established.
