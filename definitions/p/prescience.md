# prescience

prescience (= foreknowledge or foresight) is preferably pronounced /**presh**-әnts/ in AmE and /**pres**-ee-әnts/ in BrE. The old-fashioned AmE pronunciations were /**pree**-shints/ and /**pree**-shee-ints/.

The corresponding adjective *prescient* is similarly /**presh**-әnt/ (AmE) or /**pres**-ee-әnt/ (BrE)—or, in old-fashioned AmE, /**pree**-shint/ or /**pree**-shee-int/.
