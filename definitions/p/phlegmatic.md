# phlegmatic

phlegmatic \(= having a slow, calm temperament\) is pronounced /fleg-**mat**-ik/, with a hard /g/. This despite the silent *g* in the corresponding noun *phlegm* \(= \[1\] mucus produced in the nose and throat; or \[2\] *less commonly*, calm, steady equanimity in all situations\). See estranged siblings.
