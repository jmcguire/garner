# pizzicato

pizzicato \(= a musical direction that a stringed instrument is to be plucked instead of bowed\), an Italian loanword from the mid-1800s, has two plurals: *pizzicatos* and *pizzicati*. In both AmE and BrE, *pizzicati* has slightly predominated in recent years; *pizzicatos* has been closing the gap.

 - Current ratio in print \(*pizzicati* vs. *pizzicatos*\): 2.1:1
