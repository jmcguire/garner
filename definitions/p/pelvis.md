# pelvis

pelvis \(= the wide, curved bones on which the spine sits and to which the legs are joined\) has two plurals: the ordinary *pelvises* and the Latinate *pelves*. In AmE and BrE, *pelvises* bumped out *pelves* as the predominant form in the early 2000s. It's hard to imagine a reference to imitation Elvises gyrating their pelves.
