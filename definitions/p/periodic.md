# periodic

periodic; **periodical**. These two adjectives have undergone differentiation. *Periodic*, the more general word, means "occurring at regular intervals" \<periodic reviews of employee performance\>. (The word also has specialized meanings in mathematical and scientific contexts.) *Periodical* is now usually restricted to mean "published at regular intervals" \<periodical newsletters\>. And, of course, *periodical* is used as a noun meaning "a publication issued at regular intervals" \<the doctor's office subscribed to a dozen or more periodicals\>.
