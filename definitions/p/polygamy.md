# polygamy

polygamy; **polyandry**; **polygyny**. The first is the broadest and most widely used term, referring to a person's being simultaneously married to more than one spouse. *Polyandry* is the practice of having more than one husband; *polygyny* is the practice of having more than one wife. See **bigamy**.

*Polygamy* is pronounced /pә-**lig**-ә-mee/. *Polyandry* is pronounced /**pol**-ee-an-dree/ or /pol-ee-**an**-dree/. *Polygyny* is pronounced /pә-**lij**-ә-nee/.
