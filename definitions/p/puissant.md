# puissant

puissant \(= strong, powerful\), a gallicism borrowed into English in the 1400s and frequently used by Shakespeare, is preferably pronounced /**pwis**-әnt/ or /**pwee**-sәnt/ in AmE and BrE alike. Avoid such doubly anglicized and decoupled diphthongs as /**pyoo**-i-sint/ and /pyoo-**is**-әnt/.
