# professoriate

professoriate (= collectively, the professors of an academic institution) has been the standard term since the mid-1800s. Before that, *\*professorate* was standard; it is now a needless variant of either *professoriate* or *professorship*. Avoid the variant spelling *\*professoriat*.

 - Current ratio in print (*professoriate* vs. *\*professorate* vs. *\*professoriat*): 18:1.8:1
