# predicable

predicable; \***predicatable**. *\*Predicatable* is an error for either *predicable* (= capable of being asserted) or (more predictably) *predictable*—e.g.: "The conclusion is *predicatable* \[read *predictable*\], but what comes before it is good, old-fashioned fun." Diane Holloway, "Annie's Wig Needs Work," *Austin Am.-Statesman*, 17 Nov. 1995, at F1. See -able (a) & -atable.
