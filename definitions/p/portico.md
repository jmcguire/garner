# portico

portico \(= a covered entrance to a building consisting of a roof supported by two or more pillars\), an Italian loanword dating in English from the late 1500s, has predominantly made the plural *porticoes* since the 1830s in AmE and BrE alike. \**Porticos* is a variant. See plurals \(d\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*porticoes* vs. \**porticos*\): 1.5:1
