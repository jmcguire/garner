# protozoan

protozoan (= any of various single-celled organisms) is the standard spelling. *\*Protozoon* is a variant. *Protozoan* makes the plural *protozoa* or, less commonly, *protozoans*. See plurals (b).

 - Current ratio in print (*protozoan* vs. \**protozoon*): 19:1
 - Current ratio in print (*protozoa* vs. *protozoans*): 5:1
