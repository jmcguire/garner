# piebald

piebald; **skewbald**. These terms refer to spots or blotches of color on animals' coats. Although there is some overlap between the two, the basic distinction is this: *piebald* = spotted white and black; *skewbald* = spotted white and a color other than black \(usu. brown\).

Both words can function as either adjective or noun, and both most often refer to horses.
