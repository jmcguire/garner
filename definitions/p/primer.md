# primer

primer \(= an elementary or introductory textbook\) is pronounced /**prim**-әr/ in AmE but /**prī**-mә/ in BrE. Interestingly, the AmE pronunciation probably represents the older form, and BrE usage seems to have shifted during the 20th century: the original *OED* in 1909 gave precedence to the pronunciation with a short *i*.

When it comes to senses of *primer* relating to an initial coating that prepares a surface for the application of paint, varnish, lacquer, etc., the AmE pronunciation is /**prī**-mәr/. Pronounced this way, the word also has applications in cosmetics, explosives, automotive engineering, and biochemistry.
