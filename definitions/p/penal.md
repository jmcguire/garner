# penal

penal; **punitive**; **penological**. These words have distinct senses. *Penal* = of, relating to, or involving punishment or retribution. *Punitive* = serving to punish; intended to inflict punishment. *Penological* = of, relating to, or involving the study of the philosophy and methods of punishment and treatment of people found guilty of crime.

For a malapropism, see **penile**.
