# panty

panty \(= an item of women's underwear that covers the waist area\) has been predominantly so spelled in the singular since the 1930s—as opposed to \**pantie*. But the term is almost invariably plural, the single item of clothing being known as *a pair of panties*.

Current ratio in print \(*panty* vs. \**pantie*\): 37:1   
Current ratio in print \(*pantyhose* vs. \**pantihose*\): 152:1
