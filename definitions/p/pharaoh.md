# pharaoh

pharaoh (= \[1\] an ancient Egyptian ruler; or \[2\] a stringent taskmaster or tyrannical boss) is generally capitalized in reference to a particular person \<Pharaoh Necho II\> \<Pharaoh Sally, a sarcastic name\> but otherwise lowercase \<the pharaohs of ancient Egypt\>. Even many snoots must look up the word whenever they use it to ensure that they don't misspell it \**pharoah*.
