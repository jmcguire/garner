# point out

point out; **point to**; **point up**. *Point out* = to call attention to <she pointed out the four withering geraniums> <he pointed out the health benefits of eating lots of vegetables>. *Point to* = to direct attention to (as an answer or solution) <they could point to no good reason for closing the facility>. *Point up* = to illustrate <this case points up a key pitfall of the prosecutors' seeking capital punishment>.
