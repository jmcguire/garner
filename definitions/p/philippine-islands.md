# Philippine Islands

Philippine Islands, often shortened to *Philippines*, has been so spelled since the late 1600s. The native islanders are known as *Filipinos*, a word deriving from the country's name when it was a Spanish possession: *Islas Filipinas*. Dating from the 1880s, *Filipino* has had the Tagalog rival *Pilipino* as a synonym since the 1930s—but the established demonym is *Filipino*.

Current ratio in print \(*Filipino* vs. *Pilipino*\): 54:1
