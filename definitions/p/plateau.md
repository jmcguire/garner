# plateau

plateau (= \[1\] a large elevated area of flat land; or \[2\] a period during which the level of something's occurrence is stable, esp. after a period of increase), a French loanword dating in English from the mid-1700s, has formed the anglicized plural *plateaus* in AmE since about 1850 and in BrE only since 2007. The French plural \**plateaux*, now but a variant across World Englishes, predominated in BrE for most of the 1900s. See plurals (b).

 - Current ratio in print (*plateaus* vs. \**plateaux*): 5:1
