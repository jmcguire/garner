# picaresque

picaresque; **picturesque**. These words are quite different. *Picaresque* = roguish; esp., of, relating to, or involving a style of literature dealing with adventurers who are pirates, cheats, and pillagers. *Picturesque* = fit to be the subject of a picture; strikingly graphic.

*Picaresque*, adj. & n., can give rise to a miscue when used in conjunction with a reference to visual imagery. For a good example of this confusion, see miscues (a).
