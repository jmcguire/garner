# possessive

possessive; **possessory**. The terms *possessive* and *possessory* have undergone differentiation. *Possessive* = (1) exhibiting possession or the desire to possess <she dealt with him in a possessive, domineering way>; or (2) (in grammar) denoting possession <a possessive pronoun>. *Possessory* = (1) of, relating to, or involving a possessor <her possessory rights over the farm>; (2) arising from possession <possessory interest>; or (3) that is a possessor <possessory caretaker>.
