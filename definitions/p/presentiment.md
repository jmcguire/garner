# presentiment

presentiment; **presentment**. A *presentiment* is an odd feeling that something bad will soon happen; a premonition <presentiment of death>. The word is pronounced /pri-**zen**-ti-mәnt/—not /pri-**sen**-ti-mәnt/ or /pree-**zen**-ti-mәnt/. A *presentment* is \(1\) a formal statement of an issue to be decided by a court of law, \(2\) a grand jury's formal written accusation made on its own initiative, or \(3\) the formal production of a negotiable instrument for acceptance or payment, or the negotiable instrument so produced. The word is pronounced /pri-**zent**-mәnt/.

Current ratio in print \(*presentiment of death* vs. *\*presentment of death*\): 262:1
