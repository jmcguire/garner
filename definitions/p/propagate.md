# propagate

propagate \(= to reproduce or extend\) is occasionally confused with *promulgate* \(= to proclaim; put \[a policy, law, etc.\] into action\)—e.g.: "The EC tends to impose its will by regulatory edict rather than by statutes enacted by elected representatives. It *propagates* \[read *promulgates*\] regulations daily, at an annual rate of several thousands." "Taking Sides: Stranglehold of the Eurocrats," *Sunday Telegraph*, 29 July 1990, at 21.
