# poinsettia

poinsettia. The preferred pronunciation has four syllables: /poin-**set**-ee-ә/. Although /poin-**set**-ә/ spread far and wide during the 20th century—and is even listed as the first pronunciation in *WNWCD*—it is not yet standard. Another mispronunciation that adds an erroneous *t* to the first syllable \(/point-**set**-ә/\) is common in uncultivated speech.
