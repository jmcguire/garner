# encumbrance

encumbrance; **\*incumbrance**; **\*cumbrance**. The preferred spelling of this word, meaning "a claim or liability that is attached to property and that may lessen its value," is *encumbrance* in both AmE and BrE. *\*Cumbrance* is a needless variant. See en-.

 - Current ratio in print \(*encumbrance* vs. \**incumbrance* vs. \**cumbrance*\): 81:18:1
