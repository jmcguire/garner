# exhalant

exhalant, adj. & n. As an adjective, this term means "emissive; causing to move outward" <an exhalant siphon>. As a noun, it means either "anything emitted as a vapor" or "someone or something that emits vapors." The variant spelling *exhalent* occurs with both parts of speech as a close rival; it was the predominant spelling during much of the 19th century.

 - Current ratio in print (*exhalant vessels* vs. *exhalent vessels*): 1:1.6
 - Current ratio in print (*exhalant aperture* vs. *exhalent aperture*): 1:1.4
 - Current ratio in print (*exhalants* vs. *exhalents*): 1.4:1
