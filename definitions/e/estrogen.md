# estrogen

estrogen \(= a female hormone produced in the ovaries\) is predominantly so spelled in both AmE and World Englishes. In BrE, *\*oestrogen* predominated in the mid-20th century, but since the mid-1970s the simplified spelling without the digraph has become predominant even there. Cf. **estrus**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*estrogen* vs. \**oestrogen*\): 6:1
