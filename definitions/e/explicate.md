# explicate

explicate; **explain**. The terms are synonymous, but are used in different contexts. *Explain* is the ordinary term. *Explicate* \(lit., "to open up pleats; to unfold"\) is more learned and connotes formal, orderly presentation or justification, often of a text. Oddly, the adjectives *explicable* /**ek**-spli-kә-bәl/ and *inexplicable* occur more frequently in print than the verb *explicate.*
