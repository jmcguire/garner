# emery board

emery board \(= a file used as a manicuring implement\) is sometimes misspelled *\*emory board*, as if it had some connection with the Georgia university—e.g.: "With the egg suspended on straws inside the tube of paper taped to a platform of *emory* \[read *emery*\] boards, Farnhof dropped the device onto a target." Lisa Teachey, "Fun: Learning the Formula for Rice Science Olympiad," *Houston Chron.*, 27 Jan. 1997, at A13. *Emery* is the anglicized version of the French word *émeri* \(ultimately of Greek derivation\), denoting a coarse, granular mineral used for grinding or polishing.
