# esophageal

esophageal (BrE *oesophageal*), the adjective corresponding to the noun *esophagus* /i-**sof**-ә-gәs/, is a word typically pronounced only by doctors. And most American doctors say /i-sof-ә-**jee**-әl/; but the traditional pronunciation (reflected, for example, in *W2*) is /ee-soh-**faj**-ee-әl/.
