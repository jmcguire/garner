# edema

edema /e-**deem**-ә/ (= a swelling in a body part caused by an abnormal accumulation of fluid in cells or in a body cavity) has been the predominant spelling in AmE since about 1910. The variant spelling *oedema* (or *œdema*) remains standard in BrE. Pl. *edemas* (BrE *oedemas*).

 - Current ratio in print (*edema* vs. *oedema*): 5:1
