# euthanasia

euthanasia.

**A. And *mercy killing.*** These synonyms are widespread, the former perhaps being more connotatively neutral. *Mercy killing* usually applies to people exclusively, while *euthanasia* applies equally to animals.

**B. And *physician-assisted suicide.*** In *euthanasia*, the doctor may take an active role in the death, as by administering a fatal dose of a drug. In *physician-assisted suicide* (sometimes shortened to *assisted suicide*), the doctor supplies the means of death, but the patient takes the decisive step.
