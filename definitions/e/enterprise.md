# enterprise

enterprise is often misspelled *\*enterprize*—e.g.: "The study is part of an effort to figure out what to do with programs like the *enterprize* \[read *enterprise*\] zones." Aldo Svaldi, "Bidders Seek Chance to Guide State's Hand," *Denver Bus. J.*, 23 Aug. 1996, at A4. The spelling *H.M.S. Enterprize* can be seen in the opening credits of the *Star Trek* series *Enterprise*. There, the spelling reflects the correct name of an 18th-century ship.

## Language-Change Index

 - *enterprise* misspelled *\*enterprize*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*enterprise* vs. \**enterprize*): 324:1
