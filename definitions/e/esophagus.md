# esophagus

esophagus \(= the tube through which food and drink pass from the pharynx to the stomach\) has been the standard AmE spelling since about 1915. *Oesophagus*, the traditional spelling dating back to the 1700s, has long been standard in BrE, especially in the medical world. But since 1980 *esophagus* has continually threatened to overtake it in frequency within British print sources. Across World Englishes, *esophagus* predominates.

Current ratio in print \(*esophagus* vs. *oesophagus*\): 4:1
