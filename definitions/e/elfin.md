# elfin

elfin; **elfish**; **elvish**. The distinction is slight. *Elfin* = (1) of, relating to, or resembling an elf; or (2) having the magical qualities of an elf. *Elfish* = having the mischievous, prankish qualities of an elf. *Elvish* is a needless variant of *elfin*, but it's also a language in J.R.R. Tolkien's *Rings* trilogy (a capitalized proper noun in this sense).
