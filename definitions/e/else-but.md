# \*else but

\*else but; **\*else than**. Instead of either of these ungainly phrases, use a simple *but* or the more idiomatic *other than*—e.g.: "This has nothing to do with some supposed slacker mentality that strikes 20-somethings deaf and dumb to anything *else but* \[read *but* or *other than*\] the boob tube." Helen Ubinas, "What?\! This Place Doesn't Have Cable?\!," *Hartford Courant*, 1 Aug. 1997, at E1.
