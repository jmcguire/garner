# equilibrium

equilibrium. Few dictionaries record a plural form. Although *W11* and *AHD* give precedence to *equilibriums* over *equilibria*, the latter remains much more common in print sources. A survey of writers in the early 2000s found that writers under 45 were more likely to prefer *equilibriums*, which has remained little used but stable.

 - Current ratio in print \(*equilibria* vs. *equilibriums*\): 20:1
