# embezzle

embezzle; **misappropriate**; **steal**. *Embezzle* (= to fraudulently convert personal property that one has been entrusted with) is now always used in reference to fiduciaries. *Misappropriate* means "to take for oneself wrongfully" and may be used of anyone—fiduciary or not. *Steal*, like *misappropriate*, is a broader term than *embezzle*; it has the same meaning as *misappropriate*, but much stronger negative connotations. See **defalcate (a)** & **appropriate (a)**.
