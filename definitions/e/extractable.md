# extractable

extractable (= \[1\] \[of a tooth, organ, etc.\] capable of being removed; \[2\] \[of information, etc.\] capable of being elicited; or \[3\] \[of a price, etc.\] capable of being obtained by virtue of an advantageous position), an adjective dating from the 1600s, has been predominantly so spelled since the late 1840s. \**Extractible* is a variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*extractable* vs. \**extractible*): 72:1
