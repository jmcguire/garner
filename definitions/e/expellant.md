# expellant

expellant, adj. & n. As an adjective, this word means "forcing or tending to force something out." As a noun, it means "a medicine that causes either vomiting or excretion." Although the variant spelling \**expellent* was common during the 1800s and early 1900s, *expellant* has predominated since about 1955. Before that, the two spellings were in close competition for predominance.

2018 ratio in print \(*expellants* vs. \**expellents*\): 20:1
