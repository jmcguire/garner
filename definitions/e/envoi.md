# envoi

envoi; **envoy**. For the sense "a postscript to a poem or essay," both spellings are used. Major American dictionaries are divided on the point—some listing *envoi* first and others *envoy* first. In that sense, though, *envoi* is the more usual spelling by a significant margin in print.

That development makes for useful differentiation because *envoy* is now used almost exclusively in diplomatic contexts. Both words are preferably pronounced /**en**-voi/, not /**on**-voi/.
