# embiggen

embiggen \(= to enlarge or magnify\), a nonce-word used in 1884, was reintroduced as a neologism in a 1996 episode of the TV series *The Simpsons* \("A noble spirit *embiggens* the smallest man"\). It remains a jocular word, as when @GreyDuck tweeted in 2008, "I'm glad that I could help *embiggen* your vocabulary." \(Quoted in *OED*.\) The term will probably stay on the fringes of English vocabulary, but who knows? If it does become mainstream, it would still be hard to argue that the language has become seriously embiggened. Cf. **\*enlargen**.
