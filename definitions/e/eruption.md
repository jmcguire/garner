# eruption

eruption; **irruption**. *Eruption* = the act or process of bursting or breaking out \<a volcanic eruption\>. *Irruption* = the act or process of rushing or breaking in; violent entry or invasion \<an irruption of water through a breach in the dam and into the spillway\>. *Irruption* also carries the specialized sense of "an abrupt increase in the local population of an animal"—e.g.: "Ornithologists call these erratic southern movements ‘invasions' or ‘*irruptions*' and refer to these species as ‘irruptive migrants.' " Scott Shalaway, "The Wild Side," *Charleston Gaz. & Daily Mail* (W. Va.), 16 Feb. 1996, at D1.
