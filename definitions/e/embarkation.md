# embarkation

embarkation \(= \[1\] the act of going on board a means of transportation; or \[2\] the act of starting a mission or enterprise\), a French loanword dating from the 1600s, is the standard term. The word has one needless variant \(*\*embarkment*\) and several archaic variant spellings \(*\*embarcation*, *\*imbarcation*, and *\*imbarkation*\); use any one of those, and you're embarking up the wrong word. Cf. **debarkation**.
