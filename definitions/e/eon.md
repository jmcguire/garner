# eon

eon; **aeon**. The older spelling of this word, a Latin/Greek loanword dating in English from the 1600s, is *aeon*. That was long the predominant form in BrE, but today the two are neck-and-neck. In AmE the shortened *eon* became more common about 1940.

Current ratio in print \(*eons ago* vs. *aeons ago* in AmE\): 8:1   
Current ratio in print \(*eons ago* vs. *aeons ago* in BrE\): 1.3:1
