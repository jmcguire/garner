# elocution

elocution; **locution**; **allocution**. *Elocution* = style in speaking; the art of speaking persuasively. *Locution* = a word or phrase. *Allocution* = \(1\) a formal address; \(2\) a judge's formal address asking a criminal defendant to speak in mitigation of the sentence to be imposed; or \(3\) loosely, a criminal defendant's speech in mitigation of the sentence to be imposed.
