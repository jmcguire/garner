# elegance

elegance; **elegancy**. A distinction has emerged. *Elegance* is the quality or state of being elegant. *Elegancy* is a thing that is thought to be elegant. Concededly, though, the history of these words is a messy one. Since 1750, *elegance* has far eclipsed *elegancy* in frequency of occurrence.

Current ratio in print \(*elegance* vs. *elegancy*\): 204:1
