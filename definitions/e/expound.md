# expound

expound; **propound**. *Expound* = to explain. *Propound* = to set forth; to put forward for consideration. Both are preferably transitive. In the best usage, one *expounds* an idea or doctrine—rather than *expounding on* it. Likewise, one *propounds* questions to a jury.

Current ratio in print \(*expounded the virtues* vs. *expounded on the virtues*\): 1.5:1
