# endue

endue; \***indue**. Both mean \(1\) "to provide with a quality or trait"; or \(2\) "to put on \(a piece of clothing\)" \(*AHD*\). Although neither form is common, the spelling *endue* predominates. *\*Indue* is a needless variant. See en-.

 - Current ratio in print \(*endue* vs. \**indue*\): 3:1
