# enology

enology (= the knowledge or study of wines and wine-making), a word introduced in the early 1800s, is predominantly so spelled in AmE—*oenology* in BrE. Oddly, however, *oenophile* (= a lover of wines) is the standard spelling in both AmE and BrE. Whoever decided this point of AmE usage must have been tippling unduly.

 - Current ratio in print (*oenology* vs. *enology* in World Englishes): 1.3:1
