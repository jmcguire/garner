# evasion

evasion; **evasiveness**. *Evasion* \(= \[1\] the deliberate avoidance of doing what one should; or \[2\] an instance of deliberately not answering a question or not talking about something\) is always an act. *Evasiveness* \(= the tendency not to answer questions directly or not to talk about things that need to be discussed\) is always a quality.
