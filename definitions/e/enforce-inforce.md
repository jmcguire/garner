# enforce; \*inforce

enforce; \*inforce.

**A. Spelling.** The latter is an archaic spelling whose only vestige appears in *reinforce.* See en-.

 - Current ratio in print (*enforce* vs. \**inforce*): 199:1

**B. "Enforcing" a Contract.** Lawyers continually speak of *enforcing* contracts, but this term is not apt unless one is seeking specific performance. Usually, the law merely specifies a remedy for breach of contract—damages—and does not compel performance.
