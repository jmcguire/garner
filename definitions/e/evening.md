# evening

evening. What period of a day is *evening*? According to most dictionaries, it is the later part of the day through the early part of the night or \(not much more specifically\) the period between sunset and bedtime or darkness. In many places, *evening* begins when the workday is over, around 5:00 p.m. But in some regional dialects, *evening* can mean the entire time after 12:00 noon until dark.
