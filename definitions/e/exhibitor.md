# exhibitor

exhibitor; **exhibitioner**; **exhibitionist**. These terms have gone through sometimes shocking differentiation. The term in most widespread use is *exhibitor*, which denotes either \(1\) a person or company with a display of some kind at a convention, show, fair, etc., or \(2\) someone who shows a film, slideshow, etc. \(\**Exhibiter* is a variant spelling.\) *Exhibitioner* is BrE for a student who is awarded a grant or stipend. An *exhibitionist* is someone with few or no inhibitions about attracting attention, often through flashing \(that is, quickly exposing genitals or breasts\) or through outright nudity. Cf. **inhibitor**.
