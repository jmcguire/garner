# expandable

expandable \(= capable of being enlarged, increased, or spread out\), dating from the 1800s, didn't oust its competing form, *\*expansible*, until the 1960s. Both *\*expansible* and *\*expandible* are needless variants. See -able \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*expandable* vs. *\*expansible* vs. *\*expandible*\): 625:36:1
