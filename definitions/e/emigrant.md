# emigrant

emigrant; **émigré**. There is a latent differentiation between these words. An *emigrant* is someone who leaves a country to settle in another. *Émigré* has the same sense, but applies especially to one in political exile. The first acute accent is often omitted (*emigré*) in AmE. As for frequency of occurrence in English-language contexts, *emigrant* has greatly predominated over *émigré*.
