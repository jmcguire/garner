# embed

embed \(= \[1\] to put \[something\] or to be put firmly and deeply into something else; \[2\] to ingrain or instill very strongly; or \[3\] to put \[a computing feature\] into a computer program or webpage\), a verb dating in its literal sense from the late 1700s, has been predominantly so spelled since the 1880s in BrE and about 1900 in AmE. Before that time, \**imbed* was the usual spelling. See en-.

 - Current ratio in print \(*embedded* vs. \**imbedded*\): 31:1
