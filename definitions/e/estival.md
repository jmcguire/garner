# estival

estival (= of, relating to, or involving summertime), a French loanword dating in English from the 1300s, has been predominantly so spelled in all varieties of English since the 1940s. Before that, this spelling was embroiled in a long rivalry with *aestival*. See gallicisms.

 - Current ratio in print (*estival* vs. *aestival* in AmE): 3:1
 - Current ratio in print (*estival* vs. *aestival* in BrE): 3:1
