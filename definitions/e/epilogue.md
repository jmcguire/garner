# epilogue

epilogue (= a parting word at the end of a written or spoken presentation) is so spelled—preferably not *\*epilog*. Cf. **analogue**, **catalogue** & **dialogue**. For a comment on the potential decline of the *-ue* form, see -agog(ue).

 - Current ratio in print (*epilogue* vs. \**epilog*): 105:1
