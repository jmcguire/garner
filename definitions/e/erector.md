# erector

erector \(= \[1\] someone who builds something or fixes it in an upright position; or \[2\] a muscle that raises a body part or keeps it upright or extended\), an agent noun dating from the 1500s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Erecter* is a variant. See -er \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*erector* vs. \**erecter*\): 139:1
