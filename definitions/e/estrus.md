# estrus

estrus \(= the period of sexual excitability of a female placental mammal\) has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since the 1930s and across World Englishes since the 1960s. In BrE, the spelling *oestrus* \(or, with a digraph, *œstrus*\) has been consistently preferred since the 1700s. See **estrous cycle**. Cf. **estrogen**.

Current ratio in print \(*estrus* vs. *oestrus* in World Englishes\): 3:1
