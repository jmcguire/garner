# emblazon

emblazon \(= \[1\] to inscribe decoratively and conspicuously; or \[2\] to extol or treat as illustrious\), a French loanword dating in English from the 1500s, is the standard term. *\*Emblaze* and *\*imblaze* are needless variants.

 - Current ratio in print \(*emblazoned* vs. *\*emblazed* vs. *\*imblazed*\): 2,711:28:1
