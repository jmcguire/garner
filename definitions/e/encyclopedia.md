# encyclopedia

encyclopedia; \***encyclopaedia**. The *ae* was predominantly shortened to *e* in AmE by 1905 and in BrE by 1970. *\*Encyclopaedia* is becoming an archaic spelling throughout the English-speaking world, although it has long been used in the title *Encyclopaedia Britannica*. See ae.

 - Current ratio in print (*encyclopedia* vs. \**encyclopaedia*): 5:1
