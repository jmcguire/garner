# extortionist

extortionist; \***extortioner**; **\*extorter**. From 1750 until the 1950s, *\*extortioner* was standard and held the field. In 1954, *extortionist* surpassed it in AmE (in frequency of use in print sources, that is), and in 1987 the same thing happened in BrE. *Extortionist* is now standard. The others await their ransoms.

 - Current ratio in print (*extortionist* vs. \**extortioner* vs. \**extorter*): 8:6:1
