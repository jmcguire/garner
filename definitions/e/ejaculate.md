# ejaculate

ejaculate. Though one of Henry James's favorites, this verb can no longer be used in sober writing as a synonym for "exclaim." Today it inevitably carries sexual overtones, even when the exclamatory meaning is the primary one. E.g.: "An enormous kerfuffle erupted nearby, followed by a shriek of maniacal laughter. ‘Crumbs\!' *ejaculated* \[read *exclaimed*\] William. ‘What was that?' " David Aaronovitch, "Violet Voter Finds Just the Job for William," *Independent*, 9 Oct. 1997, at 6.
