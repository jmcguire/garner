# executor

executor \(= someone who is appointed in a will to administer a deceased person's estate\) is the standard spelling. *\*Executer* is an obsolete variant. The word is pronounced with the accent on the second syllable: /ig-**zek**-yә-tәr/. See **trustee \(b\)**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*executor* vs. *\*executer*\): 120:1
