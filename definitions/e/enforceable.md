# enforceable

enforceable; \***enforcible**. Since the mid-1800s, *enforceable* has been the preferred spelling in both AmE and BrE. E.g.: "A contract is *enforcible* \[read *enforceable*\] even though it does not specify the type of deed to be given." Robert Kratovil, *Real Estate Law* 83 \(1946\). Cf. **forcible**.

## Language-Change Index

 - *enforceable* misspelled *\*enforcible*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print \(*enforceable* vs. \**enforcible*\): 767:1
