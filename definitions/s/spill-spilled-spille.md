# spill > spilled > spilled

spill > spilled > spilled. So inflected in both AmE \(from 1900\) and BrE \(from 1956\). The archaic past form *spilt* still sometimes appears in metaphorical references to *spilt milk* \("don't cry over spilt milk"\), particularly in BrE. But *spilled milk* is equally common in AmE.

 - Current ratio in print \(*spilled wine* vs. *spilt wine*\): 3:1
