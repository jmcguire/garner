# suspense

suspense; **suspension**. *Suspense* is the feeling of anxiety or excitement you experience when you don't know what will happen next. *Suspension* can be \(1\) the act of holding something aloft <the suspension of particulate matter>; \(2\) the official stopping of something for a period <suspension of hostilities>; \(3\) the disallowing of someone to carry on for a time, esp. as a form of punishment <the player's suspension for four games was amply justified>; \(4\) the part of a vehicle attached to the wheels to make the ride more comfortable on bumpy roads <a car with superb suspension>; or \(5\) a liquid mixture in which minute bits of solid material are contained without dissolving <flecks of gold floating in the suspension>.
