# sepulchre

sepulchre; ***\**sepulcher**; **sepulture**. Oddly enough, *sepulchre* is the standard spelling in AmE as well as BrE—not *\*sepulcher*. The word means "burial place, tomb," and is pronounced /**sep**-әl-kәr/. *Sepulture*, sometimes a needless variant of *\*sepulcher*, justifies its separate form in the sense "burial." These words are formal, even literary. See formal words.

 - Current ratio in print \(*sepulchre* vs. \**sepulcher*\): 5:1
