# Saudi

Saudi; \***Saudi Arabian**. For a citizen of Saudi Arabia, *Saudi* \(/**sow**-dee/ or /**saw**-dee/\) is the standard term; the longer term is a needless variant. By contrast, *Arab* denotes not citizenship but ethnicity. See **Arab**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*Saudis* vs. \**Saudi Arabians*\): 54:1
