# shy

shy, adj. Since about 1800, the comparative of this word has been predominantly spelled *shyer*, the superlative *shyest*—in AmE and BrE alike. \(*W11* gives priority to the wrong forms: *\*shier* and *\*shiest*.\) *Shyly* is so spelled—not *\*shily*. Cf. **sly** & **spry**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*shyer* vs. \**shier*\): 5:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*shyest* vs. \**shiest*\): 12:1
