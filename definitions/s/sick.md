# sick

sick, adj.; **sickly**, adj. & adv.; **\*sicklily**, adv. While *sick* means "ill," *sickly* \(adj.\) means "habitually ill" <a sickly young man> or "associated with sickness" <a sickly complexion>. Because *sickly* is an adverb as well as an adjective, the term *\*sicklily* can justifiably be regarded as a needless variant. See adverbs \(c\).
