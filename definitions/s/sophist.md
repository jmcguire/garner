# sophist

sophist; **sophister**. *Sophister* is, except in historical contexts denoting a certain rank of student, a needless variant of *sophist* \(/**sof**-ist/\), which today has primarily negative connotations in the sense "a person who uses fallacious arguments; a specious reasoner." Formerly, *sophist* was a respectable word meaning "a person distinguished for erudition; a scholar."

 - Current ratio in print \(*sophists* vs. *sophisters*\): 29:1
