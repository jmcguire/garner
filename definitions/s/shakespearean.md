# Shakespearean

Shakespearean; **\*Shakespearian**. The first spelling is now standard in AmE and BrE alike. On the merits, *Shakespearean* is preferable because it preserves the final vowel in the great bard's name. *\*Shakespearian* is a variant. Cf. **Euclidean** & **Mephistophelean**.

Current ratio in print \(*Shakespearean* vs. \**Shakespearian*\): 7:1
