# suite

suite. In the phrase *bedroom suite*, the standard pronunciation of *suite* is /sweet/; in dialect, the word becomes /soot/ \(like *suit*\). Occasionally, the dialectal pronunciation affects the printed word \(*\*bedroom suit*\)—e.g.: "When I was in Denver, Colorado, in the '60s, I bought quite a bit of furniture, a bedroom *suit* \[read *suite*\] and a dining room *suit* \[read *suite*\]." Anon., "Meet the Woodworkers," *Chambersburg Public Opinion* \(Pa.\), 16 Dec. 2007, Local §, at 1.
