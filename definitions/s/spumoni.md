# spumoni

spumoni; **spumone**. The Italian term for this ice-cream dessert is *spumone* /spyoo-**moh**-nee/. Although that used to be the preferred spelling in English as well, dictionaries are now almost equally divided. In English print sources, *spumoni* appears much more often than *spumone*. *Spumoni* has predominated in English since the 1920s.

 - Current ratio in print (*spumoni* vs. *spumone*): 14:1
