# skeptic

skeptic is the standard spelling in AmE, *sceptic* in BrE. The BrE variant occasionally surfaces in AmE—e.g.: "How far toward *scepticism* \[read *skepticism*\] may students be led by their possible bewilderment?" James Sledd, "Hans Kurath on English Pronunciation," 40 *Am. Speech* 201, 205 \(1965\). Yet the AmE preference for *skeptic* has been fixed since about 1940.

Current ratio in print \(*skeptic* vs. *sceptic* in AmE\): 5:1   
Current ratio in print \(*sceptic* vs. *skeptic* in BrE\): 1.02:1
