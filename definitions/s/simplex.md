# simplex

simplex \(= \[1\] a simple word; \[2\] archaically, a single-needle telegraph; or \[3\] some other rudimentary construct, esp. in mathematics\) has three possible plurals. *Simplices* is the oldest and still the predominant form. *Simplexes* is a 20th-century anglicization that frequently appears. *\*Simplicia* is an old variant that rarely appears except in sense 1.
