# scriptorium

scriptorium \(= a room in which scribes, esp. in medieval monasteries, do their copying\) predominantly makes the Latinate plural *scriptoria*—which makes sense for people who historically were writing primarily in Latin. We don't have many scriptoria these days, but if we did we might well call them *scriptoriums*. The original *OED* editors worked in a hut majestically called a *scriptorium*.
