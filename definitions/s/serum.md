# serum

serum (= \[1\] a liquid injected into a person's blood to fight infection or poison; \[2\] the thin part of blood; or \[3\] the liquid from a plant) forms the plural *sera* or *serums*. The former has always preponderated in English-language print sources.

 - Current ratio in print (*two sera* vs. *two serums*): 1.8:1
