# scavenge

scavenge, vb.; **scavenger**, vb. The verb *scavenge* is a back-formation from the noun *scavenger*. *Scavenge* having taken hold as the predominant verb in the late 1800s—first in BrE and then in AmE—the verb *scavenger* should be considered a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*scavenged* vs. *\*scavengered*): 450:1
