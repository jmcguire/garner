# sacrosanct

sacrosanct, literally "most sacred," is now often ironic. Sometimes the irony appears unintentional—e.g.: "Ray Kroc, who founded the McDonald's empire, wrote that the french fry was ‘*sacrosanct*,' its preparation ‘a ritual to be followed religiously.' " Danny Penman, "Judgment Day for McDonald's," *Independent*, 19 June 1997, at 20.
