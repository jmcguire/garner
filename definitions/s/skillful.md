# skillful

skillful.

**A. And *skilful*.** The AmE spelling is *skillful*, the BrE *skilful.* Cf. **willful**.

**B. And *skilled*.** Since the 1500s, the adjective *skilled* has commonly appeared in such collocations as *skilled artisan*, *skilled engineer*, and *skilled seaman*. *Skilled* has always predominated in word frequency over *skillful*—which can refer to professionals but often appears in collocations such as *skillful diligence* and *skillful management*.
