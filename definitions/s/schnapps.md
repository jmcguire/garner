# schnapps

schnapps /shnaps/ \(= any of several drinks having high alcoholic content\), a German loanword dating in English from the late 1700s, has been predominantly so spelled since the late 1850s. The older spelling was **schnaps*, which—like \**shnaps* and \**shnapps*—is a variant. See germanicisms.
