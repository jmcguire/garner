# submersible

submersible; **\*submergible**; **\*submergeable**. Though the second might seem simpler \(cf. *persuadable* and *\*persuasible*\), the first has always been more common in both AmE and BrE. *\*Submergeable* is a needless variant. See -able \(a\).

Current ratio in print \(*submersible* vs. \**submergible* vs. \**submergeable*\): 4,584:78:1
