# scimitar

scimitar /**sim**-i-tәr/ or /-tahr/ \(= a saber with a curved blade—associated mainly with Arabs and Turks\), an Italian/French loanword dating in English from the 1500s, has been predominantly so written since the early 1700s. Among the variant spellings, which have been many, are **simitar*, \**scimetar*, \**scymitar*, \**scimiter*, \**cimiter*, \**cimitery*, and \**simiter*.
