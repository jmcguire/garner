# supplementary

supplementary; **supplemental**; **suppletory**; \***suppletive**. *Supplementary* has been the standard term since about 1800. The other forms have the same meaning, namely, "of the nature of, forming, or serving as a remedy for the deficiencies of something." Outside the law—which uses special phrases such as *supplemental pleading* and *suppletory oath*—the terms *supplemental*, *suppletory*, and *\*suppletive* are needless variants.
