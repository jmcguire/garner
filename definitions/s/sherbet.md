# sherbet

sherbet /**sh”ôr**-b”ôt/ is commonly mispronounced with an intrusive *r*: /**sh”ôr**-b”ôrt/. Because of this mispronunciation, the word is sometimes wrongly spelled *\*sherbert*. See pronunciation (b).

## Language-Change Index

1. *sherbet* mispronounced /**sh”ôr**-b”ôrt/: Stage 3

2. *sherbet* misspelled *\*sherbert*: Stage 2
 - Current ratio in print (*sherbet* vs. \**sherbert*): 29:1
