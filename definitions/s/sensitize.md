# sensitize

sensitize; **\*sensitivize**. Although in 1926 H.W. Fowler championed the latter, the former is now standard in AmE and BrE alike. *\*Sensitivize*, a rare word, is now rightly seen as eccentric. See -ize.

*sensitize*: Stage 5
