# sideswipe

sideswipe; **\*sidewipe**. *Sideswipe* \(= to strike a glancing blow\), first used in reference to railroads \(1891\), is the term to use. \(It was originally a noun \[1857\] and only later a verb.\) *\*Sidewipe*, an artificial form that appeared occasionally in the early 20th century, is a needless variant.

Current ratio in print \(*sideswipe* vs. \**sidewipe*\): 324:1
