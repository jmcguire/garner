# skull

skull; **scull**. *Skull* = the head of a skeleton. *Scull* = \(1\) a small light racing boat for use by one, two, or four persons; \(2\) one of a pair of oars used with such a boat; or \(3\) a single oar used at the stern of a boat to propel it forward by working the oar from side to side.
