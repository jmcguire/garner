# slicked-back hair

slicked-back hair. That's the phrase, not *\*slicked-backed hair* or \**slick-backed hair*—e.g.: "Wearing *slicked-backed* \[read *slicked-back*\] hair, a series of sleek evening outfits, and a raffish smile, he's more than a dancer." Frank Rich, *Hot Seat* 59 \(1998\).

Language-Change Index

*\*slicked-backed* for *slicked-back*: Stage 1
