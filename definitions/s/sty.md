# sty

sty (= an inflammation on the eyelid) has been the slightly predominant standard spelling in AmE since 1970. The plural is *sties*. (See plurals (e).) *Stye* is a variant that predominates in BrE (pl. *styes*). The phrase *sty in the eye* is almost twice as common across World Englishes as *stye in the eye* (the usual BrE spelling).

Another word spelled *sty* (= a pen for pigs) also has the plural *sties*. The solid word *pigsty* is the more usual term in this sense (pl. *pigsties*).
