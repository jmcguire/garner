# sobriquet

sobriquet; ***\**soubriquet**. *Sobriquet* /**soh**-bri-kay/ is the standard spelling of this term for an assumed name, a nickname, or an epithet. *\*Soubriquet* is a variant that has never predominated and has been relatively uncommon since about 1900.

Current ratio in print \(*sobriquet* vs. \**soubriquet*\): 6:1
