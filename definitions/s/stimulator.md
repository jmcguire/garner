# stimulator

stimulator \(= someone or something that prompts or provokes an activity to begin or develop further\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the early 1600s, has always been predominantly so spelled. **Stimulater* is an infrequent variant. See -er \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*stimulator* vs. *\*stimulater*\): 446:1
