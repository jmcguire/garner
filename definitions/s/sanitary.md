# sanitary

sanitary; **sanative**; \***sanatory**. *Sanitary* = of, relating to, or involving health or, more usu., cleanliness \<sanitary surgical tools\>. *Sanative* = health-producing; healthful \<sanative treatments\>. *\*Sanatory* is a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*sanitary measures* vs. \**sanatory measures* vs. \**sanative measures*): 1,421:17:1 2018 ratio in print (*sanative power* vs. *sanitary power* vs. \**sanatory power*): 41:7:1
