# suspendable

suspendable; **\*suspendible**; **\*suspensible**. The first is standard in AmE and BrE alike; the second and third are needless variants. Though *\*suspendible* is the only form listed in *W3* and the *OED* \(as of 2021\), *suspendable* is more than ten times as common. See -able \(a\).

Current ratio in print \(*suspendable* vs. \**suspendible* vs. \**suspensible*\): 16:1.6:1
