# systematic

systematic; **systemic**. *Systematic* = (1) carried out according to an organized plan \<a systematic approach to each workday\>; or (2) habitual, deliberate \<systematic reading at 7:00 p.m. each day\>. *Systemic* = affecting an entire system; systemwide \<a systemic failure\>. Typically, *systemic* should be replaced by *systematic* unless the reference is to systems of the body \<autoimmune and other systemic disorders\> or metaphors based on bodily systems \<our political order has been debilitated by a series of systemic problems\>.
