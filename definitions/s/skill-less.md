# skill-less

skill-less —so hyphenated—is sometimes misspelled *\*skilless*. E.g.: " ‘Regardless of what people think, it's not a *skilless* \[read *skill-less*\] job,' said a clerk at a west end Safeway." Mike Sadava, "No Stores to Shut if Strike Hits Safeway," *Edmonton J.*, 21 Mar. 1997, at B3. See punctuation (j).
