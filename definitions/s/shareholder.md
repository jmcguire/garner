# shareholder

shareholder; **stockholder**; \***shareowner**; **stakeholder**. The first three terms refer to someone who owns stock in a corporation. The first is the most common in both AmE and BrE, the second a fairly common equivalent, and the third so much less frequent that it has become a needless variant. *Stakeholder*, a distinct term, denotes someone who has a substantial interest in something <all our employees are stakeholders>. Originally, a *stakeholder* held the stakes for wagers or bets.

 - Current ratio in print \(*shareholder* vs. *stockholder* vs. \**shareowner*\): 642:71:1
