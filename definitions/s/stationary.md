# stationary

stationary; **stationery**. The first is the adjective \(= remaining in one place, immobile\), the second the noun \(= writing materials, esp. paper with envelopes\). For a mnemonic device, think of the *-er* in *paper*. In the old days, people knew *stationer* as an agent noun for one who sells stationery.

On *stationary* as a nongradable adjective, see adjectives \(b\).

Current ratio in print \(*office stationery* vs. \**office stationary*\): 7:1
Current ratio in print \(*stationary engines* vs. \**stationery engines*\): 280:1
