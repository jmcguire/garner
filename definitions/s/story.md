# story

story; **storey**. For the floor or level of a building, *story* is AmE and *storey* BrE. Surprisingly, the BrE preference for *storey* did not take hold until the late 1940s. The plural forms are *stories* and *storeys*. See plurals (e).

Across World Englishes, the spelling *story* predominates in this floor-of-a-building sense.

 - Current ratio in print (*room on the second story* vs. *room on the second storey*): 3:1
