# status quo

status quo; ***status quo ante***; ***\*status in quo***. *Status quo* means "the state of affairs at present"; hence *\*current status quo* is a redundancy. *Status quo ante* (= the state of affairs at a previous time) is generally confined to legal contexts. (So are the adverbial latinisms *in statu quo* and *in statu quo ante*, both meaning "in the same state of affairs as existed earlier.") *\*Status in quo* is an archaic variant of *status quo*.
