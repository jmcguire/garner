# smidgen

smidgen \(= a small amount; a scintilla\), an Americanism dating from the mid-1800s, is the standard term. \**Smidgeon* and \**smidgin* are variant spellings. One also frequently hears the casualism *smidge* today; it dates from about the same time.

Current ratio in print \(*smidgen* vs. *smidge* vs. \**smidgeon* vs. \**smidgin*\): 35:27:12:1
