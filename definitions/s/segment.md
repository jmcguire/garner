# segment

segment \(= to divide into parts\) came into use as a verb in the mid-1800s. The corresponding noun is about three centuries older. \(See functional shift \(d\).) As for *\*segmentalize* and *\*segmentize*, they're needless variants.

Current ratio in print \(*segmenting* vs. *\*segmentalizing* vs. *\*segmentizing*\): 6,139:2.3:1
