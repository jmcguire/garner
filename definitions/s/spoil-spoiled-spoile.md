# spoil > spoiled > spoiled

spoil > spoiled > spoiled. So inflected in AmE and BrE alike. *Spoilt*, a common variant of the past-tense spelling especially in BrE, has never predominated even in that variety of English. But *spoilt child* occurs almost as frequently in BrE as *spoiled child*.

 - Current ratio in print (*spoiled* vs. *spoilt*): 4:1
