# solicitude

solicitude = (1) protectiveness; or (2) anxiety. Because of these two quite different senses, the word is often ambiguous. But sense 1 is now more common—e.g.: "Afterward, in a show of *solicitude* rarely displayed during his five months at St. Stanislaus, he comforted parishioners distraught by the death of their beloved Father Willie." Mark Gillispie, "An Unlikely Murder Suspect," *Plain Dealer* (Cleveland), 12 Jan. 2003, at A1.
