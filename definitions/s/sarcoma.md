# sarcoma

sarcoma \(= a malignant tumor within the body's connective tissues\) predominantly makes the plural *sarcomas* \(standard since the late 1800s\). The etymologically correct Greek plural *\*sarcomata* was in sharp decline by 1910 and had been thoroughly eclipsed in frequency of use by 1940.

 - Current ratio in print \(*sarcomas* vs. \**sarcomata*\): 395:1
