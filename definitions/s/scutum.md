# scutum

scutum (= a bony or horned plate or scale found on some reptiles and insects) makes the plural *scuta*. But the anglicized shortened form *scute*, made by apocope, has the straightforward plural *scutes*. Since about 1875, *scutes* has occurred with greater frequency than *scuta* in English-language print sources worldwide.

 - Current ratio in print (*scutes* vs. *scuta*): 2.2:1
