# submissible

submissible; \***submissable**; **\*submittable**. Formerly labeled "rare" in the *OED*, *submissible* now occurs with some frequency in AmE and BrE alike. *\*Submissable* and *\*submittable* are needless variants.

 - Current ratio in print \(*submissible* vs. *\*submittable* in World Englishes\): 1.7:1
