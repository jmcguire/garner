# statute

statute (= a piece of legislation enacted by a legislative body), a French/Latin loanword dating in English from the 1300s, is especially susceptible to the typographical error of being spelled *statue* (= an image of a person or animal made in stone, metal, etc.). Hence it's fairly common to see such blunders as \**statue of frauds* and \**statue of limitations*.

 - Current ratio in print (*statute of limitations* vs. \**statue of limitations*): 355:1
