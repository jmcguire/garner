# show > showed > shown

show > showed > shown. So inflected. The standard past participle has been *shown*, not *showed*, since the 1600s. Hence *has shown*, not \**has showed*. In the passive voice, *shown* is mandatory <he was shown to have lied>. See irregular verbs.
