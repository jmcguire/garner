# spell \> spelled \> spelled

spell \> spelled \> spelled. So inflected in AmE (since 1850) and mostly in post-1980 BrE, though *spelt* is the more traditional past form in BrE. *Spelt* might be less desirable because, as a noun, it also refers to a type of ancient grain.

 - Current ratio in print (*spelled* vs. *spelt* in World Englishes): 3:1
