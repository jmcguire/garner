# shed > shed > shed

shed > shed > shed. So inflected. Avoid the erroneous *\*shedded*—e.g.: "Prosecutors plainly want to suggest that the missing bags contained both the knife they believe Mr. Simpson used to murder Nicole Brown Simpson and Ronald L. Goldman, and the blood-soaked clothes he quickly *shedded* \[read *shed*\] before boarding his flight to Chicago." David Margolick, "Five Bags or Three Bags? Simpson Prosecutor Asks," *N.Y. Times*, 30 May 1995, at A14. See irregular verbs.
