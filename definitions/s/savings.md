# saving(s)

saving(s). In refined prose, write *a saving of $100*, not *\*a savings of $100*. Technically, *savings* is a plural, not a singular. Yet as an adjective in the phrases *savings bank*, *savings account*, and the like, the plural form is unimpeachable. Cf. **daylight saving(s) time**.

 - Current ratio in print (*a saving of* vs. *\*a savings of *): 2.3:1
