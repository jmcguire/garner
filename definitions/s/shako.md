# shako

shako (= a stiff cylindrical military-style hat, usu. having a high crown and a flat top, and often a plume), a Hungarian loanword dating in English from the late 1700s, has always predominantly made the plural *shakos*, not \**shakoes*. See plurals (c).

 - Current ratio in print (*shakos* vs. \**shakoes*): 10:1
