# scherzo

scherzo; **scherzando**. Although the musical term *scherzo* \(= a playful, sprightly composition\) has predominantly formed the plural *scherzos* \(not *scherzi*\) since the late 1800s, the story is different for *scherzando* \(= a musical direction to perform playfully\). Only the Italianate plural *scherzandi* exists for this term.

Current ratio in print \(*scherzos* vs. *scherzi*\): 2:1
