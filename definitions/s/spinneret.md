# spinneret

spinneret \(= \[1\] the organ by which a spider or caterpillar produces threads from its silk glands; or \[2\] a machine that produces filaments for the manufacture of synthetic fibers\), a term dating from the early 1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Spinnerette* and \**spinnaret* are variants. See diminutives \(f\).

Current ratio in print \(*spinneret* vs. \**spinnerette* vs. \**spinnaret*\): 1,434:41:1
