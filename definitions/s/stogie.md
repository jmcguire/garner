# stogie

stogie \(= a long, thin cigar\) has been the standard spelling in BrE since the 1920s and in AmE since the 1940s. Even so, the word is much more common today in AmE than in BrE. *\*Stogy* \(predominant in AmE till about 1940\) and *\*stogey* are variants.

Current ratio in print \(*stogie* vs. *\*stogy* vs. *\*stogey*\): 127:33:1
