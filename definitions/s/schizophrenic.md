# schizophrenic

schizophrenic; **schizoid**. Each of these words can function as both adjective \(= characterized by schizophrenia\) and noun \(= a person with schizophrenia\). But both words are most often adjectives, and *schizophrenic* is the more common term. If any difference exists, it's that a *schizoid* \(or *schizoid personality*\) is someone who is seclusive, shut in, and unsociable, whereas a *schizophrenic* \(or *schizophrenic personality*\) has a serious psychological disorder involving greater dissociation between the intellect and the emotions.

The words are pronounced /skit-sә-**fren**-ik/ and /**skit**-soyd/.
