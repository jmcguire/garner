# specialty

specialty; **speciality**. *Specialty* has been the predominant form in AmE since the 1700s. In BrE, *speciality* predominated from the 1870s to about 2005—but now *specialty* is once again on the rise. It appears that *specialty* is becoming the standard term worldwide.

Current ratio in print \(*specialty* vs. *speciality*\): 6:1
