# splendid

splendid; **splendiferous**; **splendorous**; **\*splendrous**. *Splendid* is the ordinary choice of these words meaning "distinguished, illustrious, out-of-the-ordinary" <a splendid achievement>. *Splendiferous* is usually a comic or colloquial equivalent <the show featured dazzling dancers and splendiferous special effects>. *Splendorous*, meaning "brilliant, magnificent, glorious," is a seriously enthusiastic word <the Republicans celebrated by throwing a splendorous inaugural ball>. *\*Splendrous* is a variant of *splendorous*.

Current ratio in print \(*splendorous* vs. \**splendrous*\): 13:1
