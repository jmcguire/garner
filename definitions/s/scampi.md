# scampi

scampi \(= large prawns\) is an Italian loanword dating in English from the 1920s. Though itself plural, the word is considered a mass noun when it refers to a dish that includes large prawns <I ordered the scampi *or* I ordered scampi>. Though common, the redundancy *\*shrimp scampi* is best avoided.

Current ratio in print \(*the scampi* vs. *\*the shrimp scampi*\): 3:1
