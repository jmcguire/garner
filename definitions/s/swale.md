# swale

swale \(= a depression in the land\), an Americanism, is sometimes misspelled *\*swail*—e.g.: "Mickelson . . . played it safe at 13 and banked his tee shot off the right fringe. ‘It caught the right *swail* \[read *swale*\] and caught the bottom of the cup,' Mickelson said." Melanie Hauser, "TPC Leaves Players Amazed at High Scores," *Houston Post*, 25 Mar. 1995, at B5.

## Language-Change Index

 - *swale* misspelled *\*swail*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print \(*swale* vs. \**swail*\): 264:1
