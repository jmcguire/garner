# sequined

sequined /**seek**-wind/ \(= ornamented with sequins\), originally a gallicism \(1617\) dating as a past-participial adjective from the late 1800s, has always been predominantly so written. \**Sequinned* is a chiefly BrE variant.

Current ratio in print \(*sequined* vs. \**sequinned* in World Englishes\): 6:1
