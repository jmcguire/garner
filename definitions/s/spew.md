# spew

spew \(= to gush or vomit\) is sometimes misspelled *\*spue*—e.g.: "The enemy must be loathed where it is not feared, and let the consequent emotions *spue* \[read *spew*\] where they may." Hugo Young, "The Phobia That Lies Behind the Sneers," *Guardian*, 11 Mar. 1997, at 15.

## Language-Change Index

*spew* misspelled *\*spue*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*spewed out* vs. \**spued out*\): 42:1
