# sylvan

sylvan \(= of, relating to, or living in the woods\) has always been the predominant spelling, from the 1600s on. Fowler's 1926 objection to it on etymological grounds looks quirky today, given that the Latin word for *wood* could be spelled either *silva* or *sylva*. *\*Silvan* is a variant spelling that has never had much currency in English print sources.

 - Current ratio in print \(*sylvan* vs. \**silvan*\): 14:1
