# somersault

somersault; \***somerset**; **\*summersault**. *Somersault* is the standard spelling in AmE and BrE alike—preferred not only over the two listed, but also over *\*somersaut*, *\*somersalt*, *\*summerset*, and other odd variations.

 - Current ratio in print (*somersault* vs. \**somerset* vs. \**summersault* vs. \**summerset*): 46:3:2.2:1
