# syringe

syringe, vb. (= to inject or remove by means of a syringe \[the instrument having a needle and a hollow plastic tube\]), for the present participle, makes *syringing*, not *\*syringeing*. So the inflected form is analogous to *cringing*, *fringing*, *hinging*, *lunging*, and *tinging*—not to *bingeing* and *whingeing*. (See the entries for those other words.)

 - Current ratio in print (*syringing* vs. *\*syringeing*): 264:1
