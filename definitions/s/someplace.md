# someplace

someplace for *somewhere* is nonstandard dialect in BrE—and a casualism in AmE speech. This word first surged into popular use in AmE in the 1930s—and it has steadily increased ever since. Having originated in the late 1800s, it began seriously spreading in the mid-1900s. Cf. \***anyplace** & **\*noplace**.

 - Current ratio in print (*somewhere* vs. *someplace*): 22:1
