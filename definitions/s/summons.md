# summons

summons. Pl. *summonses* \(in AmE and BrE alike\). E.g.: "Littering is illegal, yet smokers seem strangely immune to the threat of *summonses* or public rebuke for their thoughtless actions." Letter of Alexander B. Grannis, "Smokers Litter Our Streets Without a Thought," *N.Y. Times*, 31 May 1995, at A20.

Current ratio in print \(*these summonses* vs. *these summons*\): 1.8:1
