# secrete

secrete = \(1\) to hide; or \(2\) to exude or ooze through pores or glands; to produce by secretion. Although *secrete away* is technically redundant, it avoids the possible miscue from the conflicting senses <then, in a frenzy, he secreted away all the contraband drugs>. Sense 1 is increasingly confined to literary and legal contexts.
