# synthesize

synthesize; \***synthetize**. Although H.W. Fowler called the latter spelling "the right formation" \(*FMEU1* at 593\), *synthesize* has now fully taken hold, and *\*synthetize* should be considered a needless variant. It has never been more than a linguistic cipher in either AmE or BrE.

 - Current ratio in print \(*synthesize* vs. \**synthetize*\): 108:1
