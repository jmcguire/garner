# sulfide

sulfide \(= a mixture of sulfur with some other substance\), a noun dating from the 1830s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since the 1930s. The older spelling, which long predominated in BrE, is *sulphide*. But since about 2012 *sulfide* has become prevalent even in BrE.

Current ratio in print \(*sulfide* vs. *sulphide* in AmE\): 6:1   
Current ratio in print \(*sulfide* vs. *sulphide* in BrE\): 1.8:1
