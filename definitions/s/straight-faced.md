# straight-faced

straight-faced. So spelled—not *\*strait-faced*. Perhaps by false analogy to *straitlaced*, some writers get it wrong—e.g.: "The splendour of the frocks and hats would shock this *strait-faced* \[read *straight-faced*\] economist." "Paying the High Price for Conspicuous Consumption," *Canberra Times*, 6 Nov. 2007, at 17. Cf. **straitlaced**.
