# steamroll

steamroll; **\*steamroller**, v.t. Although *\*steamroller* was once considered the standard verb, *steamroll* surpassed it in the early 1990s: it's now twice as common in print. E.g.: "From there, Levens *steamrolled* on three runs and a reception, scoring from the 3." Don Pierson, "Packers 27, Vikings 11," *Chicago Trib.*, 2 Dec. 1997, Sports §, at 1.

Current ratio in print \(*steamrolled* vs. *\*steamrollered*\): 2.2:1
