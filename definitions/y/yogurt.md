# yogurt

yogurt; **yoghurt**. The Turkish loanword *yogurt* \(= a thick cultured dairy product\) is so spelled. *Yoghurt*, a variant spelling common \(but not dominant\) in BrE, is rare in AmE. In fact, *yogurt* is more than 15 times as common as *yoghurt* in American print sources. *\*Yoghourt* \(predominant in BrE only during the 1950s\) and *\*yogourt* are likewise variant spellings best avoided.

Current ratio in print \(*yogurt* vs. *yoghurt* in World Englishes\): 3:1
