# resilience

resilience \(= \[1\] the ability of a substance such as a resin to return to its original shape after it has been beaten or pressed; or \[2\] the ability to become strong, successful, or happy again after a difficult or trying circumstance\) has been the standard noun since the 1700s. *\*Resiliency* is a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*resilience* vs. \**resiliency*\): 11:1
