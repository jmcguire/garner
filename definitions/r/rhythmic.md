# rhythmic

rhythmic; **\*rhythmical**. H.W. Fowler said, "Both forms are too common to justify any expectation of either's disappearance" \(*FMEU1* at 506\). But he did think that *\*rhythmical* is the more ordinary term. In fact, though, *rhythmic* \(the less rhythmic word\) has outnumbered *\*rhythmical* in print sources since 1890 by a wide margin. Cf. **eurythmics**.

Current ratio in print \(*rhythmic* vs. \**rhythmical*\): 9:1
