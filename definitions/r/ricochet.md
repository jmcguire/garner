# ricochet

ricochet, vb., makes *ricocheted* /**rik**-ә-shayd/ and *ricocheting* /**rik**-ә-shay-ing/ in AmE. Those are the preferred forms as well in BrE, which also has the variants *\*ricochetted* /**rik**-ә-shet-әd/ and *\*ricochetting* /**rik**-ә-shet-ing/. The *-tt-* form was superseded in AmE by 1930 and in BrE by 1950.

Current ratio in print \(*ricocheted* vs. *\*ricochetted*\): 32:1
