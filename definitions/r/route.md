# route

route is pronounced either /root/ or /rowt/. For quite some time, pronunciation specialists have heavily favored /root/, the only BrE pronunciation. But everywhere, even in BrE, people refer to computer *routers* as /**row**-tәrz/. Yet Americans who say that they're planning a cross-country route (/rowt/) would surely also say Route (/root/) 66. Cf. **en route**. For a misusage of *route*, see **root around**.
