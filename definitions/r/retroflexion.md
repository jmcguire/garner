# retroflexion

retroflexion \(= \[1\] the state or condition of being bent back; \[2\] the act or process of bending back; or \[3\] the articulation of a speech sound with the tip of the tongue curled back or turned up under the hard palate\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the early 1700s, has always been so spelled. **Retroflection* is a variant spelling. The term is more heavily influenced by the anatomical term *flexion* than by more abstract, less physical terms such as *inflection* and *reflection*. See **flexion**. Cf. **inflection** & **reflection**.
