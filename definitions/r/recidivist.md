# recidivist

recidivist, n. & adj.; **\*recidivistic**; **\*recidivous**. *Recidivist* can be both a noun \(meaning "a habitual criminal"\) and an adjective \(meaning "habitually criminal"\) <recidivist behavior> <recidivist offenders>. The adjectives \**recidivistic* and \**recidivous* are now needless variants.

 - Current ratio in print \(*recidivist offenders* vs. *\*recidivistic offenders*\): 9:1
