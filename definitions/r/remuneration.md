# remuneration

remuneration. So spelled. *\*Renumeration* is an all-too-common misspelling and mispronunciation—e.g.:

• "Joseph Cammarata . . . said he . . . would recommend rejecting any settlement that did not include financial *renumeration* \[read *remuneration*\] for Jones." Robert Kilborn & Lance Carden, "News in Brief," *Christian Science Monitor*, 2 June 1997, at 2.

• "I'm at school at 6:30 a.m., work through my lunch hour and prep period \(tutoring, revising essays with students, giving makeup tests, and/or grading papers—all without ‘extra' *renumeration* \[read *remuneration*\], by the way\), and I leave school anywhere between 3:30 and 5:30 p.m., depending on after-school meetings and student appointments." Cindy Haworth, "Nurse's Notion Is Fallacy," *Ariz. Republic*, 15 July 1997, at E7.

See metathesis.
