# refusenik

refusenik \(= \[1\] a Soviet or Russian citizen, esp. a Jewish one, denied permission to emigrate; or \[2\] someone who refuses or declines something\)—a 1970s English compound formed with the Russian suffix *nik*—has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Refusnik* is a variant.

Current ratio in print \(*refusenik* vs. \**refusnik*\): 24:1
