# ratatouille

ratatouille (= a spiced vegetable stew made of tomatoes, eggplant, zucchini, squash, onions, peppers, etc.), a French loanword dating in English from the early 1800s, is pronounced /rat-ә-**too**-ee/. If there was any serious contention by the variant pronunciation /rah-tә-**too**-ee/, it was ended in 2007 by the popular animated film *Ratatouille*, in which Remy the rat (/rat/) cooked up a critically acclaimed dish of *ratatouille*. In the popular mind, there is no rival pronunciation.
