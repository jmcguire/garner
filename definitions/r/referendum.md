# referendum

referendum. Pl. *referendums* or *referenda*. In modern AmE journalistic sources, *referendums* is four times as common, but in AmE books, *referenda* predominates. In BrE books and journalism, the homegrown *referendums* has been dominant since about 2000. See plurals (b).

 - Current ratio in print (*referenda* vs. *referendums* in AmE): 1.1:1
 - Current ratio in print (*referendums* vs. *referenda* in BrE): 2:1
