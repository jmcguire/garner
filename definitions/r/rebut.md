# rebut

rebut; **refute**. *Rebut* means "to attempt to refute." *Refute* means "to defeat (an opponent's arguments)." Hence someone who *rebuts* certainly hopes to *refute*; it is immodest to assume, however, that one has *refuted* another's arguments. *Rebut* is sometimes misspelled *\*rebutt*. See **refute**.
