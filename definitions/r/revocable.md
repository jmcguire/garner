# revocable

revocable; \***revokable**. The first has been vastly predominant since the 1500s; it is preferably pronounced /**rev**-ә-kә-bәl/, even though /ri-**voh**-kә-bәl/ is often heard. *\*Revokable* (as well as *\*revokeable*) is a needless variant. See **irrevocable**.

 - Current ratio in print (*revocable* vs. \**revokable*): 109:1
