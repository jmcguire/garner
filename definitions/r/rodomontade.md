# rodomontade

rodomontade; \***rhodomontade**. This word, meaning "boastful talk" and pronounced /rod-ә-mәn-**tahd**/ or /-**tayd**/, has been predominantly spelled *rodomontade* since about 1918. The *rh*- spelling was characteristic of the period from 1790 to 1918.

 - Current ratio in print (*rodomontade* vs. *\*rhodomontade*): 1:1
