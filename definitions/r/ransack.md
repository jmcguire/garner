# ransack

ransack (= to search thoroughly, esp. for loot; pillage) is occasionally misspelled *\*ramsack*—e.g.: "After Kentland was *ramsacked* \[read *ransacked*\] by Union troops during the Civil War, Kent rebuilt Kentland." Robert Freis, "Grand Land with a History as Rich as Its Soil," *Roanoke Times*, 10 Oct. 1995, at 8.

## Language-Change Index

 - *ransacked* misspelled *\*ramsacked*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*ransacked* vs. \**ramsacked*): 2,290:1
