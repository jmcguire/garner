# redress

redress, n. & vb. The noun *redress* \(= \[1\] the means of correcting a wrong; or \[2\] the act of correcting something wrong or unfair\) is pronounced /**ree**-dres/ in AmE but /ri-**dres**/ in BrE. The verb *redress* \(= \[1\] to set right, rectify, or remedy; or \[2\] to compensate or make up for\) is uniformly pronounced /ri-**dres**/ in standard english. On *redress* vs. *re-dress*, see re- pairs.
