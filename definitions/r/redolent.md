# redolent

redolent (= \[1\] strongly aromatic, scented; or \[2\] evocative or suggestive) is pronounced /**red**-ә-lәnt/—not /ri-**dohl**-әnt/. When a preposition follows the word, idiom requires *of* \<a potpourri redolent of lilacs\> \<an experience redolent of my boyhood\>.

 - Current ratio in print (*redolent of* vs. \**redolent with*): 3:1
