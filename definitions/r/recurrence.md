# recurrence

recurrence; \***recurrency**; **reoccurrence**. *Recurrence* refers to a repeated occurrence, especially at fairly regular intervals. *\*Recurrency* is a needless variant. *Reoccurrence* refers to another occurrence of something, with no suggestion that the thing happens repeatedly or at more or less regular intervals.

 - Current ratio in print (*recurrence* vs. \**recurrency*): 1,203:1
