# rhyme royal

rhyme royal; **rime royale**; **\*rhyme royale**. *Rhyme royal* \(= a seven-line stanza in iambic pentameter, with the rhyme scheme *ababbcc*\) is standard in both AmE and BrE. *Rime royale* \(sometimes anglicized as *rime royal*\) is the standard French phrase. *\*Rhyme royale* is a mistaken mixture of the two.

Current ratio in print \(*rhyme royal* vs. *rime royal* vs. \**rhyme royale*\): 64:16:1
