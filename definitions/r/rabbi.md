# rabbi

rabbi; \***rabbin**. *Rabbi*, the standard term, forms the plural *rabbis*—not *\*rabbies*. *\*Rabbin*, an archaic form, is a needless variant—except that it gives us the adjective *rabbinic*. We also have the cognate noun *rabbinate* \(= \[1\] the position, tenure, or office of a rabbi; or \[2\] collectively, a body of rabbis\).
