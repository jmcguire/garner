# revolutionary

revolutionary, n.; \***revolutionist**, n. Although *\*revolutionist* is nearly as old in English \(1710 in this sense\), it is now merely a needless variant of *revolutionary* \(ca. 1694\), which decisively overthrew its rival during the first half of the 20th century.

 - Current ratio in print \(*a revolutionary is* vs. *\*a revolutionist is*\): 5:1
