# ritardando

ritardando \(= a musical direction to slacken the tempo gradually\) predominantly makes the plural *ritardandos* in AmE, but the Italianate *ritardandi* in BrE. Oddly enough, the synonymous *rallentando* predominantly makes *rallentandos* in AmE and BrE alike—not *\*rallentandi*. See plurals \(b\).
