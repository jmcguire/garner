# repressor

repressor \(= \[1\] a person or institution that represses; or \[2\] a protein that prevents the cellular synthesis of a particular enzyme\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the late 1500s, has long been predominantly so spelled. **Represser* is a variant. See -er \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*repressor* vs. \**represser*\): 306:1
