# raccoon

raccoon; **\*racoon**. The animal is North American, and the AmE spelling is *raccoon*. The traditional BrE spelling—a variant in AmE—is \**racoon*. But since about 1900 the AmE spelling has become predominant also in BrE. \(Unsurprisingly, the Beatles got it right in their 1968 song "Rocky Raccoon."\) *Raccoon* should be considered standard in all varieties of English.

 - Current ratio in print \(*raccoon* vs. \**racoon* in World Englishes\): 21:1
