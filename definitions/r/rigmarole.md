# rigmarole

rigmarole (= a senselessly cumbersome, hassle-filled procedure) is the standard spelling. *\*Rigamarole* is a variant spelling that is less than half as common in print. Despite its spelling, *rigmarole* is often pronounced /**rig**-ә-mә-rohl/, though the dictionaries record /**rig**-mә-rohl/.

 - Current ratio in print (*rigmarole* vs. \**rigamarole*): 16:1
