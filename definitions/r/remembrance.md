# remembrance

remembrance (= \[1\] an instance of remembering or being remembered; \[2\] the power of someone's memory; \[3\] a particular memory or recollection; \[4\] the duration of time over which someone can remember; \[5\] a keepsake or memento; or \[6\] a commemoration or memorial) is pronounced in three syllables: /ri-**mem**-brәnts/. An epenthetic schwa sound shouldn't occur after the /b/ sound.
