# residue

residue; **residuum**; **residual**, n.; **residuary**, n. Both *residue* and *residuum* mean "that which remains." *Residue* is the usual and preferred term for most contexts. *Residuum* is a technical term used correctly in chemical contexts. *Residual*, n., = (1) a remainder; an amount still remaining after the main part of something is subtracted or taken into account; (2) a disability or discomfort that lingers after an illness or injury; or (3) additional pay given to a performer whose work has been recorded for replay—usually plural in sense 3. *Residuary*, n., is a needless variant except in legal contexts. See **residual**.
