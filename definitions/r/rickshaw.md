# rickshaw

rickshaw \(= a two-wheeled passenger vehicle pulled by someone on foot or riding an attached bicycle\), an altered Japanese loanword dating in English from the late 1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled. **Ricksha* and \**jinricksha* are variants that were common before 1900.

Current ratio in print \(*rickshaw* vs. \**ricksha* vs. \**jinricksha*\): 208:6:1
