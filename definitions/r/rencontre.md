# rencontre

rencontre; **rencounter**. The dictionaries provide little certainty about these words. *W11* lists the main entry under *rencontre*; the *COD* lists the main entry under *rencounter*, as does *W2*. Under *rencontre*, the *COD* labels both spellings archaic, although the Merriam-Webster dictionaries list *rencontre* as current in the senses \(1\) "a hostile meeting or contest between forces or individuals; combat"; and \(2\) "a casual meeting." The *OED* adds the sense "an organized but informal meeting of scientists," dating from 1975 in BrE.

Given the contradictory nature of \(1\) combat and \(2\) casual meeting, the term might be considered a contronym.

The empirical evidence shows that although the spelling *rencounter* once predominated in AmE and BrE alike, since 1820 or so *rencontre* \(a revived gallicism\) has been the predominant spelling in both varieties and for all senses.

 - Current ratio in print \(*rencontre* vs. *rencounter*\): 5:1
