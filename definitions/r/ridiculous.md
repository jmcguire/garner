# ridiculous

ridiculous has moved a long way from its etymological suggestion of "causing laughter," so that people nowadays often call *ridiculous* what causes them anger, frustration, distress, or even sadness. In other words, by slipshod extension it is frequently used when people are far from laughing. In slang, *ridiculous* has evolved even further to mean, variously, "unbelievable," "memorable," or "excellent." So we're far beyond the point of realistically insisting on etymological rigor with this word. For the sense "causing laughter," *risible* is sometimes the better term. See **risible**.
