# rent, vb

rent, vb.; **lease**, vb. In AmE, these terms are used both for what the tenant does and for what the landlord does. In BrE, the lessor *leases* \(or *lets*\) and the lessee *rents*. So in AmE, *rent* is ambiguous since it may refer to the action taken by either party. The word has had this double sense from at least the 1500s. Both the lessee and the lessor are *renters*, so to speak, though this term is usually reserved for tenants. See **lease**.
