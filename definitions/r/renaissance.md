# renaissance

renaissance \(= \[1\] a rebirth; or \[2\] \[capitalized\] the European period from the 1400s to the 1600s, when art, literature, and scientific ideas blossomed\) is pronounced /**ren**-i-sahns/ in AmE and /ri-**nay**-sәns/ in BrE. The pronunciation /ren-ә-**sah*n*s**/ is a pedantic affectation that makes the word into a pretentious gallicism.
