# retractable

retractable; \***retractible**. The word wasn't used much till the 1920s, and since then there has been no serious competition between the spellings: *retractable* is vastly predominant in AmE and BrE alike—hence standard. See -able \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*retractable* vs. \**retractible*\): 366:1
