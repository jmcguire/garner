# rapture

rapture is susceptible to a blunder analogous to the one that occurs with *rapt*. That is, some writers make it *\*wrapture*. Sometimes, of course, it's simply a facetious formation—e.g.: "Whatever you do, don't forget the glue gun, the real key to true *wrapture*." Kathy Legg, "Inspirations for Wrapping It All Up," *Wash. Post*, 7 Dec. 1995, at T12. But uses of that kind—and the fact that some women's apparel stores call themselves *Wrapture*—are likely to cause some popular confusion in years ahead.
