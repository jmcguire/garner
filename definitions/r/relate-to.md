# relate to

relate to \(= to empathize with; to understand because of one's own experience\) is a voguish expression characteristic of popular American cant dating from the 1970s on <Southern writers can relate to what it's like growing up in Georgia>. The phrasing, dating back to the 1940s, has given us the equally trendy adjective *relatable* \(from the 1960s\), meaning "arousing favorable emotions, especially empathy." These usages are losing any stigma they once had. Cf. **identify**.
