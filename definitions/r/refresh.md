# refresh

refresh \(= \[1\] to renew or replenish; \[2\] to stimulate \[the memory\]; or \[3\] to make \[somebody\] less tired or hot\), a French loanword dating in English from the 1300s, has always been the standard verb. **Refreshen*, dating from the mid-1600s, is a needless variant.

Current ratio in print \(*refreshed* vs. \**refreshened*\): 1,562:1
