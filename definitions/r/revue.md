# revue

revue \(= a musical show\) is so spelled. Avoid the erroneous variant *review* in this sense—e.g.: "Grapevine's Runway Theatre is performing ‘Leader of the Pack,' a musical *review* \[read *revue*\] of popular songs of the 1960s written by songwriter Ellie Greenwich." "Arts Roundup: For a Limited Time," *Dallas Morning News*, 24 July 1997, at G5.

Language-Change Index

*review* misused for *revue*: Stage 1
