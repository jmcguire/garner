# revisionary

revisionary; \***revisional**; **revisory**; **revisionist**. *Revisionary* = of, relating to, or made up of revision <revisionary methods>. *\*Revisional* is a needless variant. *Revisory* = having power to revise; engaged in revision <a revisory board>. *Revisionist* = having the purpose of changing the conventional beliefs about something, esp. in a political way.
