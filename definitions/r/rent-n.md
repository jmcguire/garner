# rent, n

rent, n.; **rental**, n. Generally, prefer *rent* instead of the noun *rental* whenever it will suffice. Reserve *rental* for a record of rent payments received <the rentals held in the Grosvenor Estate archives were incomplete> or the property itself <he owns only one small rental>.

Of course, *rental* is also an adjective—and has been since the 1400s <rental properties>. A U.S. sign is likely to say *rental cars*, while a U.K. sign is likely to say *car rentals*.
