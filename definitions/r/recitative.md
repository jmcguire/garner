# recitative

recitative, n. & adj., is preferably pronounced /res-ә-tә-**teev**/ as a noun \(meaning "a type of vocal music delivered in a declamatory style"\) and /ri-**sit**-ә-tiv/ or /ri-**si**-tә-tiv/ as an adjective \(meaning "narrative or repetitive"\). See Victor Fell Yellin, "The Pronunciation of ‘Recitative,'" 43 *Am. Speech* 291–96 \(1968\).
