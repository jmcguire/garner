# retina

retina (= the back-of-the-eye membrane that receives light and sends an image of what has been seen to the brain) has predominantly made the plural *retinas* since the 1950s in AmE and since the 1980s in BrE. Before that, the Latinate plural *retinae* /**ret**-әn-ee/ was prevalent in all varieties of English. See plurals (b).

 - Current ratio in print (*retinas* vs. *retinae*): 6:1
