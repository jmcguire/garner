# ratiocination

ratiocination; **rationalization**. *Ratiocination* /rash-ee-os-ә-**nay**-shәn/ = the process or an act of reasoning. *Rationalization* = (1) an act or instance of explaining (away) by bringing into conformity with reason; or (2) (colloq.) the finding of "reasons" for irrational or unworthy behavior. Sense 2 is responsible for the negative connotations of the word.
