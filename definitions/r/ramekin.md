# ramekin

ramekin \(= a small dish in which a garnish or a small portion of food can be served or cooked\), a French/Dutch loanword dating in English from the mid-1600s, has been predominantly so spelled since about 1895 in AmE and 1905 in BrE. The variant spelling \**ramequin*, identical with the French etymon, was the earlier standard.

Current ratio in print \(*ramekin* vs. \**ramequin*\): 136:1
