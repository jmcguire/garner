# ribald

ribald (= of, relating to, or involving coarse, sexually suggestive or even sexually explicit humor or remarks) is pronounced /**rib**-әld/—not /**rī**-bahld/ or /ri-**bahld**/. The corresponding noun *ribaldry* (= the quality, state, or fact of being ribald—or an instance of it) is /**rib**-әl-dree/.
