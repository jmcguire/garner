# redressable

redressable. So spelled in AmE and BrE alike—preferably not *\*redressible*. But this represents a change in spelling from the 18th-century standard \(-*ible*\), which persisted into the 20th century. *Redressable* became the predominant form in AmE in the 1940s and in BrE as recently as the 1980s. See -able \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*redressable* vs. \**redressible*\): 8:1
