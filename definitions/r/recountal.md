# recountal

recountal \(= a narration or recital\) is the noun corresponding to the verb *recount* \(as opposed to the verb *re-count* \[= to count again\]\). It dates from the early 1800s. *\*Recountment* is an obsolete variant used by Shakespeare.

 - Current ratio in print \(*recountal* vs. \**recountment*\): 63:1
