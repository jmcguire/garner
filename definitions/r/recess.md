# recess

recess, n. & vb. There is a tendency to pronounce this word /**ree**-ses/ in all senses and uses. But sticklers formerly made distinctions. The noun *recess* was pronounced /ri-**ses**/ in reference either to an alcove or niche in the wall of a room or to a secluded place where things are hidden. It was pronounced /**ree**-ses/ for a brief period of rest, play, or inactivity between sessions, lessons, etc. The verb *recess* \(= \[1\] to place within an indentation; \[2\] to make an indentation in; or \[3\] to take a break\) was pronounced /ri-**ses**/ in standard english.
