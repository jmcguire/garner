# right-of-way

right-of-way is hyphenated whether used as a phrasal adjective <a right-of-way easement> or as a noun <yield the right-of-way>. The plural has always been *rights-of-way*, not *\*right-of-ways*. See plurals (g).

 - Current ratio in print (*rights-of-way* vs. \**right-of-ways*): 10:1
