# revise

revise; **redact**; **recense**. The first is the ordinary word. The second and third refer specifically to revising texts with close scrutiny. *Redact* = (1) to make a draft of; or (2) to edit. In law, it is often used in the sense "to edit out or mask the privileged, impertinent, or objectionable matter in a document." *Recense* is more of a literary term; it relates to scholarly editing of ancient texts and the like.
