# rustically

rustically (= in a rural, countrified manner) is the adverb corresponding to *rustic*, adj. But some misspell it *\*rusticly*, maybe on the analogy of *publicly*—e.g.: "If you haven't already guessed, Newport is not for the ‘*rusticly* \[read *rustically*\] challenged.' " Adam Mertz, "State of Seclusion," *Milwaukee J. Sentinel*, 14 Oct. 1994, at B5. See -ic/-ical.
