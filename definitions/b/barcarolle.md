# barcarolle

barcarolle \(= \[1\] a song traditionally sung by Venetian gondoliers, typically in 6/8 or 12/8 time, or \[2\] by metonymy, an Italian gondolier\), introduced in the 1700s, was originally \(until the 1880s\) spelled *barcarole*. Since about 1885, the predominant spelling has been *barcarolle* \(after the French spelling\).

Current ratio in print \(*barcarolle* vs. *barcarole*\): 2:1
Current ratio in print \(*Venetian barcarolles* vs. *Venetian barcaroles*\): 2:1
