# Burmese

Burmese; **Bamar**; **Burman**; **Myanmarese**; **Myanmaran**. For any inhabitant of Myanmar \(Burma\), or for more than one, the label *Burmese* is standard. *Bamar* and *Burman* denote a member of the country's dominant ethnic group, *Bamar* being the preferred term. *Burman* has become an outdated variant for *Burmese*—e.g.: "The *Burmans* \[read *Burmese*\], when they are addressing a superior, introduce a special particle, *taunin* or something similar, into the verb." Otto Jespersen, *Mankind, Nation, and Individual from a Linguistic Point of View* 130 \(1946\).

The variant *Myanmarese* appears in BrE sources, especially in Asia. *Myanmaran* is very rare but not unknown. See demonyms.

Current ratio in print \(*The Burmese are* vs. *The Burmans are*\): 5:1
