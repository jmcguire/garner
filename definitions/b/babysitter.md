# babysitter

babysitter \(= someone who temporarily cares for children while their parents or usual caregivers are unavailable\), a term popularized in the 1940s, was not predominantly solidified until the early 1970s. Before then, \**baby-sitter* and \**baby sitter* were common.

 - Current ratio in print \(*babysitter* vs. \**baby-sitter* vs. \**baby sitter*\): 29:1.3:1
