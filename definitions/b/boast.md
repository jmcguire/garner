# boast

boast, vb. In the sense "to have \(something desirable\)," this word is objectionable primarily on grounds of being a cliché <the town now boasts 300 retail merchants>. It has been standard english since the early 1800s. E.g.: "For decades, it \[*Buffalo News*\] *boasted* profit margins well over 30 percent." Margaret Sullivan, *Ghosting the News* 37 \(2020\).

Language-Change Index

*boast* as a transitive verb <Albuquerque boasts the largest airport in New Mexico>: Stage 5
