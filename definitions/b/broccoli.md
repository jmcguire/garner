# broccoli

broccoli (the crucifer plant resembling cauliflower but having greenish buds and bearing tender shoots), a term introduced in the 1600s, has been predominantly so spelled since the early 1820s. \**Brocoli* is a variant spelling that vied closely with *broccoli* from about 1760 to about 1860.

 - Current ratio in print (*broccoli* vs. \**brocoli*): 526:1
