# biennial

biennial = occurring every two years. If we climb the numerical ladder, we have *triennial* (3), *quadrennial* (4), *quinquennial* (5), *sexennial* (6), *septennial* (7), *octennial* (8), *novennial* (9), *decennial* (10), *vicennial* (20), *centennial* (100), *millennial* (1,000). Most of these words gained currency during the second half of the 1700s. See numerical prefixes; see also bi- & **biannual**.
