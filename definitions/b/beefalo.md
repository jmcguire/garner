# beefalo

beefalo \(= a breed of cattle that is part buffalo and part domestic bovine\) has two plurals: *beefalos* and *beefaloes*. Despite the standard plural of *buffaloes* \(see plurals \(d\)), the spelling *beefalos* has predominated in print since about 2000.

Current ratio in print \(*beefalos* vs. *beefaloes*\): 6:1
