# baba ghanoush

baba ghanoush /bah-bah gә-**noosh**/ \(= a Middle Eastern appetizer consisting of puréed eggplant, tahini, garlic, olive oil, and lemon\), an Arabic loanword dating in English from the early 1970s, is predominantly so spelled. Among the variant spellings are **baba ghanouj*, \**babaghanoush*, and \**babaganouj*.

Current ratio in print \(*baba ghanoush* vs. \**baba ghanouj* vs. \**babaghanoush* vs. \**babaganouj*\): 79:11:7:1
