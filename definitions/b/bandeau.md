# bandeau

bandeau \(= \[1\] a hairband; or \[2\] a tube-top wrap that functions like a brassiere\) has predominantly formed the plural *bandeaux* since the 1840s—before that *bandeaus* having been prevalent. Today the homegrown *bandeaus* is an infrequent variant in English-language sources.

Current ratio in print \(*bandeaux* vs. *bandeaus*\): 5:1
