# bok choy

bok choy; **pak choi**; \***pak choy**; **\*bok choi**; **bak choy**; **bak choi**. The name of the Chinese cabbage with long whitish stalks and green leaves is predominantly spelled *bok choy* /**bahk** choy/ across World Englishes—except in BrE, in which *pak choi* has predominated since about 2003. Introduced into English in the mid-1800s, the term did not become common until the 1970s—when it was predominantly spelled *bok choy* even in BrE.
