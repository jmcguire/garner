# beau geste

beau geste \(= \[1\] a generous or magnanimous gesture; or \[2\] a transparently empty gesture meant to seem generous or magnanimous\) suffers from an unfortunate ambiguity. Is the term laudatory or damning? In any event, its plural is *beaux gestes* \(pronounced, like the singular, /boh **zhest**/\)—not *\*beaus gestes*.
