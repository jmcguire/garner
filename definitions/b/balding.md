# balding

balding (= becoming bald), having emerged as a neologism in the early 20th century, was once objected to because there was thought to be no corresponding verb *to bald*. In fact, though, the verb dates from the early 1600s and still occasionally appears <he's starting to bald>. There is no sound reason for objecting to *balding* (the word, that is, though many regret and try to reverse the reality it denotes).
