# biannual

biannual; **biennial**; **semiannual**. *Biannual* and *semiannual* both mean "occurring twice a year." *Biennial* means "occurring once every two years." The distinction becomes important, for example, when contractual language provides for "*biannual* increases" or "*biannual* meetings." The best advice is not to rely on words like *biannual* and *biennial* if confusion might cause problems. For absolute clarity, use *semiannual* or *once every two years*.
