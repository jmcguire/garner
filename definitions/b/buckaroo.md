# buckaroo

buckaroo \(= a cowboy or broncobuster, esp. in children's talk\) is a corrupted form of the Spanish loanword *vaquero*, dating from the early 1800s. *Buckaroo* has been predominantly so spelled since about 1910. Before that, \**buckeroo* was a common variant; it still sometimes appears.

 - Current ratio in print \(*buckaroo* vs. \**buckeroo*\): 34:1
