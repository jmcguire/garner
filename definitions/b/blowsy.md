# blowsy

blowsy \(= \[1\] \[of a person\] unkempt and sloppy; or \[2\] having a coarse and ruddy complexion\), a term introduced in the early 1700s, is predominantly so spelled. \**Blousy* and \**blowzy* are variant forms.

 - Current ratio in print \(*blowsy* vs. \**blousy* vs. \**blowzy*\): 4:2.3:1
