# burgeon

burgeon literally means "to put forth buds; sprout." Although some usage experts have considered the word objectionable in the sense "to flourish, grow," no good reason exists to avoid it in this figurative sense <the burgeoning middle class>. But the word preferably refers to growth at early stages, not to full-blown expansion.

A variant spelling is \**bourgeon*, which was fairly common until the 1880s.
