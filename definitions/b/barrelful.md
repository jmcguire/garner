# barrelful

barrelful (= \[1\] as much or as many as can fill a barrel; or \[2\] a great many, a lot), a compound dating in English from the 1300s, predominantly makes the plural *barrelfuls*, not \**barrelsful*. See plurals (g).

 - Current ratio in print (*barrelfuls* vs. \**barrelsful*): 1.1:1
