# byline

byline \(= a line of attribution for the writer at the outset of an article\), a compound dating from the early 20th century, has been predominantly so written \(as solid\) since the late 1990s. Before that, the term was predominantly hyphenated \(\**by-line*\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*byline* vs. \**by-line*\): 1.6:1
