# backhand

backhand, adj.; **backhanded**. Both adjectives are in heavy use. Normally *backhand* describes shots in tennis, badminton, etc. \<backhand stroke\>; it's the antonym of *forehand*. *Backhanded* describes indirect or sarcastic remarks \<backhanded compliment\>. But the demarcation between literal and figurative isn't entirely clean: *backhanded slap* is more common in print than *backhand slap*.

 - Current ratio in print (*backhand stroke* vs. \**backhanded stroke*): 5:1
 - Current ratio in print (*backhanded compliment* vs. \**backhand compliment*): 106:1
 - Current ratio in print (*backhanded slap* vs. *backhand slap*): 1.7:1
