# banal

banal (= pedestrian and uninteresting for lack of new ideas) is preferably pronounced /bә-**nahl**/, but in AmE the pronunciations /bә-**nal**/ and /**bay**-nәl/ are also common. It's "a word of many pronunciations, each of which has its outspoken and often intractable proponents." *BBBM* at 55.
