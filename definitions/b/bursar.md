# bursar

bursar \(= the person at a school or college who deals with accounts and office work\) is preferably pronounced /**bәr**-sәr/—not /**bәr**-sahr/—in AmE. In BrE, it's /**bә**-sә/. AmE is rhotic, so that a final *r* is enunciated, whereas in BrE it's silent.

Don't confuse *bursar* with its rhyming word *purser*, which denotes the chief administrative officer or head steward on a ship.
