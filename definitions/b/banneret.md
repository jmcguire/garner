# banneret

banneret /**ban**-ә-rәt/ or /ban-ә-**rәt**/ \(= a small banner—that is, a usu. rectangular piece of fabric bearing a message and often carried between two poles\), a French loanword dating in English from the 1300s, has always been predominantly so written. **Bannerette* is a variant. See diminutives \(f\); see also gallicisms.
