# beestings

beestings \(= the colostrum of a cow or other milk-producing farm animal\), an Old English noun dating from the 1100s, is predominantly so spelled. \**Beastings* and \**biestings* are variants.

If you're referring to the stings of a bee, hyphenate: *bee-stings*. It's important: nobody can say it's none of your beeswax.

Current ratio in print \(*beestings* vs. \**beastings* vs. \**biestings*\): 14:1.4:1
