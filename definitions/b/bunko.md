# bunko

bunko \(= a swindling trick or con game\), an Americanism dating from the late 1800s, has been predominantly so spelled since about 1987. Before that, *bunco* was the predominant spelling. Although etymologists believe that the term was probably adapted from the Spanish *banca* "card game or lottery," they aren't certain.

 - Current ratio in print \(*bunko* vs. *bunco*\): 1.03:1
