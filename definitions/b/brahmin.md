# Brahmin

Brahmin; **Brahman**; **Brahma**. With reference to people, the spelling *Brahmin* (or *Brahman*) denotes (1) the highest caste or a member of that caste in classical Indian society; or (2) a member of upper-class New England society. *Brahmin*, the older spelling, is preferred <a Brahmin holy man> <a Boston Brahmin>. The popular breed of cattle originating in India are *Brahman* or *Brahma* cattle. The males are predominantly *Brahma bulls*, whereas the females may be *Brahma* or *Brahman cows*.

 - Current ratio in print (*Brahma bulls* vs. *Brahman bulls*): 1.1:1
 - Current ratio in print (*Brahman cattle* vs. *Brahma cattle*): 5:1
