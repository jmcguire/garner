# banderole

banderole \(= \[1\] a ribbonlike scroll bearing an inscription; or \[2\] a long narrow flag, streamer, or pennant attached to a pole or lance\), a French loanword dating in English from the 1500s, has been predominantly so spelled since the mid-1800s. **Banderol* and \**bannerol* are variants. See gallicisms.
