# bicorne

bicorne; **bicorn**; **bicornuate**. These adjectives describe something having two horns or hornlike parts, usually in the form of a crescent. *Bicorne* is most common <bicorne hat>; *bicorn* might be classified as a needless variant; *bicornuate* /bī-**korn**-yә-wit/ is usually a medical or anatomical term <bicornuate uterus>.

Just as *tricorne* traditionally denotes a cocked hat with three corners, *bicorne* is the standard noun denoting a cocked hat with the brim turned up on two sides. Cf. **tricorne**.
