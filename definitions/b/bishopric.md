# bishopric

bishopric; **diocese**; see. These theological terms are closely related, even overlapping, but they can be treated as distinct. A *bishopric* is the position or rank of a bishop (or the district that the bishop is in charge of). A *diocese* is the district that a bishop is in charge of—in other words, identical with the alternative sense of *bishopric*. A *see* is a bishop's seat of authority or jurisdictional headquarters. See **diocese**.
