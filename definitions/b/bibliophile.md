# bibliophile

bibliophile (= a book lover; esp., a book collector), introduced in the early 1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled. *\*Bibliophilist* /bib-lee-**of**-i-list/, \**bibliophiliac*, and \**bibliophil* are variant forms. An extreme *bibliophile* is known as a *bibliomaniac*.

 - Current ratio in print (*bibliophile* vs. \**bibliophilist* vs. \**bibliophiliac* vs. \**bibliophil*): 1,326:140:2:1
