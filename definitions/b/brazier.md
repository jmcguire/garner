# brazier

brazier /**bray**-zhәr/ (= \[1\] an artisan who works with brass; or \[2\] a metal container designed to hold fire and provide heat in cold weather), a word dating back to Middle English, has long been predominantly so spelled. \**Brasier* is a variant.

For a humorous story involving this word, see **brassiere**.

 - Current ratio in print (*brazier* vs. \**brasier*): 30:1
