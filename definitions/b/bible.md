# Bible

Bible; **bible**. As a proper noun referring to the holy book, *Bible* is capitalized <which book of the Bible are you referring to?>. As a common noun denoting any definitive text, it should be lowercase <that cookbook is the chef's bible>. Since the 1960s, the adjective *biblical* has been predominantly lowercase, even when it refers to the Bible <the biblical account of the flood>.

Current ratio in print \(*biblical times* vs. *Biblical times*\): 4:1
