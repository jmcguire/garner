# bony

bony (= \[1\] consisting of bone; \[2\] full of bones; \[3\] having prominent bones; or \[4\] exceedingly thin), an adjective dating from the 1300s, has been predominantly so spelled throughout the period of Modern English. \**Boney* is a variant spelling.

 - Current ratio in print (*bony* vs. \**boney*): 44:1
