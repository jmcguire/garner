# biblicist

biblicist /**bib**-li-sist/ \(= \[1\] an expert on the Bible; or \[2\] someone who reads the Bible literally\), an agent noun dating from the early 1800s, has been the standard term since about 1930. Before that, \**biblist* was a close rival. The words are usually lowercase.
