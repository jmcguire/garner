# bell lyre

bell lyre (= a portable glockenspiel mounted on a lyre esp. for use in marching bands), a phrase dating only from the 1930s, has been the predominant form since the 1980s. \**Bell-lyra* and \**bell lyra* are variants formed on the Latin/Greek word *lyra*. But most band directors today don't use any form of this phrase, preferring instead the clipped form *glock*. Only time will tell whether the association of *glock* with the gun manufacturer of that name will dissuade people from using the term.
