# blindman's buff

blindman's buff \(= a game in which a blindfolded player tries to catch and identify any one of several other players\) is the traditional term; it dates from about 1600. But *blindman's bluff*, a 20th-century AmE variant, is now common and almost equally acceptable.

 - Current ratio in print \(*blindman's buff* vs. *blindman's bluff *\): 3:1
