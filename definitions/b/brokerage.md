# brokerage

brokerage; **brokage**. *Brokerage* = \(1\) the business or office of a broker <real-estate brokerage is a profession requiring knowledge and experience>; or \(2\) a broker's fee <brokerage differs from an underwriting commission>.

The obsolete *brokage* \(or *brocage*\) denoted primarily the payment of a bribe to get a job, especially an official position. It's also a needless variant of *brokerage*.
