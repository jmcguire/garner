# biennium

biennium \(= a period of two years\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the late 1600s, predominantly formed the anglicized plural *bienniums* from about 1915 to until the 1960s, when the Latinate *biennia* grew in frequency. Although the two have been rivals ever since, *biennia* has become predominant.

Current ratio in print \(*biennia* vs. *bienniums*\): 3:1
