# behemoth

behemoth \(= a huge, powerful animal\) is pronounced /bә-**hee**-mәth/. The word is sometimes misspelled \(and presumably mispronounced\) *\*behometh*—e.g.: "The other quarterfinals feature Lleyton Hewitt against Tommy Haas during the day today and Sweden's Joachim Johansson against Andy Roddick in a battle of big-serving, ace-loving *behomeths* \[read *behemoths*\] at night, weather permitting." Marc Berman, "Rain Men—Federer Leads Agassi by Set," *N.Y. Post*, 9 Sept. 2004, at 84.
