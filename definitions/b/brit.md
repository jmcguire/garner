# Brit

Brit, a colloquial shortening of *Briton* or *\*Britisher*, is recorded in the *OED* from 1884. As an adjective, it describes anyone or anything British. Unlike many other national and ethnic casualisms, it is arguably not pejorative. The earliest recorded use comes from Texas. See **Briton (a)**.

 - Current ratio in print (*Britons* vs. *Brits* vs. *\*Britishers*): 16:5:1
