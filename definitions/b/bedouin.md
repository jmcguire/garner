# Bedouin

Bedouin /**bed**-ә-wәn/ \(= one of the nomadic Arabs who traditionally lived in tents in the deserts of North Africa, the Arabian Peninsula, Iraq, and the Levant\), a French loanword dating in English from the 1400s, has always been so capitalized and spelled. **Beduin* is a variant. See gallicisms.

 - Current ratio in print \(*Bedouin* vs. \**Beduin*\): 59:1
