# beau

beau \(an old-fashioned word for a woman's boyfriend\) forms the plurals *beaus* and *beaux*, the second of which is predominant in both AmE and BrE. \(See plurals \(b\).) Perhaps because of its quaintness, the word has been very much on the wane since about 1950.

Current ratio in print \(*two beaux* vs. *two beaus*\): 2.2:1
