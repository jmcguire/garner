# Brussels sprout

Brussels sprout (= a small, edible bud akin to cabbage) is so spelled and capitalized—not *brussels sprout* and especially neither *\*brussel sprout* nor *\*Brussel sprout*.

 - Current ratio in print (*Brussels sprouts* vs. *brussels sprouts* vs. *\*Brussel sprouts* vs. *\*brussel sprouts*): 30:8:1.2:1
