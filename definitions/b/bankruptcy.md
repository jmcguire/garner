# bankruptcy

bankruptcy \[fr. L. *bancus* "table" \+ *ruptus* "broken"\] is sometimes misspelled *\*bankruptsy*—e.g.: "John Canney, *bankruptsy* \[read *bankruptcy*\] trustee for Ascutney, \[called\] Plausteiners' $1.1 million purchase ‘the bargain of the year, maybe of the decade.' " Tony Chamberlain, "Plausteiners Bid to Revive Ascutney," *Boston Globe*, 16 Dec. 1993, Sports §, at 99.
