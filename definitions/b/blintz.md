# blintz

blintz; **blintze**. American dictionaries are about equally divided between these two spellings for the name of a rolled pancake stuffed with cottage cheese or another filling. But the predominant spelling since the 1950s has been *blintz*, not the original Yiddish *blintze*.

 - Current ratio in print \(*blintz* vs. *blintze*\): 5:1
