# basophil

basophil /**bay**-sә-fil/ \(= a substance or structure that readily stains with basic dyes\), a technical term dating in English from the late 1800s, has been predominantly so spelled since the 1920s. Before that, the variant \**basophile* predominated.

Current ratio in print \(*basophil* vs. \**basophile*\): 31:1
