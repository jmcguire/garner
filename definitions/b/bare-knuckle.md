# bare-knuckle

bare-knuckle, adj.; **\*bare-knuckled**; **\*bareknuckle**; **\*bareknuckled**. When this compound adjective originated in the late 1800s, it described gloveless fistfights. By the 1950s, the term was being used figuratively <bare-knuckle politics>. The standard form has always been *bare-knuckle*, with the hyphen and without a final *d*.

 - Current ratio in print \(*bare-knuckle* vs. \**bare-knuckled* vs. \**bareknuckle* vs. \**bareknuckled*\): 23:6:6:1
