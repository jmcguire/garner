# burqa

burqa; **burka**; \***burkha**. *Burqa* \(= a loose garment that covers the whole head and body, with an open or veiled slit for vision, usu. worn by Muslim women\) is the predominant spelling in all varieties of English. *Burka* is a less common but acceptable variant. *\*Burkha* is a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*burqa* vs. *burka* vs. \**burkha*\): 14:7:1
