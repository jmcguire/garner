# bateau

bateau /ba-**toh**/ \(= a small, lightweight, flat-bottomed riverboat\), a French loanword dating in English from the early 1700s, has been predominantly so spelled in BrE since the 1820s and in AmE since the 1850s. The spelling **batteau* is, according to the *OED*, a "less correct" variant. The standard plural is *bateaux*—not \**bateaus*. See plurals \(b\); see also gallicisms.

 - Current ratio in print \(*bateau* vs. \**batteau*\): 5:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*the bateaux* vs. *the bateaus*\): 70:1
