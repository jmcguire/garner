# bandolier

bandolier (= a belt worn diagonally over the shoulder, often for suspending something such as ammunition), a French loanword dating in English from the 1500s, has been predominantly so spelled since about 1890. \**Bandoleer* is a variant that has been recorded as the main headword in many 20th- and 21st-century dictionaries—despite the contrary data. Cf. **pistoleer**. See gallicisms.

 - Current ratio in print (*bandolier* vs. \**bandoleer*): 4:1
