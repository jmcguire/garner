# benthic

benthic \(= of, relating to, or involving the bottom of a body of water, esp. a sea or ocean\), a Greek derivative dating in English from about 1900, is the usual adjective corresponding to *benthos* \(denoting all the plants and animals living down there\). The adjective is often used with nouns such as *invertebrates*, *organisms*, and *species*. \**Benthonic* is a variant.

Current ratio in print \(*benthic* vs. \**benthonic*\): 105:1
