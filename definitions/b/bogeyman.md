# bogeyman

bogeyman; **\*boogeyman**; **\*boogieman**; **\*bogyman**; **\*boogyman**. To denote either an imaginary monster that frightens children or someone who is seen as evil and unpleasant, *bogeyman* /**boh**-gee-man/ or, in AmE, /**bʊg**-ee-man/ has been the standard spelling since about 1900. For the decade before that \(the word was introduced about 1890\), the predominant spelling had been \**bogyman*.
