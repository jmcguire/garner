# baptistery

baptistery \(= \[1\] a place in or near a church devoted to baptisms, or \[2\] a tank or fountain inside a church for baptisms\), a word introduced in the 1300s, has been predominantly so spelled since the early 1700s. \**Baptistry* is a variant that reflects the properly syncopated pronunciation /**bap**-tis-tree/, in three syllables.

Current ratio in print \(*baptistery* vs. \**baptistry*\): 5:1
