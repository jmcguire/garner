# briar

briar \(= a wild bush having a thorny stem and prickly leaves\), an Old English word dating from about 1000, has been competing with the spelling *brier* since at least 1700. Only as recently as 1900 was there any real separation between the two terms in frequency of use—and even with that the pair are separated by only a small margin.
