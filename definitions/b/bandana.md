# bandana

bandana (= a large, colorful handkerchief) is an 18th-century borrowing from Hindi. In AmE, the spelling *bandanna* was long standard. In BrE, that spelling contended with *bandana* from the 1700s to the 1900s. Since the 1990s, the two spellings have vied closely in all varieties of English.

 - Current ratio in print (*bandana* vs. *bandanna* in World Englishes): 1.2:1
