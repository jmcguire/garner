# bowdlerize

bowdlerize \(= to remove all the passages in a book, play, etc. containing offensive and esp. sexual references\) is preferably pronounced /**bowd**-lә-rīz/— not /**bohd-**/. The term derives from the name of Thomas Bowdler \(1754–1825\), who expunged all the sexual and scatological references from his edition of Shakespeare's plays \(1818\). The verb has been predominantly lowercase since the late 1800s.
