# luster

luster \(= \[1\] a shiny, attractive appearance; or \[2\] an interesting or exciting quality\) has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since the 1930s. In BrE, *lustre* has always been standard. See -er \(b\).

Current ratio in print \(*lost its luster* vs. *lost its lustre* in AmE\): 11:1   
Current ratio in print \(*lost its lustre* vs. *lost its luster* in BrE\): 1.5:1
