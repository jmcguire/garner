# locknut

locknut \(= a fastener screwed tightly onto a bolt to prevent loosening\), a compound dating from the mid-1800s, has been predominantly so written, as solid, since the late 1960s. The earlier nonsolid forms, \**lock nut* and \**lock-nut*, are variants.

 - Current ratio in print \(*locknut* vs. \**lock nut* vs. \**lock-nut*\): 17:10:1
