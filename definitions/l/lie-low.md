# lie low

lie low; **lay low**. The latter phrase is common but loose. The two phrasings both appear in print, but the correct *lying low* is more common than *laying low*. See **lay \(b\)**.

Current ratio in print \(*lying low* vs. *laying low*\): 3:1
Current ratio in print \(*to lie low* vs. *to lay low*\): 1.1:1
