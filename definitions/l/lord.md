# Lord

Lord, a traditional term for the Christian God, has come under attack: "Some U.S. churches are beginning to question the title of Lord for Jesus Christ, reports the *Washington Post*, noting that the word is laden with negative meaning; some groups are replacing the title with gender-neutral terms such as Redeemer, Comforter or Friend. Rev. James Crawford of Boston's Old South Church said his United Church of Christ should be seeking ‘metaphors for Ultimate Reality that do not assume a cosmos or creation where . . . some male-like figure or being is in charge.' " "Religion Watch," *Globe & Mail* \(Toronto\), 24 Aug. 1993, at A16. Whether the term will continue to flourish or dwindle in use remains to be seen, but a long struggle seems assured. See sexism.
