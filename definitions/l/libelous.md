# libelous

libelous \(= constituting or involving a defamatory statement expressed in a fixed medium such as print or an electronic broadcast\), an adjective dating from the 1600s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since the 1880s. Before that, *libellous*, which still predominates in BrE, was universally the standard spelling.

Current ratio in print \(*libelous* vs. *libellous* in AmE\): 7:1
Current ratio in print \(*libellous* vs. *libelous* in BrE\): 3:1
