# Lucullan

Lucullan /loo-**kәl**-әn/ (= lavish, luxurious) has been the standard term since the 1700s. The term derives from the name Licinius Lucullus, a rich Roman consul and general who was famous for his banquets. *\*Lucullian* and *\*Lucullean* are variants.

 - Current ratio in print (*Lucullan* vs. *\*Lucullian* vs. *\*Lucullean*): 6:1.2:1
