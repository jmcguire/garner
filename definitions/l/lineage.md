# lineage

lineage; **linage**. The first is a common term meaning "ancestry." The second is \(1\) an uncommon term meaning "the number of lines of something \(such as a newspaper article\)"; or \(2\) a bowling term for the number of games played. Although most dictionaries give *lineage* as a variant of *linage*, in fact each word is occasionally confounded with the other. Yet the differentiation is well established.

*Linage* has two syllables: /**lī**-nij/. *Lineage* has three: /**lin**-ee-ij/.
