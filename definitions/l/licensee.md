# licensee

licensee (= someone who has received official, usu. written, permission to do something), a noun dating from the mid-1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Licencee* is a variant. In everyday BrE, *licence-holder* denotes the proprietor of a public house.

 - Current ratio in print (*licensee* vs. \**licencee*): 454:1
