# lyricist

lyricist; **lyrist**. A *lyricist* /**lir**-ә-sist/ writes lyrics. Eric Partridge erroneously called it a "rare word" \(*U&A* at 176\), but in fact it's quite common.

A *lyrist* /līr-ist/ plays the lyre. Although *lyrist* has occasionally been used as a synonym for *lyricist*, in that sense it's a needless variant.
