# libel

libel.

**A. Pronunciation.** Pronounce it /**lī**-bәl/. The word is often mispronounced, through epenthesis, the way *liable* /**lī**-ә-bәl/ is pronounced. See pronunciation.

**B. As a Verb.** *Libel* (= \[1\] to defame in a permanent medium such as written words; or \[2\] to sue in admiralty) preferably makes *libeled*, *libeling*, and *libeler* in AmE. The BrE forms *libelled*, *libelling*, and *libeller* predominate across World Englishes. For the noun sense, see **defamation**.
