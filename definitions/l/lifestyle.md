# lifestyle

lifestyle. The word is recorded in the modern sense from around 1915 \(though originally as two words or hyphenated\). When it was viewed \(wrongly\) as a neologism in the 1960s through the 1980s, it was often disparaged as an unnecessary innovation and as a vogue term. But today it seems serviceable as denoting a concept widely regarded as important: the way one or more people live, including where they live, the things they own, the work they do, and the activities they engage in.
