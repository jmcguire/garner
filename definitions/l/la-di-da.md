# la-di-da

la-di-da /**lah**-dee-**dah**/—an expression that, since the 1870s, has been used in mockery of pretension, affected elegance, or ostentatious refinement—is predominantly so spelled. Only boorish lowbrows spell it \**la-de-da*, \**lah-di-dah*, \**la-di-dah*, \**lah-de-dah*, \**la-dee-da*, \**la-dee-dah*, or \**lah-dee-dah*.
