# lithe

lithe; **lissome**; **lissom**; **\*lithesome**. *Lithe* = supple; flexible; limber. *Lissome* \(sometimes spelled *lissom*, especially in BrE\) is synonymous with *lithe*, except that *lissome* additionally suggests graceful movement <perhaps the most lissome ballet dancer in the troupe>. Although *\*lithesome* dates from the late 1700s and is illustrated in the *OED* with three examples from the 1800s, it remains a needless variant of *lithe*.

Current ratio in print \(*lithe* vs. *\*lithesome*\): 84:1
Current ratio in print \(*lissome* vs. *lissom*\): 1.2:1
