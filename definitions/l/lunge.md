# lunge

lunge \(= to thrust oneself or make a sudden forward movement\), for the present participle, makes *lunging*, not *\*lungeing*. So the inflected form is analogous to *cringing*, *fringing*, *hinging*, and *tinging*—not to *bingeing* and *whingeing*. \(See the entries for those other words.\)

 - Current ratio in print \(*lunging at* vs. *\*lungeing at*\): 519:1
