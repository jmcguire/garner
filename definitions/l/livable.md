# livable

livable (= \[1\] bearable, endurable; or \[2\] suitable to an agreeable arrangement in life), an adjective dating from the early 1600s, is predominantly so spelled in AmE and BrE alike. *\*Liveable* is a chiefly BrE variant. See spelling (d). Cf. **likable** & **lovable**.

 - Current ratio in print (*livable* vs. *\*liveable*): 3:1
