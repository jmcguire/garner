# likely

likely. Traditionally, this word had to be preceded by a modifier such as *very* or *quite* \<they will quite likely require additional supplies\>. Today, however, it is common in AmE to use *likely* as an equivalent of *probably* and not to insist on the modifier \<they will likely require additional supplies\>. See **apt (b)**.

## Language-Change Index

 - *likely* as an equivalent of *probably* (as in *We'll likely be on time*): Stage 5
