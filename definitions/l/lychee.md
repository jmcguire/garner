# lychee

lychee \(the small round fruit of a Chinese tree of the soapberry family\) is now predominantly so spelled in AmE and BrE alike—though the variant spelling *litchi* \(prevalent in AmE and BrE from 1930 to 1980\) remains popular in many parts of the English-speaking world. *\*Lichi* is another variant spelling—one that has fallen out of favor since the early 20th century.

Current ratio in print \(*lychee* vs. *litchi* vs. \**lichi* in AmE\): 69:39:1   
Current ratio in print \(*lychee* vs. *litchi* vs. \**lichi* in BrE\): 134:22:1   
Current ratio in print \(*lychee* vs. *litchi* vs. \**lichi* in World Englishes\): 30:28:1
