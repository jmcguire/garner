# lagniappe

lagniappe /**lan**-yap/ \(= something extra given gratuitously or through a show of generosity\), a Cajun Americanism dating from the mid-1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Lagnappe* is an infrequent variant.

Current ratio in print \(*lagniappe* vs. \**lagnappe*\): 200:1
