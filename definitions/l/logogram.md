# logogram

logogram; \***logograph**; **logogriph**. A *logogram* \(dating from 1840\) is a sign, letter, or symbol that represents an entire word \(e.g., $, &, @, and \#\). \**Logograph* \(dating from about the same time\) is a needless variant. A *logogriph* \(dating from the late 1500s\) is a word puzzle such as an anagram.
