# lovesick

lovesick \(= obsessed with an unrequited love\), a compound dating from the 1400s, has been predominantly so written, as solid, since about 1935 in AmE and 1973 in BrE. Before that, the hyphenated *love-sick* was standard. Cf. **loving-kindness**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*lovesick* vs. *love-sick* in World Englishes\): 4:1
