# legitimacy

legitimacy; **legitimation**; **\*legitimization**; **\*legitimatization**. *Legitimacy* = the fact of being legitimate. *Legitimation* = \(1\) the act or process of declaring someone or something legitimate; or \(2\) the act of making something lawful; official authorization. E.g.: "Both halves of the grand bargain outlined then—security and *legitimation* for Israel, land and power for Palestinians—are now in doubt." Barton Gellman, "Israel–Palestinian Peace Process ‘Hanging by a Thin Thread,' " *Pitt. Post-Gaz.*, 25 Mar. 1997, at A3. *\*Legitimization* and *\*legitimatization* have been needless variants since they first appeared in the 1800s.
