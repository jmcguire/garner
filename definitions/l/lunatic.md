# lunatic

lunatic does not have the respectable medical background of words such as *moron* and *idiot*, once-technical terms that were lost to popular usage and abandoned in medicine. Its past lurks more in superstitious folklore: derived from *Luna*, it originally denoted someone whose mad behavior was governed by the phases of the moon. Those connotations have faded, however, and today the term merely denotes an insane person. It is useful in that sense, and also as an adjective <lunatic fringe>. But since it's a term of opprobrium not sanctioned by the medical establishment <are you some kind of lunatic?>, it should be used cautiously, if at all.
