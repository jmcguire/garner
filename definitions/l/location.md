# location

location; **locale**; **locality**. These broadly equivalent terms are ordered from most concrete to most abstract. *Location* = \(1\) a particular place or spot in relation to others <a geographical location>; \(2\) the position of something <the precise location of the accident>; \(3\) a place where filming is done other than a studio <the movie was shot on location in Palo Duro Canyon>; or \(4\) the act or process of ascertaining or establishing something's position <the well-digger's principal difficulty was location of the aquifer>. *Locale* = the neighborhood or area where something happens or a story takes place <the locale for the short story>. *Locality* = a region, district, or other small area <crime reports in several different localities>.
