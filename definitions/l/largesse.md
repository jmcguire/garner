# largesse

largesse; **\*largess**. *Largesse* \(= generous giving; munificence\) has long been the standard spelling in English. The gallicism was first borrowed into Middle English and was fully anglicized as \**largess* by the 1500s. AmE mostly stuck with that spelling through about 1950, while BrE had reverted to the Frenchified spelling *largesse* by 1900. Today *largesse* predominates in AmE as well as BrE. Although the preferred pronunciation is the anglicized /lahr-**jes**/, the word is often pronounced in the French way, that is, /lahr-**zhes**/.

Current ratio in print \(*largesse* vs. \**largess*\): 3:1
