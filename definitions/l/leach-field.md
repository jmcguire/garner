# leach field

leach field \(= a network of underground perforated pipes laid in gravel-filled trenches to dissipate the effluvia from a septic tank or other water-based collection and treatment technology\), a phrase dating from the 1950s, is sometimes misleadingly misspelled \**leech field*—as if the field were teeming with blood-sucking worms.

Current ratio in print \(*leach field* vs. \**leech field*\): 26:1
