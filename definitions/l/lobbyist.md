# lobbyist

lobbyist \(= someone who seeks to influence public officials to take some desired action, esp. through legislation\), an agent noun dating from the 1860s, has been the standard term from the beginning. *\*Lobbyer* and *\*lobbier* are needless variants.

 - Current ratio in print \(*lobbyist* vs. *\*lobbyer* vs. *\*lobbier*\): 2,429:8:1
