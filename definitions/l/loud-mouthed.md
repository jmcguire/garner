# loud-mouthed

loud-mouthed \(= habitually noisy and offensive in speech\), a phrasal adjective dating from the early 1600s, is traditionally and today predominantly so written. *Loudmouthed* and the attributive *loudmouth* are variants. But *loudmouth* is the standard noun for someone who talks noisily and offensively.

Current ratio in print \(*loud-mouthed braggart* vs. *loudmouthed braggart* vs. *loudmouth braggart*\): 2:1.7:1
