# long-haired

long-haired; **longhair**, adj. The standard expressions are *long-haired breeds* \(of cats, dogs, etc.\) but *longhair music*. Hence *long-haired* means "having long hair," while *longhair* \(esp. in AmE\) means "typical of hippies." Surprisingly, the noun *longhairs* has denoted impractical bohemians or countercultural types since about 1920. Groovy.
