# \*undoubtably

\*undoubtably is an archaic nonword equivalent to the standard *undoubtedly* or *indubitably*. E.g.: "As quarterback Damian Poalucci begins his final campaign against New Haven Saturday, he's also starting a season where he will *undoubtably* \[read *undoubtedly*\] be the Warriors' most-watched player—by opponents and fans alike." Jeff Schuler, "Poalucci Has Gaudy Numbers to Live Up To," *Morning Call* \(Allentown, Pa.\), 12 Sept. 1997, at S9. See **\*doubtlessly**. Cf. **supposedly**.

## Language-Change Index

*\*undoubtably* for *undoubtedly*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*undoubtedly* vs. *\*undoubtably*\): 916:1
