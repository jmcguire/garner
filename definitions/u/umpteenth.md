# umpteenth

umpteenth is sometimes misspelled *\*umteenth*—e.g.: "A House subcommittee is at work on Virginia's *umteenth* \[read *umpteenth*\] study of campaign-finance reform." "Campaign-Finance Reform: Mandate Disclosure," *Virginian-Pilot & Ledger Star* (Norfolk), 8 Aug. 1996, at A18.

 - Current ratio in print (*umpteenth* vs. \**umteenth*): 535:1
