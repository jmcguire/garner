# ugly

ugly is sometimes used in the sense "ill-tempered, mean" \<Mike is being ugly again\>. The *OED* dates *ugly* in the sense "cross, angry" from the 1600s. *Ugly customer* (= a person who is difficult to deal with or likely to cause trouble) appears in Charles Dickens's *Martin Chuzzlewit* (1844) and several other 19th-century sources.
