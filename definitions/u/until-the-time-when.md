# \*until the time when

\*until the time when is verbose for *until*: "He renounced the whole of womankind *until the time when* \[read *until*\] he met the sweet little daughter of the innkeeper." Jaroslav Hasek, *The Red Commissar* 252 \(Cecil Parrott trans., 1981\). This prolixity has occurred especially in BrE since the late 1700s.
