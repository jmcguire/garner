# unbiased

unbiased (= impartial as a result of neither having a self-interest in the matter nor being subject to outside influences), an adjective dating from the 1600s, has been predominantly so spelled since about 1860 in AmE and about 1930 in BrE. The older spelling, \**unbiassed*, is a variant. See **bias**.

 - Current ratio in print (*unbiased* vs. \**unbiassed*): 31:1
