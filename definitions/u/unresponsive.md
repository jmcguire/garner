# unresponsive

unresponsive; **nonresponsive**; \***irresponsive**. The general, most prevalent term has been widely used since the late 1800s: *unresponsive* = not reacting to or affected by something. *Nonresponsive*, most frequently a legal term, means "\(of an answer to a question, esp. while under oath\) not directly answering the question asked." *\*Irresponsive* is a needless variant of the other two.
