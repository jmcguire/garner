# urban

urban; **urbane**. *Urban* = \(1\) of, relating to, or located in a city; or \(2\) characteristic of city life. *Urbane* = suave; sophisticated; debonair. Occasionally *urbane* is misused for *urban*—e.g.: "Looking at a computer as a miracle machine is akin to spouting the glories of dense *urbane* \[read *urban*\] living or fossil fuels." James Hague, "Maybe You Just Need a File Box and a Typewriter," *Countryside & Small Stock J.*, July 1995, at 37.
