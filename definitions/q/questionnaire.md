# questionnaire

questionnaire; \***questionary**. *Questionnaire*, the standard term, is occasionally misspelled *\*questionaire*—e.g.: "The *questionaires* \[read *questionnaires*\] ask several kinds of questions." Steven Amick, "Community's Views on Policing Needs Solicited," *Oregonian* (Portland), 16 Sept. 1997, at B2. Cf. **doctrinal** & **millionaire**.

*\*Questionary* is a needless variant.

## Language-Change Index

 - *questionnaire* misspelled *\*questionaire*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*questionnaire* vs. \**questionaire*): 1,202:1
