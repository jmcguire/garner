# quantum

quantum \(= amount; measure\) predominantly forms the plural *quanta*. \(See plurals \(b\).) The word can often be simplified—e.g.: "If necessary, he will enter into arrangements with financial institutions and stockbrokers to place out an appropriate *quantum* \[read *number*\] of PWE shares to ensure its continued listing." Jennifer Jacobs, "Ting Makes Offer for 6.56m PWE Shares at RM7.75 Each," *Bus. Times* \(Singapore\), 14 May 1996, at 6.
