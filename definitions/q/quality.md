# quality

quality. Note the different meanings this word bears in the phrases *quality control* and *quality relationship*. When used as an adjective meaning "of high quality," this is a vogue term and a casualism <a quality brewery> <a quality set of linens>. Use *good* or *fine* or some other adjective of better standing.

For a misuse of *quality*, n., for *quantity*, see **unknown quantity**.

## Language-Change Index

*quality* as an adjective meaning "of high quality": Stage 4
