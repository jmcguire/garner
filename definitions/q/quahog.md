# quahog

quahog /**koh**-hog/ or /**kwah**-hog/ \(the common round clam found along the Atlantic coast of North America\), a Narragansett loanword dating in English from the mid-1700s, is an AmE regionalism that has been predominantly so spelled since about 1940. Before that, \**quahaug* was standard.

Current ratio in print \(*quahog* vs. \**quahaug*\): 8:1
