# Quebecer

Quebecer; **Quebecker**. The traditional form of this demonym is *Quebecker*—the form still predominant in BrE. Since the early 1980s, the irregular spelling *Quebecer* has predominated in North American English. A French-speaking Quebecer is often referred to as a *Québécois* \(masculine\) or *Québécoise* \(feminine\), written with two accented *é'*s; some editorial styles recommend omitting the accents.

Current ratio in print \(*Quebecer* vs. *Quebecker* in AmE\): 1.7:1   
Current ratio in print \(*Quebecker* vs. *Quebecer* in BrE\): 1.3:1
