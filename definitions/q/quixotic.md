# quixotic

quixotic (= impractically based on wholly unrealistic dreams of improving the world) is pronounced /kwik-**sot**-ik/—not /kee-**hoh**-tik/. True, the adjective derives from the name of Cervantes's character Don Quixote /don kee-**oh**-tee/, but the adjective acquired the anglicized pronunciation as early as the 1700s.
