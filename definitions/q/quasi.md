# quasi

quasi —pronounced /**kway**-zī/ or \(less good\) /**kwah**-zee/—means "as if; seeming or seemingly; in the nature of; nearly." In legal writing, *quasi* may stand alone as a word, but as a prefix it's generally hyphenated. The term has been prefixed to any number of adjectives and nouns, e.g., *quasi-compulsory*, *quasi-domicile*, *quasi-judicial*, and *quasi-monopoly*.
