# curry

curry \(the spicy food with a pungent sauce\), a Tamil loanword dating in English from the late 1600s, was once spelled a variety of ways (**curree*, \**currey*, even \**carrie*\). But since 1800 its vastly predominant spelling has been *curry*. \**Currie* remains \(barely\) as the one live variant form: it has gone stale.

Current ratio in print \(*curry powder* vs. \**currie powder*\): 504:1
