# cantilever

cantilever \(= a long bracket or truss that extends from an upright post or wall to support a shelf, bridge, balcony, or the like\), a word of unknown origin dating in English from the mid-1600s, has been predominantly so spelled since the mid-1700s. \**Cantalever* and \**cantaliver* are variant forms.

Current ratio in print \(*cantilever* vs. \**cantalever* vs. \**cantaliver*\): 21,859:1.5:1
