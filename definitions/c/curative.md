# curative

curative; **\*curatory**; **curatorial**. For the meaning "of, relating to, or involving the cure of diseases," *curative* is preferred over *\*curatory*, which is a needless variant. *Curatorial* = of, relating to, or involving a curator. E.g.: "The entries by the museum *curatorial* staff are brief but intelligent." David Bonetti, "The Pick of the Art Books," *S.F. Examiner*, 10 Dec. 1996, at D1.
