# croquet

croquet; **croquette**. *Croquet* /kroh-**kay**/ \(dating in English from the 1850s\) denotes the lawn game in which players use wooden mallets to drive wooden balls through metal hoops in a designated order. *Croquette* /kroh-**ket**/ \(dating from the early 1700s\) is a cookery term denoting a fried sphere made up of rice, potato or other vegetable, mincemeat, egg, seasoning, and bread crumbs. Don't try croquet with croquettes.
