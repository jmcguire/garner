# consignor

consignor; **\*consigner**. *Consignor* is the standard correlative of *consignee*. A *consignor* dispatches goods to another on *consignment*. Perhaps because the very nature of this relationship is legal and technical, the *-or* spelling has been much more pervasive since the terms came into common use about 1800. The term has two distinct pronunciations: /kon-sī-**nor**/ and /kәn-**sī**-nor/. *\*Consigner* is a variant spelling. Cf. **consignee**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*consignor* vs. *\*consigner*\): 27:1
