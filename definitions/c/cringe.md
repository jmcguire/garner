# cringe

cringe (= \[1\] to flinch or shrink back; or \[2\] to wince or feel embarrassment) makes the present participle *cringing*. So the inflected form is analogous to *fringing*, *hinging*, *lunging*, and *tinging*—not to *bingeing* or *whingeing*. (See the entries for those other words.)

 - Current ratio in print (*cringing* vs. \**cringeing*): 505:1
