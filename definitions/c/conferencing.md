# conferencing

conferencing. Although the *OED* records *conference* as a \(rare\) verb from 1846, most 20th-century unabridged dictionaries omitted it. Increasingly common in AmE after 1970, *conferencing* is often a bloated needless variant of *conferring*. The word has also become rather widespread in the forms *teleconferencing* and *videoconferencing*, favorite activities in American business. The word is likely to survive in those forms.

Language-Change Index

1. *conference* as a verb: Stage 4   
Current ratio in print \(*conferring* vs. *conferencing*\): 2.5:1

2. *conference* in the compounds *teleconferencing* and *videoconferencing*: Stage 5
