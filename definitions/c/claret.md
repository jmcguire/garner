# claret

claret \(= a red wine from the Bordeaux region, or a similar wine from elsewhere\) is pronounced /**klar**-әt/. It's an English word that was borrowed from French and anglicized in Middle English. Some people today mistakenly give it a Frenchified pronunciation \(/kla-**ret**/ or, worse, /kla-**ray**/\). Cf. **Meritage**.
