# consul

consul; **counsel**; **council**. *Consul* /**kon**-sәl/ = a governmental representative living in a foreign country to oversee commercial matters. *Counsel* /**kown**-sәl/ = a legal adviser or group of legal advisers. (See **counsel** & **lawyer**.) *Council* /**kown**-sәl/ = a body of representatives. (See **council**.)
