# camellia

camellia /kә-**meel**-yә/ \(a plant with red, white, or pink roselike flowers\) has been predominantly so spelled since the term was first used in the mid-1700s. \(The flower is named after a Moravian Jesuit missionary named Georg Josef Kamel \[1661–1706\], a naturalist—but the name was Latinized, there being no *K* in Latin.\) \**Camelia* is a variant spelling that more immediately suggests the pronunciation—in fact, it may actually be a misspelling based on the pronunciation—but it has never become standard.

 - Current ratio in print \(*camellia* vs. \**camelia*\): 16:1
