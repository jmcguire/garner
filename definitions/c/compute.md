# compute

compute; \***computate**. The first is standard. The second is a back-formed needless variant—e.g.: "The OSRAP gives logisticians the ability to vary the parameters used in *computating* \[read *computing*\] requirements." John R. Millard, "Computing Requirements for a Changing Army," 33 *Army Logistician*, Mar. 2001, at 21. See back-formations.
