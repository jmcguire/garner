# catholically

catholically; \***catholicly**. Both may mean either "with wide sympathies" or (when capitalized) "in a manner inclined toward Catholicism." No differentiation has emerged, but *catholically* (pronounced /kә-**thol**-ik-lee/) is about six times as common as *\*catholicly*, which can now be branded a needless variant. That form, however, was predominant through the mid-1800s.
