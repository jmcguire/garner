# classy

classy; **class act**. These terms are distinctly *déclassé*: "I . . . instantly dismiss anyone who tells me that some other person ‘has class,' ‘is classy,' or ‘is a class act,' the last of these being the most arrogant. What these speakers are telling you is that since they are among the few people who recognize class, it is their obligation to point it out to sorry-ass folks like you." George Carlin, *Brain Droppings* 142 (1997).
