# capsize

capsize has always been so spelled. Yet the misspelling *\*capsise* occasionally appears in both BrE and World Englishes. E.g.: "Ships at sea are cracked like straws, curraghs and rafts in the lough *capsise* \[read *capsize*\]." Carolyn White, *A History of Irish Fairies* 92 \(2005\).

Current ratio in print \(*capsize* vs. \**capsise*\): 1,328:1
