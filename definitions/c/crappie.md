# crappie

crappie \(= a freshwater sunfish found in the central and eastern U.S.\) is the standard spelling. *\*Croppie*, a variant, is closer to a phonetic spelling. Both words are pronounced /**krop**-ee/, though some dictionaries list *crappie* as /**krap**-ee/.

 - Current ratio in print \(*crappie* vs. \**croppie*\): 231:1
