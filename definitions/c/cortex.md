# cortex

cortex \(= the outer layer of a bodily organ, esp. the brain\) has predominantly formed the plural *cortices* since the 1600s. *Cortexes* is a latecoming variant. In print today, *cortices* is strongly predominant. The corresponding adjective is *cortical* /**kor**-ti-kәl/.

 - Current ratio in print \(*cortices* vs. *cortexes*\): 24:1
