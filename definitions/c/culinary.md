# culinary

culinary is traditionally pronounced /**kyoo**-lә-ner-ee/in AmE, but primarily /**kәl**-ә-ner-ee/ in BrE. The latter gained popularity in AmE at least as early as the 1930s and started appearing in American dictionaries in the 1960s. "Since then it has proliferated in educated speech," writes Charles Harrington Elster, "no doubt given a huge boost by the late Julia Child," who used the latter pronunciation on her television show.
