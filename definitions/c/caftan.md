# caftan

caftan \(= a usu. lightweight, loose robe or tunic of Arabic origin\) has predominated over *kaftan* in both AmE and World Englishes since the 1920s. But in the last few years, *kaftan* and *caftan* have been used almost equally in BrE, *kaftan* now being more common.

 - Current ratio in print \(*caftan* vs. *kaftan* in World Englishes\): 1.2:1
