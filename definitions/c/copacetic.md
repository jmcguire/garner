# copacetic

copacetic; \***copasetic**; **\*copesetic**; **\*copesettic**. The first is the standard spelling for this tongue-in-cheek term meaning "first-rate; quite satisfactory." Dating from the early 1900s, the word is slangy and jocular. Its etymology—variously attributed to Italian, Creole French, and Hebrew—is unknown. The second, third, and fourth spellings are variants (the last one especially rare). See slang.
