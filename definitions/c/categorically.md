# categorically

categorically \(= unconditionally, absolutely\), dating from the early 1600s, conveys the idea of a category having no exceptions. E.g.: "Sells, a half-Serb, *categorically* condemns the behavior of the Serbian political, military and literary elite." Istvan Deak, "With God on Their Sides," *New Republic*, 25 Nov. 1996, at 31.

For a malapropism involving this word, see \***uncategorically**.
