# cumin

cumin; \***cummin**. *Cumin* (the parsley-related herb whose seeds are used as a spice esp. in Mexican and Indian cooking) became the predominant spelling in AmE about 1930 and in BrE a few years later. Pronounced /**kyoo**-min/, *cumin* has universally replaced the older spelling *\*cummin* (pronounced /**kәm**-in/).

 - Current ratio in print (*cumin* vs. \**cummin*): 30:1
