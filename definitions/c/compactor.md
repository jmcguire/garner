# compactor

compactor \(= a device that compresses things into small bundles\) has been predominantly so spelled only since the late 1940s, when the trash compactor was invented. Before that time, *compacter* \(meaning, archaically, "one who frames or puts together"\) was the predominant spelling. See -er \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*compactor* vs. *compacter*\): 13:1
