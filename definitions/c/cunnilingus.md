# cunnilingus

cunnilingus; **\*cunnilinctus**. The standard spelling of this Latin loanword, dating from the late 1800s (in the usual sense), is *cunnilingus* \(an agent-noun use \[pl. *cunnilingi*\] dates from the 1600s\). *\*Cunnilinctus*, a variant that appears in some dictionaries, rivaled the standard form in print during the 1930s, but it ebbed and is rarely used today. Cf. **anilingus**.

Current ratio in print \(*cunnilingus* vs. \**cunnilinctus*\): 60:1
