# combat

combat, in AmE, is traditionally pronounced /**kom**-bat/ as a noun, /kәm-**bat**/ as a verb. Although some authorities accept the inflected forms *combating* and *combated*, those spellings suggest the pronunciations /kәm-**bay**-ting/ and /kәm-**bay**-tәd/. *Combatting* and *combatted* are more in keeping with general principles of English word formation: the stress is on the /**bat**/ syllable before and after the suffix is added. \(See spelling \(c\).) Admittedly, however, the spellings *combated* and *combating* predominate in actual usage—doubtless coinciding with a tendency to stress the first syllable. See pronunciation \(c\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*combating* vs. *combatting*\): 5:1
