# coliseum

coliseum; **colosseum; Colosseum**. For the amphitheater of Vespasian in Rome, *Colosseum* is the proper name. For any other large building or assembly hall, the word is traditionally *coliseum* (AmE) or *colosseum* (BrE). But since 1970 or so, BrE has tracked AmE on this point, so that *coliseum* is preferred in both the primary varieties of English \<Memorial Coliseum\>.
