# Caribbean

Caribbean is sometimes misspelled *\*Caribean* or *\*Carribean*. The pronunciation /kar-i-**bee**-әn/ is preferred because of its derivation from *Carib* /**kar**-ib/, the name of the native inhabitants of the islands that Christopher Columbus landed on and explored in 1493 \(the Lesser Antilles\) and of the northern coast of South America. The pronunciation /kә-**rib**-ee-әn/ is common, especially in BrE, but not standard.
