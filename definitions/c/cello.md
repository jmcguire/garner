# cello

cello. This word is an 18th-century clipping of *violoncello*; until the late 20th century, it was often printed *'cello*. Today the form *cello*, without the apostrophe, is standard. See **violoncello**.

Since 1880, the standard english plural has been *cellos*, not *\*celli*.

Current ratio in print \(*cello* vs. *'cello*\): 14:1

Current ratio in print \(*cellos* vs. *\*celli*\): 10:1
