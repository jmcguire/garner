# calyx

calyx /**kay**-liks/ \(= the green whorl on a flower that protects it before it opens\) predominantly makes the plural *calyces* in all varieties of English. Although the anglicized plural *calyxes* enjoyed predominance in English-language sources from about 1790 to 1820, it has seriously receded since then.

Current ratio in print \(*calyces* vs. *calyxes*\): 5:1
