# contractual

contractual is sometimes erroneously written \(or pronounced\) *\*contractural*, with an intrusive *r*. E.g.: "KCTS refused to release the budget figures, citing *contractural* \[read *contractual*\] obligations." Chuck Taylor, "KCTS Confronts Its Future," *Seattle Times*, 17 Nov. 1996, at M1. For similar mistakes, see **contextual** & **pretextual**. See also **tortuous**.

Language-Change Index

*contractual* misspelled *\*contractural*: Stage 1   
Current ratio in print \(*contractual* vs. \**contractural*\): 819:1
