# choky

choky; **chokey**. Dating from the late 1500s, this word—meaning "tending to choke or become choked or choked up"—has been predominantly spelled *choky* in AmE since the 1820s. That was also the predominant spelling in BrE from about 1840 to the early 1930s; since then, *chokey* has been somewhat more common in BrE.

Current ratio in print \(*choky* vs. *chokey* in World Englishes\): 1.9:1
