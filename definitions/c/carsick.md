# carsick

carsick, adj. Although this adjective has been predominantly solid since about 1940, the corresponding noun remains predominantly *car sickness* (not *carsickness* or \**car-sickness*).

 - Current ratio in print (*get carsick* vs. \**get car sick* vs. \**get car-sick*): 28:10:1

 - Current ratio in print (*car sickness* vs. *carsickness* vs. \**car-sickness*): 11:8:1
