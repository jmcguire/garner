# crozier

crozier; \***crosier**. Although *crozier* (= a bishop's ornate staff resembling a shepherd's crook) has been the predominant spelling since the early 20th century across World Englishes, some American dictionaries have been slow to recognize its preeminence and continue listing the word under the variant spelling *\*crosier*.

 - Current ratio in print (*crozier* vs. \**crosier*): 1.4:1
