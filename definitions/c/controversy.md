# controversy

controversy is pronounced /**kon**-trә-vәr-see/ in AmE and BrE, but often also /kәn-**trov**-ә\[r\]-see/ in BrE. The word appears surprisingly often in the mangled form *\*controversary*. One example suffices: "It is only fitting that we delve into Irish history to relate a tale fraught with *controversary* \[read *controversy*\] for lo these past 144 years." Max Haines, "A Scream from the Past," *Toronto Sun*, 17 Mar. 1996, at 5.

## Language-Change Index

 - *controversy* misspelled *\*controversary*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*controversy* vs. \**controversary*): 17,809:1
