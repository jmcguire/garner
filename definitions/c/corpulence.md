# corpulence

corpulence; \***corpulency**. The predominant literary synonym for obesity was *\*corpulency* in the 1700s. But in the 1800s, *corpulence* gradually became the standard term in print—first in BrE \(about 1850\) and then in AmE \(about 1870\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*corpulence* vs. \**corpulency*\): 4:1
