# copywriter

copywriter; **copyrighter**. A *copywriter* is a person who writes copy, especially for advertisements and public relations. \(A copyeditor then corrects the copywriter's mistakes.\) A *copyrighter* is a person who obtains or owns the copyright to an expressive creation. In that correct sense, the word is a shorter but obscure variant of *copyright owner* or *copyright holder*. *Copyrighter* is sometimes misused for its homophone—e.g.: "About a dozen people turned out. One of them was Robert Montecalvo, a 63-year-old advertising *copyrighter* \[read *copywriter*\]." Richard Salit, "North Providence Republicans Looking to Get Back in Race," *Providence J.-Bull.*, 28 Feb. 2001, at C1.
