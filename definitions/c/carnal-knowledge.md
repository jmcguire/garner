# carnal knowledge

carnal knowledge. This is an old legal euphemism for sexual intercourse—dating back at least to the 1500s. The phrase is often paired, in references to rape, with *ravish*, a word that may strike some readers as romanticizing a horrible criminal act. (See **ravage**.) Except in quotations and historical references, the phrase *carnal knowledge* can be advantageously replaced by *sexual intercourse*.
