# chichi

chichi \(= \[1\] fashionable and expensive; or \[2\] excessively concerned with fashionable and expensive things\), an adjective dating from the 1920s, has been predominantly solid since about 1979 in AmE and 1985 across World Englishes. Before that, the hyphenated form *\*chi-chi* was prevalent.

Current ratio in print \(*chichi restaurants* vs. \**chi-chi restaurants*\): 4:1
