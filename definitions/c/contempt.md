# contempt

contempt; **contemptibility**; **contemptuousness**; **contumacy**. These words are quite distinct. *Contempt* = \(1\) \(generally\) the act or state of despising; the condition of being despised; \(2\) \(in law\) action interfering with the administration of justice. *Contemptibility* = the quality or fact of being worthy of scorn. *Contemptuousness* = the quality of being scornful or disdainful. *Contumacy* = willful contempt of court \(*\*contumacity* being a needless variant\). For more, see **contumacy**.
