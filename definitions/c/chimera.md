# chimera

chimera.

**A. Spelling and Pronunciation.** The form *chimera* is now standard, *\*chimaera* rarely appearing. The word is pronounced /kī-**meer**-ә/.

 - Current ratio in print \(*chimera* vs. \**chimaera*\): 14:1

**B. Plural.** The better plural is *chimeras*. The form *chimerae* is an affectation—e.g.: "Similarly grotesque creatures can be found in French furniture of the Francis I period \(1483–1547\), when *chimerae* \[read *chimeras*\], draped human figures, and stylized scrolls were popular motifs." Page Talbott, "Allen and Brother, Philadelphia Furniture Makers," *Mag. Antiques*, May 1996, at 716. See plurals \(b\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*chimeras* vs. *chimerae*\): 93:1
