# calculus

calculus \(= \[1\] a stony mass or deposit found in the body; \[2\] the part of mathematics dealing with changing quantities; or \[3\] a method of analyzing or calculating\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the mid-1600s, has always predominantly formed the Latinate plural *calculi*—not \**calculuses*.

 - Current ratio in print \(*calculi* vs. \**calculuses*\): 176:1
