# chamois

chamois. When referring to the leather, this word is pronounced /**sham**-ee/. \(It has the variant spellings *\*chammy*, *\*shammy*, *\*shamois*, and *\*shamoy*.\) But when referring to the goatlike antelope, the word may be pronounced either that way or /sham-**wah**/. Even so, the antelope is famously indifferent to the pronunciation used.

Current ratio in print \(*chamois* vs. \**shammy* vs. \**shamoy* vs. \**chammy* vs. \**shamois*\): 739:16:3:3:1
