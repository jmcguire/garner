# coupon

coupon is best pronounced /**koo**-pon/, not /**kyoo**-pon/. The second (chiefly U.S.) pronunciation is sufficiently enduring and widespread that it has been condemned in local newspaper columns since at least 1906—on grounds that it betrays an ignorance of French and of the finer points of English. Imagine *coup d'etat* pronounced with /kyoo/ as the first syllable. See pronunciation (b).
