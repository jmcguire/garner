# circumscribable

circumscribable; *\****circumscriptible**; *\****circumscriptable**. *Circumscribable* \(= \[1\] capable of being confined within bounds; or \[2\] \(of a page, photograph, etc.\) amenable to have a border\) is standard. Until about 1950, the standard term was *\*circumscriptible*—but the reversal in word frequency has been so pronounced since then that the older form has become a needless variant. See -able \(a\).

Current ratio in print \(*circumscribable* vs. *\*circumscriptible*\): 23:1
