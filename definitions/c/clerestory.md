# clerestory

clerestory /**kleer**-stor-ee/ or /-stә-ree/ \(= the upper part of a multistory wall containing windows, light, or ventilation\) a term dating from the 1400s, has always been predominantly so spelled in Modern English. \**Clerestorey*, \**clearstory*, and \**clearstorey* are variant forms.

 - Current ratio in print \(*clerestory* vs. \**clearstory* vs. \**clerestorey* vs. \**clearstorey*\): 161:4:1.4:1
