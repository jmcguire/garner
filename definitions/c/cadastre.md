# cadastre

cadastre /kә-**das**-tәr/ \(= a tax record or register of the ownership and value of the tracts of land within a country or one of its political subdivisions\), a French loanword dating in English from the late 1700s, has almost always been predominantly so spelled. **Cadaster* is a chiefly AmE variant. See gallicisms.

Current ratio in print \(*cadastre* vs. \**cadaster*\): 4:1
