# celesta

celesta /sә-**lәs**-tә/ \(= a percussion instrument consisting of a small keyboard connected to hammers that strike metal plates to produce bell-like sounds\), a loanword dating in English from the late 1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled. **Celeste*, modeled on the actual French etymon, is considered a variant spelling in English. That's curious: the *OED* notes that *celesta* is a pseudo-Latinization of the French term.

Current ratio in print \(*The celesta* vs. \**The celeste*\): 2.2:1
