# conservative

conservative is surprisingly often, through a kind of visual metathesis, printed *\*conversative*—e.g.: "The Hoover Institution, a *conversative* \[read *conservative*\] California-based think tank at Stanford University, also found that the hijackers were eligible to vote in state and federal elections." Katrice Franklin, "Immigrants Trapped by DMV Requirements," *Virginian-Pilot & Ledger Star* \(Norfolk\), 23 July 2002, at B1. For the meaning of *conservative*, see **conservational**.
