# chuckwalla

chuckwalla (= a heavy, squat iguana that lives in the southwestern U.S. and in northwest Mexico), a term dating in English from the late 1800s and deriving from the Mexican Spanish term *chacahuala*, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Chuckawalla* is a variant form.

 - Current ratio in print (*chuckwalla* vs. \**chuckawalla*): 32:1
