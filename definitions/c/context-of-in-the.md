# context of, in the

context of, in the; **in a . . . context**. These phrases are often used superfluously. "During the seventh century BC, Egypt was repeatedly though always briefly occupied by Assyrian armies and *later* *infiltrated by Greek and other Aegean elements in a military and subsequently a commercial context* \[read *later infiltrated militarily and then commercially by Greek and other Aegean elements*\]."
