# callous

callous; **callus**. The first is the adjective ("hardened, unfeeling"), the second the noun ("hardened skin"). Unfortunately, during the early 1990s Dr. Scholl's—the company specializing in foot products—mistakenly advertised *\*callous removers* instead of *callus removers*, encouraging further confusion.

The plural of *callus* is *calluses*, preferably not *\*calli*—which, surprisingly enough, predominated until the mid-20th century. See plurals (b).

 - Current ratio in print (*calluses* vs. *\*calli*): 4:1
