# cellaret

cellaret \(= a case or small cabinet that holds bottles, decanters, glasses, etc.\), a noun dating from the early 1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Cellarette* is a variant spelling that vied closely for predominance from about 1910 to 2000. See diminutives \(f\).

Current ratio in print \(*cellaret* vs. \**cellarette*\): 3:1
