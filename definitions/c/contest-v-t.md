# contest, v.t

contest, v.t.

**A. And *contend*.** In the sense "to fight," *contest* is almost always transitive <to contest an election>, and *contend* is intransitive <to contend against an opponent>. *Contend* may be transitive when it means "to maintain, assert" and is followed by a *that*-phrase <the striking workers contend that the pension fund is inadequate>.

**B. Pronunciation.** The noun is pronounced /**kon**-test/; the verb is pronounced /kәn-**test**/.
