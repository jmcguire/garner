# cognitive dissonance

cognitive dissonance \(= internal conflict resulting from a belief in or commitment to contradictory ideas\), a phrase introduced in the 1950s by the psychologist Leon Festinger \(1919–1989\), is sometimes mistakenly rendered as a mondegreen: \**cognitive dissidence* \(an error dating from the 1970s\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*cognitive dissonance* vs. \**cognitive dissidence*\): 722:1
