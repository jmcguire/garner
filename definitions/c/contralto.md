# contralto

contralto. Pl. *contraltos*, preferably not *\*contralti* (an affectation). From 1850 (when the term first became fairly common) to the present day, *contraltos* has seriously outranged the Italianate plural in frequency of use. See plurals (b).

 - Current ratio in print (*contraltos* vs. \**contralti*): 13:1
