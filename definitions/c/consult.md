# consult

consult, as an intransitive verb, takes the preposition *with* (another person), or *on* or *about* (a matter). The verb may also be used transitively <to consult the document itself>. As with *invite* (for *invitation*), avoid the casual use of the verb to mean "consultation."

For a brief mention of *consult* as a noun in place of *consultation*, see **disconnect**. Cf. **invite**.
