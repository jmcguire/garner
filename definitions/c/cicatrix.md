# cicatrix

cicatrix (= a scar in tissue where a wound has healed) makes the plural *cicatrices*—not *\*cicatrixes*. The back-formation of the variant plural—the supposed singular *\*cicatrice*—is to be avoided in AmE and BrE alike.

 - Current ratio in print (*cicatrices* vs. *\*cicatrixes*): 50:1
