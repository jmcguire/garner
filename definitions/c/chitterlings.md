# chitterlings

chitterlings \(= cooked pig intestines\) is the standard term. Despite its spelling, it's pronounced /**chit**-linz/. As an almost inevitable result, the variant spellings *\*chitlings* and *\*chitlins* have emerged. Cf. **victuals**.

Current ratio in print \(*chitterlings* vs. *\*chitlins* vs. *\*chitlings*\): 20:9:1
