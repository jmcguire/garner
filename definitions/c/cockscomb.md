# cockscomb

cockscomb; **coxcomb**. A *cockscomb* is the fleshy red growth on a rooster's head. A *coxcomb* is either a hat resembling a cockscomb \(formerly worn by jesters\) or a foolishly conceited dandy or fop. Both terms are pronounced /**koks**-kohm/. The figurative *coxcomb* has always been the more frequent term in Modern English. Its corresponding noun, *coxcombry*, means "foppery."
