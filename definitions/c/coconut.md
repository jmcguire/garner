# coconut

coconut is sometimes spelled *\*cocoanut*, which was actually the standard spelling up to about 1920 (under the confusing influence of *cocoa*/*cacao*)—e.g.: "The bar offers . . . appetizers, salads, sandwiches and pizza, including spicy *cocoanut* \[read *coconut*\] calamari and sashimi." Susan F. Yim, "Maui, Hawaii," *Star Trib.* (Minneapolis), 16 Feb. 1997, at G8. Unless you're writing a historical novel, stick to *coconut*.

 - Current ratio in print (*coconut* vs. \**cocoanut*): 29:1
