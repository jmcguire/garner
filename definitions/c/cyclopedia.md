# \*cyclopedia

\*cyclopedia; \***cyclopedic**. During the 20th century, these forms were superseded by *encyclopedia* and *encyclopedic*. The words *\*cyclopedia* and *\*cyclopedic* are now needless variants.

In BrE the tendency has been to spell all forms of these words *-paed-* or *-pæd-*, but today the *-ped-* spellings are very much on the rise because of the AmE influence. See ae.
