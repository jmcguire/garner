# consortium

consortium is pronounced /kәn-**sor**-sh\[ee\]-әm/ and now also, in BrE occasionally, /kәn-**sor**-tee-әm/. The plural *consortia* has been standard since the 1700s. The plural *consortiums* didn't come into common use until the late 1900s, and it is still far less frequent than *consortia*. See plurals \(b\).

Current ratio in print \(*consortia* vs. *consortiums*\): 7:1
