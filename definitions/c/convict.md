# convict

convict, v.t. A person is *convicted of* a crime, *for* the act of committing a crime, or *on* a particular count. But a person is not *\*convicted in a crime*: "A Palestinian suspected in the bombing of Pan Am Flight 103 was *convicted* today along with three co-defendants *in* \[read *for*\] a series of attacks in northern Europe four years ago." "Pan Am Bombing Suspect Convicted *in* \[read *for*\] Other Attacks," *N.Y. Times*, 22 Dec. 1989, at 3.
