# codex

codex. Although the more easily understandable plural of this noun—meaning "a bound sheaf of manuscript leaves, esp. of an ancient or classic work or of the Scriptures"—might be *codexes*, the form *codices* is standard: it greatly predominates in print. See plurals \(b\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*codices* vs. *codexes*\): 61:1
