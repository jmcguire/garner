# calcimine

calcimine /**kal**-si-mīn/ (= a tinted wash used esp. on walls and ceilings, consisting of a mixture of whiting or zinc white with glue and water) has been predominantly so spelled since the 1920s. The most prominent variant spellings are \**kalsomine*, the original spelling that dates from the mid-1800s, and \**calsomine*.

 - Current ratio in print (*calcimine* vs. \**kalsomine* vs. \**calsomine*): 27:13:1
