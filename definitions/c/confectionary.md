# confectionary

confectionary; **confectionery**. Despite some overlap, these terms are usefully distinguished from each other. *Confectionary*, normally an adjective, means (1) "of, relating to, or resembling a confection"; or (2) "of, relating to, or involving confectioners." *Confectionery*, always a noun, means (1) "confections collectively; candy"; or (2) "a confectioner's business or place of business; candy store." The latter is the more commonly used term. For a similar differentiation with *stationary*/*stationery*, see **stationary**.
