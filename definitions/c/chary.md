# chary

chary \(= cautious\) is a formal word close in meaning to *wary* \(and rhyming with it\)—e.g.: "Indonesia, once *chary* of foreign investment, is now worried about losing out to China, Vietnam and other countries." Andrew Pollack, "Companies Rediscovering Indonesia," *N.Y. Times*, 6 Dec. 1994, at D1. The word sometimes implies "sparing, ungenerous" <chary of praise>.
