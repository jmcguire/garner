# clitoridectomy

clitoridectomy; \***clitorectomy**. *Clitoridectomy* is the traditional and usual spelling of the term denoting the surgical removal of part or all of the clitoris. It appears much more often than *\*clitorectomy*, which was virtually unknown before the mid-20th century.

 - Current ratio in print (*clitoridectomy* vs. *\*clitorectomy*): 10:1
