# constitutionalist

constitutionalist; **\*constitutionist**. The standard form of the term is *constitutionalist* \(= \[1\] someone who studies or writes on the Constitution; or \[2\] a supporter of constitutional principles\). The form *\*constitutionist* is a needless variant. E.g.: "*Constitutionists* \[read *Constitutionalists*\] could manage to gain a strong enough foothold in Congress to back a president who would use his legitimate veto power to curtail spending." "Perils of the Line Item Veto," *Wash. Times*, 29 Sept. 1996, at B5.
