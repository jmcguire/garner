# cockamamie

cockamamie \(= outlandishly silly or ridiculous\), an Americanism dating from the late 1950s, originated as a slangy alteration of *decalcomania*—which denotes the transfer of decals from paper onto a surface such as glass or wood. \**Cockamamy* is a variant spelling.

Current ratio in print \(*cockamamie* vs. \**cockamamy*\): 8:1
