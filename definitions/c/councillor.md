# councillor

councillor; **counselor**. A *councillor* is a member of a council. \(*\*Councilor* is a variant spelling.\) A *counselor* is either a lawyer or someone else who gives advice. \(In BrE, it's spelled *counsellor*.\) See **lawyer**. Cf. **counselor**.

Current ratio in print \(*councillor* vs. *\*councilor*\): 3:1
