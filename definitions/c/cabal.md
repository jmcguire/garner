# cabal

cabal /kә-**bahl**/ or /kә-**bal**/ (= \[1\] the collective intrigues and machinations of a small group of plotters; or \[2\] a small group of people secretly united for some purpose) has been a standard spelling since the 1600s. Among the variant spellings are \**kabal*, \**cabbal*, and \**kabbal*. The term is related to *kabbalah*. See **kabbalah**.

 - Current ratio in print (*cabal* vs. \**kabal* vs. \**kabbal* vs. \**cabbal*): 7,961:28:5:1
