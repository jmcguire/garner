# cactus

cactus. Pl. *cacti* or *cactuses*. The latter is common in ordinary usage—e.g.: "Masters stands in the desert, flanked by *cactuses*." Jason Zengerle, "Talk Shop," *N.Y. Times Mag.*, 27 Mar. 2022, at 7, 8. But *cacti* predominates in botanical contexts. See plurals \(b\).

Current ratio in print \(*cacti* vs. *cactuses*\): 6:1
