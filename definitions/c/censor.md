# censor

censor, n.; **censer; sensor**. The first of these homophones is someone who suppresses; the second is either the vessel in which incense is burned or the person who carries that vessel; the third is something that detects. To compare the nouns *censor* and *censure*, see **censor**; **censure \(b\)**.

Language-Change Index

*censor* misused for *censer*: Stage 1 Current ratio in print \(*golden censer* vs. \**golden censor*\): 67:1
