# Celt

Celt; **Celtic; \*Kelt; \*Keltic**. The Boston *Celtics* will always be /**sel**-tiks/. Scotland's *Celtic* football team is most emphatically the /**sel**-tiks/, too. But increasingly the early Britons (*Celts*) are called /kelts/, and the things relating to them are *Celtic* /**kel**-tik/. Historians generally prefer the /k/ sound. *\*Kelt* and *\*Keltic* are variant spellings.

 - Current ratio in print (*Celts* vs. *\*Kelts*): 25:1

 - Current ratio in print (*Celtic* vs. *\*Keltic*): 85:1
