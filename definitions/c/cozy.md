# cozy

cozy (= warm and comfortable) is the standard spelling in AmE, *cosy* in BrE. The same distinction holds for the noun (*tea cozy* in AmE, *tea cosy* in BrE). *\*Cosey*, *\*cozie, \*cosie,* and *\*cozey* are variants.

 - Current ratio in print (*cozy* vs. *cosy* vs. \**cosey* vs. \**cozie* vs. \**cosie* vs. \**cozey*): 9,057:5,134:151:6:1.3:1
