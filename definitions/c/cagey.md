# cagey

cagey \(= sly, cunning\) is the standard spelling. *\*Cagy* is a variant. In an anomaly of English spelling, however, the spelling of the corresponding noun has always been *caginess*, not *\*cageyness*. Cf. **stagy**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*cagey* vs. *\*cagy*\): 20:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*caginess* vs. *\*cageyness*\): 19:1
