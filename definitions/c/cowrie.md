# cowrie

cowrie \(= \[1\] a gastropod that inhabits warm seas and produces a brightly colored shell; or \[2\] the shell of this creature, esp. as once used as a medium of exchange in parts of Africa and Asia\) has been predominantly so spelled since the 1920s. *\*Cowry* is a variant spelling.

 - Current ratio in print \(*cowrie* vs. *\*cowry*\): 3:1
