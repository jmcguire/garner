# cede

cede; **secede; concede**. *Cede* = to give up, grant, admit, or surrender <by treaty, the tribe ceded about 20 million acres of aboriginal land>. *Secede* = to withdraw formally from membership or participation in <South Carolina was the first to secede from the U.S.>. *Concede* = (1) to admit to be true <I concede your point>; (2) to grant (as a right or a privilege) <in settlement, the landowner conceded a right-of-way to his neighbor>; or (3) to admit defeat (as in an election) <Dole conceded to Clinton in a gracious, moving way>.
