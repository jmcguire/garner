# cerebral

cerebral. In AmE and BrE alike, this word is pronounced /sә-**ree**-brәl/ in all contexts except one—the phrase *cerebral palsy*, which is most often pronounced /**ser**-ә-brәl **pahl**-zee/. This exception derives from an old pronunciation of *cerebral*: in the early 20th century, the preference was for the first syllable to be accented.
