# cabalist

cabalist**; kabbalist**. Although there is undoubtedly overlap, these terms have undergone serious differentiation. A *cabalist* is someone who is part of a cabal (secret society). (See **cabal**.) A *kabbalist* is someone who professes or adheres to some form of *kabbalah*—that is, an esoteric religious doctrine. (See **kabbalah**.) \**Cabbalist* is a variant spelling of both terms.
