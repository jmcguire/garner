# chili

chili \(= \[1\] a hot pepper; or \[2\] a spicy beef stew that, when prepared north of Texas's Red River, often incorrectly contains beans\) is so spelled in AmE—*chilli* in BrE. \(The normativity of this book extends beyond language to chili.\) Except in sense 1, avoid the Spanish spelling *chile*.

 - Current ratio in print \(*chili peppers* vs. *chile peppers*\): 3:1

 - Current ratio in print \(*hot chiles* vs. *hot chilis*\): 8:1
