# commute

commute.

**A. And *commutate*.** *Commute* = (1) to travel back and forth regularly, esp. over a significant distance and esp. to one's job; (2) to replace (a punishment or penalty) with one of less severity; or (3) to change (one kind of payment) into or for another. *Commutate* is a technical term meaning "to regulate the direction of (an electric current)."

**B. And *pardon*.** In sense 2, to *commute* a punishment or penalty is to reduce it, or to substitute in its place a milder punishment or penalty. To *pardon* someone who has been convicted or punished is to excuse the person without exacting any further penalty.
