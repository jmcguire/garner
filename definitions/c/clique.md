# clique

clique \(= a small group of people who keep to themselves and treat others as outsiders\) is pronounced either /kleek/ or /klik/ \(the former being the preference of most cultivated speakers\). The corresponding adjective is *cliquish* \(/**kleek**-ish/ or /**klik**-ish/\), meaning "snobbishly confining one's interests to a small in-group." E.g.: "Jews and homosexuals appear in the hater's mind as small, *cliquish* and very powerful groups, antipathetic to majority values." Andrew Sullivan, "When Plagues End," *N.Y. Times*, 10 Nov. 1996, § 6, at 52. *\*Cliquey* \(sometimes also spelled *\*cliquy*\) is a needless variant.

Current ratio in print \(*cliquish* vs. *\*cliquey* vs. *\*cliquy*\): 18:16:1
