# clematis

clematis /**klem-**ә**-**tis/ \(= a flowering plant that attaches itself to trees, buildings, or fences as it grows\) functions as both singular and plural—*\*clematises* and *\*clemati* being rare variants, the latter of which is downright wrong.

Current ratio in print \(*clematis are* vs. *\*clematises are*\): 12:1
