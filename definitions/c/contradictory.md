# contradictory

contradictory; \***contradictive**; **\*contradictional**; **contradictious**. *Contradictory* = opposite, contrary. *\*Contradictive* and *\*contradictional* are needless variants of *contradictory*.

*Contradictious* = inclined to contradict or quarrel. The word is applied to people—e.g.: "But General Gordon had always been a *contradictious* person—even a little off his head, perhaps, though a hero; and besides he was no longer there to contradict." Carol Brightman, "Character in Biography," *Nation*, 13 Feb. 1995, at 206.
