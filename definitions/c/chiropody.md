# chiropody

chiropody; **chiropodist**. The first syllable of these words is preferably pronounced /ki/, not /chi/. But /shi/ is common and \(barely\) acceptable. The words are labeled "factitious" by the *OED*; are now less common than their synonyms, *podiatry* and *podiatrist*; and are becoming less and less common. But not until 1970 did *podiatrist* overtake *chiropodist* in frequency of use in English-language sources; the latter predominated from 1800 to 1970.

 - Current ratio in print \(*podiatrist* vs. *chiropodist*\): 4:1
