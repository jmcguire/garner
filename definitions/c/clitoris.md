# clitoris

clitoris is traditionally and preferably pronounced /**klit**-ә-ris/, though /kli-**tor**-is/ is listed as an alternative pronunciation in most current dictionaries. The corresponding adjective is *clitoral* /**klit**-ә-rәl/—preferably not *\*clitoric*.

 - Current ratio in print (*clitoral* vs. *\*clitoric*): 139:1
