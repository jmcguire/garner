# cupola

cupola (= a small round structure on the top of a building, usu. shaped like an upside-down bowl) is pronounced /**kyoo**-pә-lә/—not /**kyoo**-pә-loh/ or /**koo**-pә-lә/. It has universally formed the plural *cupolas* since the 1700s—not \**cupolae*. The inflected form is *cupolaed* <a cupolaed building>. For a mistake sometimes made with this word, see **copula**.
