# granary

granary (= a place where grain is stored, esp. wheat) is most traditionally pronounced /**gran**-ә-ree/, though in AmE /**gray**-nә-ree/ is also acceptable. The spellings *\*grainary* and *\*grainery* are obsolete variants.

 - Current ratio in print (*granary* vs. *\*grainery* vs*. \*grainary*): 1,047:5:1
