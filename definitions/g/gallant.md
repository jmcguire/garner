# gallant

gallant. In all senses as an adjective, this word is best pronounced /**gal**-әnt/. (In the dated sense "polite and attentive to women," it may also be pronounced /gә-**lahnt**/ or /gә-**lant**/.) As a noun, however, the word is pronounced either /gә-**lahnt**/ or /gә-**lant**/. Today the noun is considered literary.
