# gelatin

gelatin \(= \[1\] a clear, jellylike substance made by boiling animal tissues; or \[2\] any similar-looking substance\), a French loanword dating in English from the late 1700s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since the 1890s and in BrE since the 1920s. The earlier spelling, now a primarily BrE variant, is **gelatine*. See gallicisms.

Current ratio in print \(*gelatin* vs. \**gelatine*\): 5:1
