# genome

genome /**jee**-nohm/ (= the genetic material of an organism), a modern noun dating from the 1930s, is so spelled. It is occasionally misspelled *gnome* /nohm/, especially in the proper noun *Genome Project* (mistakenly \**Gnome Project*). A *gnome* is a fictitious creature in children's stories—typically a little old man who wears a pointed hat, lives underground, and guards gold, jewels, and other valuables.

 - Current ratio in print (*Genome Project* vs. \**Gnome Project*): 1,366:1
