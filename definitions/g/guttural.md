# guttural

guttural. So spelled—not *\*gutteral*, which is a frequent misspelling. The confusion probably occurs because of a mistaken association with *gutter*, when in fact *guttural* derives from the Latin *guttur* \("throat"\). E.g.: "I find myself gripping the handle and uttering a *gutteral* \[read *guttural*\] ‘vroom\! vroom\!' " Ron Alexander, "Metropolitan Diary," *N.Y. Times*, 30 Oct. 1996, at C2. See **gut reaction**.

## Language-Change Index

*guttural* misspelled *\*gutteral*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*guttural* vs. \**gutteral*\): 86:1
