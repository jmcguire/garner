# grievous

grievous /**gree**-vәs/ is frequently misspelled *\*grievious* and mispronounced /**gree**-vee-әs/, just as *mischievous* is frequently misspelled *\*mischievious* and similarly mispronounced. These are grievous and mischievous malformations. See **mischievous**.

Current ratio in print \(*grievous* vs. *\*grievious*\): 667:1
