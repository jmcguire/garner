# godforsaken

godforsaken \(= \[of a place\] far away from urban civilization and devoid of interest, appeal, and cheer\), a compound dating from the mid-1800s, has been predominantly so written—as a lowercase word and therefore presumably with a metaphorical meaning—since about 1975. Before that, the more literal-looking \**God-forsaken* predominated. Other variants are \**Godforsaken* and \**god-forsaken*.

Current ratio in print \(*godforsaken* vs. \**God-forsaken* vs. \**Godforsaken* vs. \**god-forsaken*\): 11:3:1.8:1
