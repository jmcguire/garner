# goiter

goiter \(= a permanent and morbid swelling of the thyroid gland\), a French loanword dating in English from the 1600s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since about 1910. Before that, the AmE spelling was *goitre*—which has always been the standard BrE spelling.

 - Current ratio in print \(*goiter* vs. *goitre* in AmE\): 6:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*goitre* vs. *goiter* in BrE\): 2.1:1
