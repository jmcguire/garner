# gonorrhea

gonorrhea \(the sexually transmitted disease\), a Latin/Greek loanword dating in English from the early 1500s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since about 1900. The standard BrE spelling has always been *gonorrhoea*—though the AmE spelling has made inroads into BrE since about 1970.

Current ratio in print \(*gonorrhea* vs. *gonorrhoea* in AmE\): 13:1   
Current ratio in print \(*gonorrhoea* vs. *gonorrhea* in BrE\): 1.7:1
