# ganglion

ganglion \(= \[1\] an encapsulated group of nerve cells external to the brain and spinal cord; or \[2\] a cystic tumor on a tendon sheath\) has predominantly formed the plural *ganglia* since the early 1800s—not *\*ganglions*.

 - Current ratio in print \(*ganglia* vs. \**ganglions*\): 67:1
