# generally

generally has three basic meanings: \(1\) "disregarding insignificant exceptions" <the quality of the acting is generally very high>; \(2\) "in many ways" <he was the most generally qualified applicant>; and \(3\) "usually" <he generally leaves the office at five o'clock>. Sense 3 is the least effective in formal writing, although at times it merges with sense 1.
