# guerrilla

guerrilla (Sp. for "raiding party") = a member of a small band of military fighters who, mostly through surprise raids, try to harass and undermine occupying forces. The word has been predominantly so spelled since 1935—not *\*guerilla* (the standard spelling from the early 1800s till 1935). The word is often used attributively \<guerrilla warfare\>.

The malapropism *\*gorilla forces* occasionally surfaces.

 - Current ratio in print (*guerrilla* vs *\*guerilla*): 5:1
 - Current ratio in print (*guerrilla warfare* vs. *\*gorilla warfare*): 367:1
