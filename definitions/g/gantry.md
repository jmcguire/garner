# gantry

gantry \(= a large metal frame that supports heavy machinery or railroad signals\), a noun of uncertain origin dating from the 1400s, has been the established spelling since the late 1700s. *\*Gauntry* and *\*gauntree* are variant forms.

 - Current ratio in print \(*gantry* vs. *\*gauntry* vs. *\*gauntree*\): 24,639:2.5:1
