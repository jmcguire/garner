# gully

gully \(= a small ravine\) is sometimes misspelled *\*gulley*—perhaps because of the similarity to *valley*. The correct plural is *gullies*. E.g.: "Its six small fields, severed by roads, *gulleys* \[read *gullies*\] and a river, made it awkward for modern machinery, and much of the land was unproductive." Larry Towell, "The World from My Front Porch," *Life*, Sept. 1997, at 62.

Language-Change Index

*gully* misspelled *\*gulley*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*gully* vs. \**gulley*\): 11:1
