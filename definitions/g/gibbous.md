# gibbous

gibbous (= \[1\] protuberant, convex; or \[2\] having or resembling a hump; humpbacked) is most traditionally pronounced /**gib**-әs/, but today the standard pronunciation is /**jib**-әs/. The traditional pronunciation still holds, though, for phases of the moon: *waxing gibbous* from first quarter to full moon and *waning gibbous* from full to third quarter.
