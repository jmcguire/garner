# gendarmerie

gendarmerie /zhan-dahr-mә-**ree**/ (= a body of French police officers, esp. of a military nature), a French loanword dating in English from the 1500s, has long been predominantly so spelled. (An individual soldier in the armed police force of France is known as a *gendarme.*) \**Gendarmery* is a variant spelling. \**Gendarmarie* is an outright misspelling. See gallicisms.

 - Current ratio in print (*gendarmerie* vs. \**gendarmery* vs. \**gendarmarie*): 417:4:1
