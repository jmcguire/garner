# guess what

guess what. This is normally an imperative phrase, not an interrogative one. So it shouldn't end with a question mark. Hence the title of Mervin Bishop's *Guess What\!* (1995) is preferable to the title of Mem Fox's *Guess What?* (1990), Beau Gardner's *Guess What?* (1985), and Taro Gomi's *Guess What?* (1992).
