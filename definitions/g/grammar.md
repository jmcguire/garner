# grammar

grammar is often misspelled *\*grammer*—e.g.: "Writing Center tutors go through extensive training before being assigned to students. They complete notebooks on how to tutor and meet weekly to role-play, hear guest lecturers, complete *grammer* \[read *grammar*\] exercises and look at their own writing." "Center Has the Write Stuff for Students," *Boston Herald*, 11 Aug. 2002, Mag. §, at M2. All of which raises the question: Which comes first, spelling or *grammar* \(properly spelled\)? See spelling \(a\).
