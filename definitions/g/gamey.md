# gamey

gamey (= \[1\] having the strong taste of a wild animal that is hunted for food, esp. one that has slightly decomposed; or \[2\] sordid, scandalous, or salacious), an adjective dating from the early 1800s, was long spelled \**gamy* in AmE. That spelling also predominated in BrE until the 1860s, when the spelling *gamey* came to be a close rival for many decades—until the early 1980s, when *gamey* became predominant. AmE followed suit about 2010.

 - Current ratio in print (*gamey* vs. \**gamy* in AmE): 1.3:1
 - Current ratio in print (*gamey* vs. \**gamy* in BrE): 4:1
