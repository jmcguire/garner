# grudging

grudging (= reluctant; with grumbling) is the standard adjective in phrases such as *grudging acceptance*. Avoid the participial adjective *\*begrudging*—e.g.: "I have heard even some die-hard Hillary-haters express *begrudging* \[read *grudging*\] admiration for her determination, resourcefulness and fierce advocacy for her beliefs." Clarence Page, "Obama's Next Hurdles," *Chicago Trib.*, 14 May 2008, News §, at 27.

## Language-Change Index

 - *\*begrudging* for *grudging*: Stage 2
 - Current ratio in print (*a grudging respect* vs. *\*a begrudging respect*): 11:1
