# goosey

goosey \(= \[1\] of, relating to, or resembling a goose; \[2\] foolish; or \[3\] hypersensitive to being prodded or startled\) is the standard spelling. *\*Goosy* is a variant. *Loosey-goosey* is an AmE casualism meaning "relaxed" (as in "loose as a goose") or, in describing machinery, "rattletrap."

Current ratio in print \(*goosey* vs. *\*goosy*\): 10:1
