# hecto-/centi-

hecto-/centi-. *Hecto-* derives from the Greek *hekaton*, *centi-* from the Latin *centum* (both meaning "one hundred"). Whereas the root *hecto-* means "multiplied by 100," *centi-* means "divided by 100." Hence a *hectometer* is 100 meters; a *centimeter* is a hundredth of a meter.
