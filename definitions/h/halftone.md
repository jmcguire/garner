# halftone

halftone \(= \[1\] a musical interval equivalent to 1/12 of an octave; \[2\] a method of black-and-white photographic printing in which different shades of gray are depicted by different concentrations of dots; or \[3\] a photograph printed by this method\), a compound dating in sense 1 from the 1600s, has been predominantly one solid word in AmE since about 1920. \**Half tone* and *half-tone* are traditional BrE variants.

Current ratio in print \(*halftone* vs. *half-tone* vs. *\*half tone*\): 12:4:1
