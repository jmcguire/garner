# hands and knees

hands and knees. The mental image of a person begging has the pleader on knees. Yet speakers often embellish that image unnecessarily—e.g.: "The Muncie City Council's finance committee last week made final recommendations on next year's municipal budget after listening to tax protesters do everything but beg on *hands and knees* \[read *their knees*\]." Larry Riley, "Muncie City Council Failed to Curtail Spending," *Star Press* (Muncie, Ind.), 23 Sept. 2007, at A7.
