# hippopotamus

hippopotamus. Pl. *hippopotamuses* or *hippopotami*. The anglicized plural \(*-muses*\) appears almost four times as often as the other in journalistic publications. But when scientific sources are added to the mix of print sources, *hippopotami* has predominated since the late 1700s. See plurals \(b\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*hippopotami* vs. *hippopotamuses*\): 1.4:1
