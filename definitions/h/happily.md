# happily

happily means "fortunately," not "in a happy manner," when used as here:

• "*Happily*, the title sequence, which always gives me a delicious feeling of nervous apprehension, is unchanged." William Leith, "Television: Saturday 14 September," *Observer*, 8 Sept. 1996, at 58.

• "*Happily*, some things never change." Craig Smith, "Music Hall Madness Will Reign Again," *Santa Fe New Mexican*, 30 Nov. 1996, Feliz Navidad §, at 10.

See adverbs \(f\). Cf. **hopefully**.
