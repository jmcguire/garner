# Halloween

Halloween, a contracted form of *All Hallow Even*, is so spelled. *\*Hallowe'en* was predominant across World Englishes until the mid-1940s. The solid form overtook the contracted spelling in the 1940s in AmE and then in the 1970s in BrE.

Since the mid-1940s, the empty-looking misspelling \**Holloween* has occasionally appeared. But it's comparatively rare.

 - Current ratio in print (*Halloween* vs. *\*Hallowe'en* vs. \**Holloween*): 2,414:117:1
