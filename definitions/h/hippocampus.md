# hippocampus

hippocampus \(= \[1\] a mythological sea monster with the head and front legs of a horse and the tail of a dolphin or fish; or \[2\] a ridge along the lower section of each ventricle of the brain, where memory is stored\) has predominantly formed the plural *hippocampi* since the 1700s—in AmE and BrE alike. *\*Hippocampuses* is a rare variant.

Current ratio in print \(*hippocampi* vs. *\*hippocampuses*\): 55:1
