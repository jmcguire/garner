# horsey

horsey; \***horsy**. For this adjective \(as opposed to the diminutive noun *horsie*\), the standard spelling has been *horsey* since the 1700s—in AmE and BrE alike. The *SOED* misgauges the historical evidence by listing *\*horsy* first.

 - Current ratio in print \(*horsey face* vs. *\*horsy face*\): 4:1
