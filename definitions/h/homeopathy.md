# homeopathy

homeopathy \(= a therapeutic system, developed in the 1700s, premised on the idea that "like cures like," that infinitesimally small doses of medicine are best, and that only one medicine should be taken at a time\) is pronounced /hoh-mee-**ah**-pә-thee/. The corresponding adjective is *homeopathic* /hoh-mee-ә-**path**-ik/.
