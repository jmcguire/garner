# hindrance

hindrance (= \[1\] someone or something that makes the accomplishment of something difficult; or \[2\] the act of making something difficult for someone to do something) is two syllables: /**hin**-drin\[t\]s/. Avoid the epenthetic schwa between the two syllables: there is no *hinder* in *hindrance*.

 - Current ratio in print (*hindrance* vs. *\*hinderance*): 40:1
