# hardly

hardly. In dialect, this word appears in at least three erroneous forms: *\*can't hardly* \(for *can hardly*\), *\*not hardly* \(for *hardly*\), and *\*without hardly* \(for *almost without*\). See **can hardly** & **\*not hardly**. Cf. **scarcely any**.

## Language-Change Index

1. *\*can't hardly* for *can hardly*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*can hardly* vs. *\*cannot hardly*\): 75:1

2. *\*not hardly* for *hardly*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*is hardly* vs. *\*is not hardly*\): 208:1

3. *\*without hardly* for *without*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*without being* vs. *\*without hardly being*\): 80,498:1
