# habitability

habitability; \***inhabitability**. Because of confusion over the prefix *in-*, which is intensive and not negative in *\*inhabitability* \(or, e.g., in *inflammable*\), today the positive form is *habitability* and the negative form *uninhabitability*. *\*Inhabitability* has never been much used—and whenever it is used it's ambiguous. See **flammable** & negatives \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*habitability* vs. \**inhabitability*\): 19:1
