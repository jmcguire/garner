# hierarchy

hierarchy \(= \[1\] a system of organization by which people or things are ranked by importance; or \[2\] the upper echelon of an organization\), a French/Latin/Greek loanword dating in English from the 1300s, is so spelled. Since the mid-1960s, the word has occasionally been misspelled **higherarchy*.

Current ratio in print \(*hierarchy* vs. \**higherarchy*\): 117,810:1
