# hobo

hobo \(= \[1\] a homeless, impecunious vagrant; or \[2\] a migratory worker\), an Americanism dating from the late 1800s, has traditionally formed the plural *hoboes*—not *hobos*. But today the two forms are close competitors. See plurals \(d\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*hoboes* vs. *hobos*\): 1:1
