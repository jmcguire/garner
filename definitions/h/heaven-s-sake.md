# heaven's sake

heaven's sake. So written—not \(as often erroneously written\) *\*heaven's sakes*, *\*heavens sake*, *\*heaven sakes*, or *\*heavens' sake*.

Current ratio in print \(*heaven's sake* vs. \**heaven's sakes* vs. \**heavens sake* vs. \**heaven sakes* vs. \**heavens' sake*\): 255:5:2.4:1.6:1
