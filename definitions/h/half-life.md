# half-life

half-life \(= the length of time it takes a radioactive substance to lose half its radioactivity\), a compound dating from the early 20th century, has been predominantly so hyphenated since 1935. \**Half life* and \**halflife* are variants.

 - Current ratio in print \(*half-life* vs. \**halflife* vs. \**half life*\): 23:1.1:1
