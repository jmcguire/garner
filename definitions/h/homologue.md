# homologue

homologue /**hom**-mә-log/ or /**hoh**-mә-log/ \(= some organ, part, or thing that corresponds in structure, type, or position to some other organ, part, or thing\), a French/Greek loanword dating in English from the mid-1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled—not **homolog*. Today, though, the competition is close.

Current ratio in print \(*homologue* vs. \**homolog*\): 1:1.6
