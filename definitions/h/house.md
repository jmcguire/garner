# house

house; **home**. In the best usage, the structure is always called a *house*. So it is not good form to speak of a recently built *home* that hasn't yet been sold. Nor should one point to the building and call it one's *home*: it's a *house* except in non-U speech. (See class distinctions.) The word *home* connotes familial ties.

The plural *houses* should be pronounced /**howz**-әz/, not /**hows**-әz/.
