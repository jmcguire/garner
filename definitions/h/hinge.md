# hinge

hinge \(= \[1\] to attach to a fastener that swings open and shut; or \[2\] to depend completely on\), a verb popular since about 1800, makes *hinging*, not *\*hingeing*. So the inflected form is analogous to *cringing*, *fringing*, *lunging*, and *tinging*—and not to *bingeing* and *whingeing*. \(See the entries for those other words.\)

 - Current ratio in print \(*hinging on* vs. *\*hingeing on*\): 48:1
