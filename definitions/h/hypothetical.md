# hypothetical

hypothetical \(= based on an imagined or postulated situation\), dating from the 1500s, has always been the predominant adjective. \**Hypothetic*, dating from the 1600s, is a variant. The word *hypothetical* also functions as a noun meaning "a postulated hypothesis or condition."

 - Current ratio in print \(*hypothetical* vs. \**hypothetic*\): 130:1
