# handicapped

handicapped. The phrases *\*handicapped-accessible* and *\*handicap-accessible* are illogical. Although *wheelchair-accessible* makes sense (accessible by wheelchair), *\*handicapped-accessible* does not—unless we do some contortions to suggest that it means "accessible to the handicapped."

Through the vicissitudes of usage, *handicapped* has become nearly taboo in BrE. It is much less so in AmE, in which *handicapped parking* (so written) is a well-known example of hypallage.

 - Current ratio in print (*handicapped parking* vs. *\*handicap parking*): 4:1
