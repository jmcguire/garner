# hitherto

hitherto; **thitherto**. *Hitherto* = heretofore; up to this time. *Thitherto* = theretofore; up to that time. Although *hitherto* often appeared in print from 1600 to 1850, it has been in steady decline since then; *thitherto* has always been rare. Both words are classifiable as archaisms. Cf. **up to now**.
