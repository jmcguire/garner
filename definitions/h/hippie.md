# hippie

hippie (= someone, esp. in the late 1960s and 1970s, who was fervently antiestablishment, as characterized by unusual clothes, nontraditional hairstyles, and drug-taking), a noun dating from 1965, has always been predominantly so spelled. *Hippy*, a variant spelling of the word, is also an adjective meaning "having large hips."

 - Current ratio in print (*hippie commune* vs. \**hippy commune*): 4:1
