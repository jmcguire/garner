# hee-haw

hee-haw \(= \[1\] a donkey's bray; or \[2\] a crude loud laugh\), an onomatopoeic word dating from the early 1800s, has been predominantly so written since the 1840s. \**Heehaw* and \**hee haw* are variants.

 - Current ratio in print \(*hee-haw* vs. \**heehaw* vs. \**hee haw*\): 8:2.2:1
