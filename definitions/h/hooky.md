# hooky

hooky \(= a truant student\), a noun dating from the mid-1800s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE only since about 1945. In pre-1945 AmE, *hookey* was the usual spelling. In BrE, *hooky* and *hookey* vied for predominance from the 1920s until about 2005, when *hooky* took over. \**Hookie* is a variant form.

Current ratio in print \(*played hooky* vs. *played hookey* vs. \**played hookie* in World Englishes\): 68:27:1
