# homage

homage is traditionally pronounced /**hom**-ij/. The word was anglicized many centuries ago. It is a silly \(but increasingly common\) pretension to use the Frenchified pronunciation /oh-**mahzh**/. Cf. **herb** & **humble**, adj.

1850 ratio in print \(*a homage* vs. \**an homage*\): 3:1 1960 ratio in print \(*a homage* vs. \**an homage*\): 3:1
Current ratio in print \(*a homage* vs. \**an homage*\): 1:1.6
