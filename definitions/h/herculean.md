# Herculean

Herculean is pronounced either /hәr-**kyoo**-lee-әn/ or /hәr-kyә-**lee**-әn/. Although the first of these was traditionally considered the better pronunciation, today the second predominates. The word has traditionally been capitalized—since the 1600s. For the period from 1865 until 1927, the lowercase *herculean* was predominant in English-language books.

 - Current ratio in print (*Herculean* vs. *herculean*): 1.5:1
