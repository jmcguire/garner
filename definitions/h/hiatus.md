# hiatus

hiatus. The standard plural has been *hiatuses* since about 1800. The Latinate plural *hiatus* is not only rare but also liable to cause confusion. (See plurals (b).) *\*Hiati* is a malformed plural—e.g.: "They have offers from Columbia for four movies during their *hiati* \[read *hiatuses*\]." Army Archerd, "Just for Variety," *Daily Variety*, 10 Nov. 1992. See hypercorrection (a). Cf. **apparatus**.

## Language-Change Index

 - *\*hiati* for *hiatuses*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*hiatuses* vs. *\*hiati*): 48:1
