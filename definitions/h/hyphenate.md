# hyphenate

hyphenate; **hyphen**, vb.; **\*hyphenize**. *Hyphenate* had become the standard verb in AmE by 1900 and in BrE by 1940. In BrE, *hyphen* is a variant. *\*Hyphenize* is a needless variant in both AmE and BrE—except perhaps in the sense analogous to *hyphenization* described in the immediately following entry.

Current ratio in print \(*hyphenated* vs. *hyphened* vs. *\*hyphenized*\): 1,136:13:1
