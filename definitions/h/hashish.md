# hashish

hashish \(a strong form of cannabis\) has been an English word since the late 1500s. In its early years, the Arabic transliteration took on a wild variety of forms—almost as if the users of the word had been users of the stuff. During the 1800s, the two spellings *hashish* and \**hasheesh* vied for predominance, and by 1900 *hashish* had decisively won the battle. It remains the standard form.

Current ratio in print \(*hashish* vs. \**hasheesh*\): 17:1
