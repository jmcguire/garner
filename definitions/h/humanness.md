# humanness

humanness. So spelled—not *\*humaness*. But the misspelling is fairly common—e.g.: "*Humaness* \[read *Humanness*\] is overpowered by conformity and order, and beauty is rendered irrelevant." Margaret Hawkins, "Sculptures Pit Uniqueness vs. Uniformity," *Chicago Sun-Times*, 18 Mar. 1994, Weekend Plus §, at 27.

## Language-Change Index

 - *humanness* misspelled *\*humaness*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*humanness* vs. \**humaness*): 564:1
