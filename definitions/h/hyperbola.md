# hyperbola

hyperbola /hī-**pәr**-bә-lә/ \(= in geometry, a curved line that moves so that the difference of its distances from two fixed points is constant\) has formed the standard plural *hyperbolas* since the 1700s—the classical plural *\*hyperbolae* /hī-**pәr**-bә-lee/ being both a variant and an unfortunate homophone of *hyperbole*.

Current ratio in print \(*hyperbolas* vs. *\*hyperbolae*\): 6:1
