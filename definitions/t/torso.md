# torso

torso (= \[1\] the trunk of a human body; or \[2\] a sculpture of the trunk of a human body), an Italian loanword dating in English from the early 1700s, has predominantly formed the plural *torsos* since the early 1890s. The Italianate plural \**torsi* is a variant in English.

 - Current ratio in print (*two torsos* vs. \**two torsi*): 32:1 2017 ratio in print (*two torsos* vs. *two \*torsoes*): 299:1
