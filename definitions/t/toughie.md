# toughie

toughie (= \[1\] a tough person; esp., a ruffian or thug; or \[2\] an exceedingly difficult problem or situation), a noun dating from the early 20th century, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since about 1936 and in BrE since about 1945. \**Toughy* is a variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*toughie* vs. \**toughy*): 15:1
