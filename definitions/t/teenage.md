# teenage

teenage, adj.; \***teenaged**; **\*teen-age**; **\*teen-aged**. The first has been the standard spelling since the early 1960s. The others are variants. In its 2002 revision, the *AP Stylebook* finally dropped the hyphenated forms. But for now they live on in *The New Yorker*.

 - Current ratio in print (*teenage* vs. \**teenaged* vs. \**teen-age* vs. \**teen-aged*): 258:20:2.1:1
