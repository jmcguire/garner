# transferable

transferable. Since the 1700s, the word has been so spelled in AmE and BrE alike—not *\*transferrable* or *\*transferrible*. This despite the accent on the second syllable: /tranz-**fәr**-ә-bәl/. In this way, the word is anomalous. Cf. **deferrable** & **inferable**.

 - Current ratio in print (*transferable* vs. *\*transferrable* vs. *\*transferrible*): 25,002:2,377:1
