# Turkmen

Turkmen. For a citizen of Turkmenistan, this is the standard term. The plural is *Turkmens*. See demonyms.

Two archaic variants are *Turcoman* (prevalent from the 1700s to about 1875) and *Turkoman* (prevalent from about 1875 to 1950). They formed their plurals in *-mans*, not *-men*. *Turkmen* has been vastly predominant since about 1950.

 - Current ratio in print (*Turkmen* vs. *Turkoman* vs. *Turcoman*): 10:1.4:1
