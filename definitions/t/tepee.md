# tepee

tepee \(= a conical tent made by American Indians of the Plains and Great Lakes regions\) is the standard spelling of this quintessentially American word, whether in AmE or BrE. *\*Teepee* and *\*tipi* have been variants since the 1700s.

 - Current ratio in print \(*tepee* vs. *\*tipi* vs. *\*teepee*\): 1.3:1:1
