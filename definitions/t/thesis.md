# thesis

thesis \(= \[1\] a long research paper prepared as part of a degree requirement; \[2\] an idea or opinion posited on a subject; or \[3\] the statement of an essay's primary point\) makes the plural *theses* /**thee**-seez/.

 - Current ratio in print \(*theses* vs. *\*thesises*\): 3,765:1
