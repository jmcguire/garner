# titillate

titillate; **titivate**. *Titillate* = to cause arousal or excitement, esp. of a sexual nature <titillating photographs>. *Titivate* = to smarten or spruce up <speed-dating aficionados who relentlessly titivate themselves>. A variant spelling of the latter is *\*tittivate*. For the difference between *titillate* and *tantalize*, see **tantalize**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*titivate* vs. *\*tittivate*\): 15:1
