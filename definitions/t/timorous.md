# timorous

timorous; **temerarious**. These similar-sounding words are antonyms. *Timorous* /**tim**-ә-rәs/ = fearfully reluctant to act. (It's related to *timid*.) *Temerarious* /tem-ә-**rair**-ee-әs/ = foolhardily daring; rash and reckless. (It's related to *temerity*. See **temerity**.) The latter, a literary word, has never been in widespread use.
