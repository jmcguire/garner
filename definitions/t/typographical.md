# typographical

typographical; **typographic**. Generally speaking, the first is preferred in both AmE and BrE—but each appears on both sides of the Atlantic. (See -ic/-ical.) In AmE, *typographical* is the usual choice when the following word begins with a vowel. It's most often used in the set phrase *typographical error*.

 - Current ratio in print (*typographical error* vs. *\*typographic error*): 19:1
