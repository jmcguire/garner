# tempo

tempo (= \[1\] the speed at which music is or should be played; or \[2\] the speed at which something happens), an Italian loanword dating in English from the early 1700s, has predominantly formed the anglicized plural *tempos* since about 1945 in AmE and 1982 in BrE (in which the Italianate *tempi* remains just about as common).

 - Current ratio in print (*slow tempos* vs. *slow tempi*): 1.9:1
 - Current ratio in print (*faster tempos* vs. *faster tempi*): 2.4:1
