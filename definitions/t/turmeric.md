# turmeric

turmeric /**tәr**-mә-rik/ \(= the yellow powder used as a spice, esp. in Indian food\), a word of uncertain origin dating from the 1400s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Tumeric* is an infrequent misspelling \(and mispronunciation\).

Current ratio in print \(*turmeric* vs. \**tumeric*\): 160:1
