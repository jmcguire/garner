# together

together appears in many a redundancy, such as *\*blend together*, *\*connect together*, *\*consolidate together*, *\*couple together*, and *\*merge together*. These phrases should be avoided, except when part of a set phrase (e.g., *join together* in a marriage ceremony). See *\***associate together** & *\***congregate together**. Cf. **mutual (a)**.

For the distinction between *altogether* and *all together*, see **altogether**.
