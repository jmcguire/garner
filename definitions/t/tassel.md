# tassel

tassel (= a hanging ornament made of threads or strips) is sometimes misspelled *\*tassle*—e.g.: "Shauvin spends at least an hour on weekends polishing and fixing her motorcycle with the shiny, gold skulls and long leather *tassles* \[read *tassels*\]." Virginia de Leon, "Va-Va-Va-Vroooom," *Spokesman Rev.* (Spokane), 10 Aug. 1997, at E1.

As a verb, *tassel* makes *tasseled* and *tasseling* in AmE, *tasselled* and *tasselling* in BrE. See spelling (c).
