# typography

typography; **topography**. *Typography* = the study and techniques of using type in printing, esp. as a designer or a typesetter. *Topography* = the three-dimensional shape of terrain. On occasion the first word gets misused for the second—e.g.: "The highest and best use of the property is the mining of limestone, says the appeal, ‘given the *typography* \[read *topography*\], the high grade of plattin limestone deposits, the character of the surrounding property and the historic use of the subject property and the surrounding property.' " Ralph Dummit, "Pressure Prompts Hearing on Quarry," *St. Louis Post-Dispatch*, 12 Aug. 1998, St. Charles Post §, at 1.
