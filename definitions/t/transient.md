# transient

transient, adj.; **transitory**; **transitive**. *Transient* = coming and going; impermanent; temporary <transient workers>. *Transitory* (= fleeting) has virtually the same meaning but is more commonly applied to things or events than to people <transitory renown>. *Transitive* is a grammatical term denoting a verb that takes a direct object <transitive verbs>.

*Transient* is best pronounced /**tran**-shәnt/ in AmE. In BrE it is pronounced with three syllables: /**tran**-zee-әnt/ or /-see-/.
