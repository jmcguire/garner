# tinge

tinge \(= to imbue with a trace of color, emotion, or some other quality\), for the present participle, makes *tinging*, not *\*tingeing*. So the inflected form is analogous to *cringing*, *fringing*, *hinging*, and *lunging*—not to *bingeing* and *whingeing*. \(See the entries for those other words.\)

 - Current ratio in print \(*tinging* vs. \**tingeing*\): 1.1:1
