# tonsillar

tonsillar /**ton\[t\]**-si-lәr/ (= of, relating to, affected by, or involving the tonsils), a Latin loanword dating in English from the early 1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled in all varieties of English. **Tonsilar* is a variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*tonsillar* vs. \**tonsilar*): 51:1
