# tercentenary

tercentenary; **\*tricentennial**; **\*tercentennial**; **\*tricentenary**. Since the mid-1800s, the first has been the usual spelling for both the noun meaning "a period of 300 years" or "a 300-year anniversary," and the adjective meaning "of, relating to, or involving a 300-year period or anniversary." The other three are variants. See **centennial**.

*Tercentenary* is pronounced /tәr-sәn-**ten**-ә-ree/ or /tәr-**sen**-tә-ner-ee/.

 - Current ratio in print \(*tercentenary* vs. \**tricentennial* vs. \**tercentennial* vs. \**tricentenary*\): 26:4:3:1
