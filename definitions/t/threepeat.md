# threepeat

threepeat, a portmanteau word dating from late-1980s AmE, denotes a third consecutive championship won by a competitor or team. It is wordplay that combines *three* and *repeat*. In sports talk, the word has become something of a vogue term. It is also used as a verb \<do you think the team might threepeat?\>. It should be solid, as shown, not hyphenated.
