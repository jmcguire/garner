# tablespoonful

tablespoonful \(= the amount of something that fills a tablespoon\), a compound dating from the early 1700s, has been predominantly so written since about 1830—in AmE and BrE alike. \**Tablespoonsful* is a variant. \(Cf. **spoonful.\)** Pl. *tablespoonfuls*. See plurals \(g\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*tablespoonfuls* vs. \**tablespoonsful*\): 15:1
