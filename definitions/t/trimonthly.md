# trimonthly

trimonthly \(= \[1\] occurring once every three months; or \[2\] lasting for three months\) is typically inferior to the more common *quarterly*—e.g.: "The money that they collect this month will allow them to continue to provide such services as publishing a *trimonthly* \[read *quarterly*\] newsletter." Darren Becker, "Montreal Centre Wages Campaign for Peace," *Montreal Gaz.*, 15 Dec. 1996, at D3.

It's wrong to use *trimonthly* in the sense "three times a month," as some writers do. Cf. bi-.
