# tranquillity

tranquillity; **\*tranquility**. Since the 1600s, *tranquillity* has been considered the standard spelling, and *\*tranquility* a variant. This despite the spelling *\*tranquility* in the preamble to the U.S. Constitution. But since about 1980, the two spellings have been in close competition in AmE. On this linguistic front, things remain more placid in BrE.

 - Current ratio in print \(*tranquillity* vs. \**tranquility*\): 1.3:1
