# tracheotomy

tracheotomy; **tracheostomy**. The two are distinct. *Tracheotomy* (dating from the early 1700s) refers to any surgical cutting through the skin into the trachea. *Tracheostomy* (dating from the early 1900s) refers to the surgical formation of a hole through the neck and into the trachea to allow the placement of a tube. Since the early 1960s, *tracheostomy* has appeared more frequently in print.
