# tasty

tasty; **tasteful**. Although *tasteless* serves as the antonym of both—and although *tasteful* once meant "appetizing"—the differentiation between the words has been complete since the late 1800s. *Tasty* (= pleasing to the palate; delicious; appetizing) is literal. *Tasteful* (= having and displaying refinement in manners, the arts, and other matters of aesthetic judgment) is figurative.
