# trip odometer

trip odometer; **tripmeter**; \***tripometer**. For the vehicle instrument that records the distance of an individual journey as determined by the operator, *trip odometer* has been the standard term since emerging in the early 20th century. The shortened compound *tripmeter* has grown in popularity especially since 1980. Occasionally \**tripometer* /tri-**pom**-i-tәr/ is heard, but it is significantly less common than *tripmeter*.

 - Current ratio in print (*trip odometer* vs. *tripmeter*): 4:1
