# troubled

troubled; **troublesome**; **troublous**. *Troubled* = (1) anxious or worried \<he had a troubled look\>; or (2) having lots of problems \<a troubled relationship\>. *Troublesome* = causing many pesky problems \<troublesome dietary restrictions\>. *Troublous* = (1) stormy and difficult \<these troublous times\>; or (2) causing serious trouble; troublesome \<a troublous position to take in the litigation\>. *Troublous* is a literary, old-fashioned word.
