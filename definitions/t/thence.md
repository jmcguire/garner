# thence

thence; **whence**; **hence**. *Thence* = from that place or source; from that reason or fact. *Whence* = from what source, cause, or place. *Hence* = (1) for this reason; therefore; (2) from this source; (3) from this time; from now; or (4) from this place; away. They're literary archaisms—except for *hence* in sense 1. See **hence** & **whence**.
