# tussah

tussah; **tussore**; **tussar**. A 16th-century Hindi loanword denoting a type of silk that first became widely popular in the late 1800s, *tussah* is the standard spelling in AmE; *tussore* in BrE; and *tussar* in Indian English. Across World Englishes, *tussah* has predominated since about 1990.
