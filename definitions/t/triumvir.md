# triumvir

triumvir (= one of three officers forming an administrative or rulemaking group, which is called a *triumvirate*) forms the plural *triumvirs* or (less good) *\*triumviri*. (See plurals (b).) The word is pronounced /trī-**әm**-vәr/.

 - Current ratio in print (*triumvirs* vs. *\*triumviri*): 8:1
