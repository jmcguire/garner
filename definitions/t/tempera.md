# tempera

tempera; **tempora**; **tempura**. *Tempera* /**tem**-pә-rә/ is a type of paint in which the color is mixed with a thick liquid, such as egg yolk. *Tempora* /**tem**-por-ah/ is the Latin plural of *tempus* "time"; it appears in the Latin exclamation *O tempora\! O mores\!* ("Oh, the times\! Oh, the customs\!"). *Tempura* /tem-**pʊr**-ә/ is a type of Japanese food consisting of fish or vegetables battered and fried.
