# tortellini

tortellini; **tortelloni**. *Tortellini*, the more usual word, normally denotes small squares of pasta that are formed into rings or hat shapes after stuffing. While *tortellini* is both singular and plural in English, the Italian singular is *tortellino* (= a small cake or fritter). Depending on the cookbook you consult, *tortelloni* either refers to a larger version of *tortellini* or is another name for *ravioli*.
