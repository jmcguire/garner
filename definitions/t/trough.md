# trough

trough (= \[1\] a long narrow container for animals' water or food; \[2\] a channel for moving water; \[3\] a short period of low activity, depressed prices, etc.; \[4\] the hollow area between two waves; or \[5\] in meteorology, an area of low pressure) is pronounced /trof/ and mispronounced /troth/ or /troh/.
