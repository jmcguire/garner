# triennium

triennium \(= a period of three years\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the mid-1800s, has predominantly formed the Latinate plural *triennia* since 1958 in AmE—before which time that form was in close competition with *trienniums*. In BrE, *triennia* appears to be the exclusive form of the plural. See plurals \(b\).

Current ratio in print \(*triennia* vs. *trienniums* in World Englishes\): 3:1
