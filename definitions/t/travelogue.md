# travelogue

travelogue \(= a lecture or film documentary about travel to a particular place\) is the standard spelling in AmE and BrE alike. *\*Travelog* is a variant. \(See **catalogue**, **dialogue** & **pedagogue**.\) For a comment on the potential decline of the *-ue* form, see -agog\(ue\).

Current ratio in print \(*travelogue* vs. *\*travelog*\): 109:1
