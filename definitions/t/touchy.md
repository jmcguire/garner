# touchy

touchy; **tetchy**; **techy**. *Touchy* = (1) oversensitive; irritable <he was a bit touchy during the interview>; or (2) requiring caution or tact in handling <a touchy subject>. Even though it predates *touchy*, *tetchy* (as well as its alternative spelling *techy*) is now a variant (in sense 1). *Tetchy* occurs more often in BrE than in AmE.

For *techy* as an adjective meaning "involving one or more techies" (technology whizzes), see **techie**.
