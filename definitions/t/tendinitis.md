# tendinitis

tendinitis; **\*tendonitis**. *Tendinitis* \(= inflammation of a tendon\), a medieval Latin loanword dating in English from about 1900, takes its spelling directly from the Late Latin word. *\*Tendonitis*, incorrectly arrived at by association with the spelling of the English noun *tendon*, has become fairly common since the 1950s. See spelling \(b\). For a strained misuse, see **tendentious \(d\)**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*tendinitis* vs. \**tendonitis*\): 1.4:1
