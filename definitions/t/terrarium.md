# terrarium

terrarium \(= a large clear container for growing indoor plants or keeping small animals such as snakes and turtles\), a modern latinism dating in English from the late 1800s, has predominantly formed the anglicized plural *terrariums* since the 1950s in AmE and the 1990s in BrE. \**Terraria* is a variant.

Current ratio in print \(*terrariums* vs. \**terraria*\): 5:1
