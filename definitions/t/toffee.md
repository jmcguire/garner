# toffee

toffee \(= the sticky brown candy made by boiling water, sugar, and butter together\), originally an altered form of the word *taffy* in the early 1800s, has been predominantly so spelled in BrE since about 1875 and in AmE since about ten years later. \**Toffy* is a variant.

*Toffee-nosed*, by the way, is a derogatory Briticism meaning "conceited and snobbish" <toffee-nosed hypocrites>.

Current ratio in print \(*toffee* vs. \**toffy*\): 42:1   
Current ratio in print \(*toffee-nosed* vs. \**toffy-nosed*\): 22:1
