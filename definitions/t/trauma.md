# trauma

trauma, in pathology, means "a serious wound or shock to the body," but in popular contexts it has been largely confined to figurative (emotional) senses. (Cf. **insult.)** Pl. *traumas*—preferably not the pretentious *\*traumata* (in steep decline since about 1960).

 - Current ratio in print (*traumas* vs. *\*traumata*): 32:1
