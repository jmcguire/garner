# transmissible

transmissible \(= \[1\] capable of being sent out; or \[2\] capable of being passed from one person or place to another\), a French/Latin loanword dating in English from the 1600s, has long been predominant in AmE and BrE alike. **Transmittable*, \**transmissable*, and \**transmittible* are variants.

Current ratio in print \(*transmissible* vs. \**transmittable* vs. \**transmissable* vs. \**transmittible*\): 233:36:1.6:1
