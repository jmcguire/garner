# transfusable

transfusable. Despite the worldwide predominance of *fusible*, not *\*fusable*, the spelling *transfusable* has become standard in AmE and BrE alike. That spelling overtook the traditional *\*transfusible* in the 1960s and 1970s and is now unquestionably preponderant. See -able (a).

 - Current ratio in print (*transfusable* vs. \**transfusible*): 1.4:1
