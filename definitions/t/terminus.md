# terminus

terminus; **terminal**, n. *Terminus* = the place at the end of a travel route, esp. a railroad or bus line. Pl. *termini* or \(much less commonly\) *terminuses*. \(See plurals \(b\).) *Terminal* = \(1\) a station on a transportation route; \(2\) a computer workstation; or \(3\) a point for connecting wires in an electrical circuit.

Current ratio in print \(*termini* vs. *terminuses*\): 50:1
