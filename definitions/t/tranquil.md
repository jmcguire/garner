# tranquil

tranquil (= pleasantly calm, peaceful, and quiet), a French/Latin loanword dating in English from the 1400s, has most commonly made a periphrastic comparative (*more tranquil*) and a periphrastic superlative (*most tranquil*), not inflected ones (*tranquiller*, *tranquiler*; *tranquillest*, *tranquilest*). But among the inflected forms, the *-ll-* spellings are more common in AmE and BrE alike.
