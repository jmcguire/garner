# triage

triage (= a utilitarian and humanitarian method of deciding who receives medical treatment first according to how serious the need is) is sometimes misused for *trio*—e.g.:

 - "At points during the monstrous task of consumption, the flavors changed from just bacon and egg to the full *triage* \[read *trio*\] of bacon, eggs and beef, and then just beef and egg." BryAnn Becker, "Whisk & Chop Aims for Diner Feel, Misses Mark," *Argus Leader* (Sioux Falls), 9 May 2010, at D1.

 - "Durham said that the *triage* \[read *trio*\] of drama, dance, and vocals was part of the what made the musical a success." "Leon High School Drama Students Performing in Musical '13,'" *Tallahassee Democrat*, 13 Mar. 2011, Breaking News §.
