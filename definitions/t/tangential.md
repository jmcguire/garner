# tangential

tangential (= peripheral, incidental) is the standard form in AmE and BrE alike. *\*Tangental* is a needless variant. Occasionally the word is misspelled *\*tangenital*, which is something of a malapropism resulting from metathesis—e.g.: "In an effort to ensure at least a *tangenital* \[read *tangential*\] spot in boxing, Hearns in January will don a new suit of clothes." W.H. Stickney Jr., " ‘Hit Man' Gets into Promoting," *Houston Chron.*, 1 Oct. 1995, at 30.
