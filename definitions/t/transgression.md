# transgression

transgression for *transition* is a malapropism—e.g.: "Ms. Ash said the laser center ‘is a natural *transgression* \[read *transition*\] into a new technology.' " Raquel Santiago, "Firms Turn Eyes to Laser Surgery," *Crain's Cleveland Bus.*, 29 Jan. 1996, at 3.

## Language-Change Index

 - *transgression* misused for *transition*: Stage 1
