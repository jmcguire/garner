# typing

typing; **typewriting**. *Typing* is the standard term for operating an alphanumeric keyboard whether done on a typewriter, a computer, a PDA, or any other device. *Typewriting* refers to typing on a typewriter; on other devices it's become an obsolescent variant. *Typewriting* continues to be a useful term for (1) the act of operating a typewriter, or (2) the words physically printed on paper by a typewriter.
