# farrago

farrago \(= a confused or jumbled mixture; hodgepodge\) is pronounced /fә-**ray**-go/ or /fә-**rah**-go/ and makes either of two plurals: *farragoes* and *farragos*. The *-oes* spelling is somewhat more common. See plurals \(d\).

Current ratio in print \(*farragoes* vs. *farragos*\): 1.5:1
