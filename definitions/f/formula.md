# formula

formula. Pl. *formulas* or *formulae* /**form**-yә-lee/. The native plural, ending in *s*, predominates in all but scientific writing—e.g.: "Their appearances, after all, are mostly ceremonial, and they are expected to mouth empty and generally comforting *formulae* \[read *formulas*\]." Richard Mitchell, *Less than Words Can Say* 77 \(1979\). See plurals \(b\).

Current ratio in print \(*mathematical formulas* vs. *mathematical formulae*\): 1.7:1
