# flexion

flexion; \***flection**. *Flexion* = \(1\) the act of bending or flexing; esp., the bending of a joint with flexor muscles; or \(2\) a bend in something. \**Flection* is a needless variant that has never been common in any sense. Among American desktop dictionaries, only *W11* and *NOAD* correctly list *flexion* as the main headword for all senses.

 - Current ratio in print \(*flexion* vs. \**flection*\): 194:1
