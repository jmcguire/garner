# footsie

footsie \(= \[1\] an under-the-table flirtation in which someone playfully touches feet with someone else; or \[2\] secret cooperation or negotiation that occurs dishonestly\), a casualism dating from the 1940s, has always been predominantly so spelled. **Footsy* is a variant.

Among the reduplicative synonyms are *footsie-tootsie* and *footsie-wootsie*—both of which excite much merriment between the players.

 - Current ratio in print \(*play footsie* vs. \**play footsy*\): 10:1
