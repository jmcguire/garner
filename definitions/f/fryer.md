# fryer

fryer \(= \[1\] someone who fries; \[2\] something intended for frying, esp. a small chicken; or \[3\] a large frying pan\) has been predominantly so spelled since the late 1930s in AmE and the early 1960s in BrE. Today *fryer* appears in print sources more than 40 times as often as *\*frier*, a needless variant. Of course, *fryer* shouldn't be confused with *friar* \(= a member of a mendicant religious order\).
