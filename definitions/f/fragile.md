# fragile

fragile; **frangible**; **friable**. *Fragile* /**fraj**-il/ or, esp. in BrE, /**fra**-jīl/, the most common term, means "delicate, brittle" \<a fragile vase\> \<a fragile ego\>. *Frangible* means "breakable" but doesn't necessarily imply inherent weakness \<frangible bones\>. *Friable* means "crumbly" \<friable asbestos\>. See **friable**.
