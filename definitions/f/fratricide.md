# fratricide

fratricide (= \[1\] the killing of one's brother; or \[2\] someone who kills his or her own brother) has been conscripted by the military to denote (1) the accidental or intentional killing of one's brother- or sister-in-arms, also known as *friendly fire*; or (2) the detonation or disablement of one nuclear warhead by the explosion of another missile aimed at the same target. See -cide.
