# fist bump

fist bump; **fist pump**. A *fist bump* is a greeting made by bumping knuckles. A *fist pump* is a celebratory gesture made by holding out a fist with the elbow cocked and jerking down \(or sometimes up or away from the body\). Although the terms are sometimes confounded, this writer got both right: "She looked at the stands and saw the Wolf Pack and gave them a *fist pump*. Andrew came over to her and said, ‘Crab legs with all the trimmings.' Micala smiled and gave her trainer a *fist bump*." D.K. Barnes, *High Country Dreams* 104 \(2005\).
