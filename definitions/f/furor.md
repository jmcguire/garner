# furor

furor; **furore**. *Furor* /**fyʊr**-әr/ is the standard spelling in AmE, *furore* /fyʊ-**ror**-ee/ in BrE. Why the difference? Although both AmE and BrE predominantly adopted the Latin spelling of the word (*furor*) in the 1700s, in the late 1800s BrE publications increasingly adopted the Italian spelling (*furore*), which became unquestionably predominant in BrE during the 1940s.
