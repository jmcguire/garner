# fibula

fibula (= the outer of the two bones between a vertebrate's knee and ankle), perhaps because it appears mostly in technical contexts, predominantly forms the Latinate plural *fibulae* /**fib**-yә-lee/ in AmE and BrE alike—*fibulas* being a variant. Cf. **tibia**. See plurals (b).

 - Current ratio in print (*fibulae* vs. *fibulas*): 6:1
