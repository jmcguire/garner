# fortune-teller

fortune-teller \(= a soothsayer\), a compound dating from the late 1500s, has been predominantly so hyphenated since the early 1700s. \**Fortune teller* and \**fortuneteller* are variants.

Current ratio in print \(*fortune-teller* vs. \**fortune teller* vs. \**fortuneteller*\): 4:2.3:1
