# frisson

frisson /free-**soh*n***/, a gallicism dating in English from the 1700s, means "a shiver of excitement or emotion"—e.g.: " ‘But I don't know what that is,' she answered, trying to persuade herself, as she spoke, that the touch of his hand was giving her a tremendous *frisson*." Aldous Huxley, *Brief Candles* 232 \(1957\).
