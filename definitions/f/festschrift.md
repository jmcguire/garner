# *Festschrift*

*Festschrift*  \(= a collection of writings forming a volume presented by the authors as a tribute to a \[usu. senior\] scholar\), a German loanword, forms the plurals *Festschrifts* and *Festschriften*. The German plural predominates in English-language contexts, just as the German capitalization is retained. For the reasons given at plurals \(b\), it would be understandable if one preferred the anglicized *Festschrifts*. See germanicisms.

Current ratio in print \(*Festschriften* vs. *Festschrifts*\): 3:1
