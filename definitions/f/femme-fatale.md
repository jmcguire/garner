# femme fatale

femme fatale /fem fә-**tal**/ or, in BrE esp., /**fam**/ \(= an attractive, charismatic woman who leads men into compromising situations, danger, or even ruin\), a gallicism, retains its French plural: *femmes fatales*. See plurals \(b\).

Current ratio in print \(*femmes fatales* vs. \**femme fatales*\): 1.9:1
