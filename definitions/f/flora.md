# flora

flora, like *fauna*, is a singular word with a collective implication. It refers to the plant life in a particular region. The word has two plurals, both rarely used: *floras* and *florae*. The first is standard. See plurals \(b\). Cf. **fauna**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*The flora is* vs. *\*The flora are*\): 344:1
