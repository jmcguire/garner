# frangipani

frangipani /fran-ji-**pan**-ee/ (= \[1\] a tropical American shrub; \[2\] a perfume obtained from the shrub's flowers; or \[3\] an almond-flavored tart or custard) is the standard spelling. *\*Frangipane* and *\*frangipanni* are variants.

 - Current ratio in print (*frangipani* vs. *\*frangipane* vs. *\*frangipanni*): 34:15:1
