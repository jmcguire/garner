# flock

flock. Although this word has two plurals, *flocks* and *flock*, the first vastly predominates. E.g.: "Longshore has three *flocks* of hens and one flock of toms for people who want 30- to 40-pound birds." Mike Lafferty, "Turkeys Turn Tail on Cramped Pens," *Columbus Dispatch*, 24 Nov. 1996, at C1.
