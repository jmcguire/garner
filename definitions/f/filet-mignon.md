# filet mignon

filet mignon. The plural of this phrase is *filet mignons* \(or, more stuffily, *filets mignons*\). \(See plurals \(b\).) But when the second word is dropped, the plural is *filets*. While AmE uses the spelling *filet* /fi-**lay**/, BrE spells it *fillet* /**fil**-әt/.

Current ratio in print \(*filet mignons* vs. *filets mignons*\): 1.1:1
