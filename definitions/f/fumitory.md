# fumitory

fumitory \(= a flowering plant of the genus *Fumaria*\) is sometimes misspelled *fumatory*—e.g.: "Here's what's blooming this week: Basil Balm, Bee Balm, Butterfly Bush, Carolina Phlox, Fringe Loosestrife, *Fumatory* \[read *Fumitory*\]." "Botanical Gardens," *Asheville Citizen-Times* \(N.C.\), 5 July 1996, at C2. Actually, *fumatory* is a rare word meaning "a smoking room or section."

## Language-Change Index

*fumatory* misused for *fumitory*: Stage 1
