# fetid

fetid /**fet**-id/ \(= emitting an offensive odor; stinky\) is a formal word <brown fetid water at the bottom of the creek>. The pronunciation /**feet**-id/ is a nonstandard variant. The obsolescent variant spelling *\*foetid* has not been predominant in BrE print sources since the late 1600s. It has always been a recessive spelling across World Englishes. The corresponding noun is *fetor* /**fet**-әr/ \(= stench\).

Current ratio in print \(*fetid* vs. *foetid*\): 7:1
