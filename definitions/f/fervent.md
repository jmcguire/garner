# fervent

fervent; **fervid**; **perfervid**. These adjectives describe three gradations of ardor or zeal (listed also in order of word frequency in print sources). To be *fervent* is either to show warm feelings or to be strongly devoted to something. To be *fervid* is to have those qualities in a stronger degree. And to be *perfervid* is to be fanatically zealous. The prefix *per-* is an intensifier here.
