# for-/fore-

for-/fore-. As you'll observe in many of the following entries, these prefixes cause a great deal of confusion. You can usually arrive at the correct prefix for any given word by remembering that *fore-* always means "before" <forebears = ancestors>. *For-* may mean either "completely" <forfeit = to lose \(something\) completely> or "against" <forbear = to refrain \(from some action\) against one's inclination>.
