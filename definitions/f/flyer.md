# flyer

flyer (= \[1\] a sheet of paper that advertises something; \[2\] an airplane pilot; or \[3\] someone who travels by airplane) is now the predominant spelling in AmE and BrE alike. American desktop dictionaries tend to record the term under the spelling \**flier*, but that spelling predominated in AmE only during the 1940s.

 - Current ratio in print (*distributing flyers* vs. \**distributing fliers*): 4:1
 - Current ratio in print (*frequent flyers* vs. \**frequent fliers*): 2.1:1
 - Current ratio in print (*high flyer* vs. \**high flier*): 3:1
