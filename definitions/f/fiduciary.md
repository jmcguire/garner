# fiduciary

fiduciary, adj. & n.; **fiducial**, adj. For all ordinary purposes, use *fiduciary* as both adjective \<fiduciary relationship\> and noun \<as trustees, they are fiduciaries\>. *Fiducial* is mostly confined to physics and mathematics. A *fiducial line* or *point* is one used as a base—as a boiling point is in thermometry. A *fiducial edge* is the thin edge of a ruler or scale against which a line is drawn.
