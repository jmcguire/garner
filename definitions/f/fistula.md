# fistula

fistula (= an abnormal opening into a natural canal or hollow organ) has predominantly formed the plural *fistulas* in AmE since about 1940, but the Latinate plural *fistulae* /**fis**-tyә-lee/ has remained usual in BrE since the mid-1800s. See plurals (b).

 - Current ratio in print (*fistulas* vs. *fistulae* in World Englishes): 1.8:1
