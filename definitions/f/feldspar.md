# feldspar

feldspar \(= a crystalline mineral found in igneous rocks\) has been the standard spelling since about 1890. *\*Felspar*, a variant, predominated for most of the 1800s, but about 1875 the etymologically superior spelling—closer to the German loanword *Feldspat*—replaced it.

Current ratio in print \(*feldspar* vs. *\*felspar*\): 15:1
