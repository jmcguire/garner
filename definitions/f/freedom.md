# freedom

freedom.

**A. And *liberty*.** These synonyms have connotative distinctions. *Freedom* is the broader, all-encompassing term that carries strong positive connotations. *Liberty*, slightly less emotive, generally suggests the past removal of restraints on specific freedoms.

**B. *Freedom of* vs. *freedom from*.** Both are correct, the first denoting possession of a right and the second denoting protection from a wrong. Note the shift in forms: *freedom of speech* but *freedom from oppression*, *pestilence*, *coercion*, etc.
