# frambesia

frambesia; **framboesia**. The technical term for the tropical skin disease known also as *yaws* is *frambesia* in AmE, *framboesia* in BrE. The term derives from the French *framboise* (= raspberry), from the appearance of the lesions. Both words are pronounced /fram-**bee**-zhee-ә/ or /fram-**bee**-zhә/.
