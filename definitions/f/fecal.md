# fecal

fecal; **faecal**. Meaning "of, relating to, or consisting of waste from the bowels," the word *fecal* is so spelled in AmE, *faecal* in BrE. Before 1800, *fecal* was the universal spelling, and in AmE it has persisted. It is also a frequent variant in BrE. Since 1900 or so, World Englishes have tended toward *fecal*.
