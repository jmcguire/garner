# funeral

funeral; **funereal**; **funerary**; \***funebrial**. *Funeral*, commonly a noun, serves as its own adjective <funeral expenses>. *Funereal*, adj., which is frequently confused with *funeral*, means "solemn, mournful, somber." *Funerary* = of, used for, or connected with burial. *\*Funebrial* is a needless variant of *funereal*. H.W. Fowler wrote that no one who can help it uses *funerary* or *\*funebrial* \(*FMEU1* at 205\).
