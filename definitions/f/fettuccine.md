# fettuccine

fettuccine (the Italian style of pasta consisting of narrow, flat noodles) has been predominantly so spelled since about 1960. Before that time (from 1912, when the word was introduced into English), the predominant spelling was \**fettuccini*, which is now but a variant form. The word was borrowed as a plural noun but is normally used in singular constructions in English \<the fettuccine is excellent\>. \**Fettucine* is a variant spelling.

 - Current ratio in print (*fettuccine* vs. \**fettuccini*): 4:1
 - Current ratio in print (*fettuccine* vs. \**fettucine*): 6:1
