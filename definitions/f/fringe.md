# fringe

fringe \(= to furnish with a decorative edge\), for the present participle, makes *fringing*—not *\*fringeing*. So the inflected form is analogous to *cringing*, *hinging*, *lunging*, and *tinging*—not to *bingeing* and *whingeing*. \(See the entries for those other words.\)

Current ratio in print \(*fringing* vs. \**fringeing*\): 711:1
