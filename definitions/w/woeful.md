# woeful

woeful (= grievous or deplorable), a Middle English compound dating from the 1300s, has been predominantly so spelled only since about 1900. Before that, the variant \**woful* predominated in AmE and BrE alike.

 - Current ratio in print (*woeful* vs. \**woful*): 5:1
 - Current ratio in print (*woefully* vs. \**wofully*): 17:1
