# Whitsunday

Whitsunday; \***Whit Sunday**; **\*Whitsun Day; Whitsuntide**. The first three denote the seventh Sunday after Easter, on which the Christian festival of Pentecost is celebrated, commemorating the Holy Spirit's descent upon the Apostles. *Whitsunday* is the standard spelling in AmE and BrE alike. \**Whit Sunday* and \**Whitsun Day* are chiefly BrE variants. *Whitsuntide* is either the full week beginning with Whitsunday or the first three days of that week.

 - Current ratio in print \(*Whitsunday* vs. \**Whit Sunday* vs. \**Whitsun Day*\): 57:15:1
