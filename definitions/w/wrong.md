# wrong

wrong; **wrongful**. The distinction is important. *Wrong* = \(1\) incorrect; unsuitable <the quoted figures were simply wrong> <it was wrong of us to expect them so soon>; or \(2\) contrary to law or morality; wicked <cloning just to get human organs is wrong>. *Wrongful* = \(1\) characterized by unfairness or injustice; contrary to law <Iraq's wrongful aggression against Kuwait>; or \(2\) \(of a person\) not entitled to the position occupied <the wrongful officeholder>.
