# wiry

wiry (= \[1\] of or like wire; \[2\] tall and thin; or \[3\] sounding like a vibrating wire) is so spelled. *\*Wirey* is a common misspelling—e.g.: "The *wirey* \[read *wiry*\] Cuban will start the season as a reserve middle infielder." Ryan Corazza, "Wild Thing," *Chicago Trib.*, 31 Mar. 2008, at 14.

 - Current ratio in print (*wiry* vs. \**wirey*): 556:1
