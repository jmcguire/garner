# witness

witness predominantly forms the possessive *witness's* in AmE, *witness*' in BrE. The BrE preference is anomalous, however, because BrE print sources show a decided preference for the singular possessive *Jones's* over *Jones'*. See possessives (a).

 - Current ratio in print (*witness's statement* vs. *witness' statement* in World Englishes): 4:1
