# wallet

wallet; **billfold**. The traditional distinction is that a *wallet* holds paper money unfolded and contains compartments for coins and the like, whereas in AmE a *billfold* (as the name suggests) holds it folded and does not contain extra compartments. But *wallets* now fold, so most people use the words interchangeably. And *billfold* is largely seen as sex-specific for males. Coined in the late 1800s and common only since the 1930s, *billfold* has never been in as widespread use as *wallet* (common since the early 1700s).
