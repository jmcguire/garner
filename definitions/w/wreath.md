# wreath

wreath; **wreathe**. *Wreath* is the noun <a Christmas wreath>, *wreathe* the verb <they plan to wreathe the door in garlands>. Note that the *th* is pronounced differently in the two words, as in *breath*/*breathe*. For a similar difference in *loath*/*loathe*, see **loathe**.

Current ratio in print \(*to wreathe the* vs. *\*to wreath the*\): 1.6:1
