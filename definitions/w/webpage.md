# webpage

webpage. Preferably one word—e.g.: "When you click your mouse on a marked link on a *webpage*, here's what happens." H. Eric Branscomb, *Casting Your Net* 66 \(2000\). Yet *web page* is exceedingly common. Cf. **World Wide Web**.

Current ratio in print \(*web page* vs. *webpage*\): 1.4:1
