# winey

winey \(= having the taste or smell of wine\), an adjective dating from the 1300s, has been predominantly so spelled only since the 1990s. Before that, *winy* \(now a secondary variant except in BrE\) was about equally common.

Current ratio in print \(*winey smell* vs. *winy smell* in AmE\): 1.9:1   
Current ratio in print \(*winey smell* vs. *winy smell* in BrE\): 2.2:1
