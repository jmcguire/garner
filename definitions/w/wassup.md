# wassup

wassup, labeled an interjection by the staid *OED*, is a colloquial Americanism for the question *What's up?* It can mean anything from a friendly "What's happening?" to a concerned "What's the matter?" Since the 1990s, it has been used as a greeting—a youthful casualism. Interestingly, it can be traced back in nonstandard English as far as 1902. The term is especially associated with African-American English. Since about 2000, the shortened version *sup* has spread into widespread use in speech, text messages, etc.: *Sup? Not much. Sup wit you? Just chillin*. See **African-American English**.
