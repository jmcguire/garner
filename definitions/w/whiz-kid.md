# whiz kid

whiz kid \(= a highly skillful or successful young person\), dating from the mid-20th century, is so spelled in AmE and BrE alike. The variant spelling \**whizz kid* was common in BrE from about 1960 to 1990, but it has steadily fallen in frequency of use since then. Today in BrE *whiz kid* predominates by a 3:1 ratio. The one-word variants \(\**whizkid* and \**whizzkid*\) are to be avoided, especially by precocious youngsters.

 - Current ratio in print \(*whiz kid* vs. *\*whizzkid* vs. *\*whizkid* in World Englishes\): 25:1.2:1
