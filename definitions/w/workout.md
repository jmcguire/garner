# workout

workout, n.; **work out**, v.i. & v.t. Although the noun is one word <a good workout>, the verb should be two—e.g.: "The Longhorns will *workout* \[read *work out*\] once today, at 4:35 p.m." "Extra Points," *Austin Am.-Statesman*, 22 Aug. 1995, at C3. See phrasal verbs. For more on *work out* in its intransitive use, see **calculate out**.
