# worsen

worsen, vb., traceable to the 1200s, was once principally a transitive verb \<he worsened his situation\>. But beginning in the late 1700s, its intransitive uses became more common \<his situation worsened\>. Today, in other words, *worsen* is an ergative verb. Interestingly, the word wasn't used much in print until the 1930s—and it has climbed steadily ever since.
