# weevily

weevily \(= infested with the pests known as weevils\), an adjective dating from the mid-1700s, has been predominantly so spelled since about 1913 in AmE and BrE alike. Before that, \**weevilly* was about equally common. Cf. **weaselly**.

Current ratio in print \(*weevily* vs. \**weevilly*\): 1.8:1
