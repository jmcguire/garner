# wanderlust

wanderlust \(= a strong desire to travel to many places\), a German loanword dating in English from the late 1800s, was borrowed directly from German. The word grew popular in English after 1900. Beginning in the 1950s, some writers mistook the first part of the compound and erred by writing \**wonderlust*. See germanicisms.

Current ratio in print \(*wanderlust* vs. \**wonderlust*\): 119:1
