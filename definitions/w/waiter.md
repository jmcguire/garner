# waiter

waiter. If women can be actors and sculptors, then surely they can be *waiters*. Yet in looking for nonsexist alternatives to *waitress*, various groups have championed the silly terms *\*waitperson* and *\*waitron*. Let *waiter* \(or, if need be, *server*\) do for either sex. See sexism \(d\).

 - *waiter* as a gender-neutral term: Stage 5
