# Argentine

Argentine; **Argentinian**. These terms are essentially interchangeable in practice, but *Argentine* predominates as both noun and adjective. The disparity in use is almost great enough to label *Argentinian* a needless variant. See demonyms.

 - Current ratio in print \(*Argentines* vs. *Argentinians*\): 3:1

 - Current ratio in print \(*Argentine writer* vs. *Argentinian writer*\): 2.2:1
