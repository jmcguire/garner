# autogiro

autogiro /ot-oh-**jī**-roh/ (a predecessor of the modern helicopter) has, since its introduction in the 1920s, almost always been predominantly so spelled. But since the mid-1950s, the competing form \**autogyro* has vied for predominance. Oddly enough, a variant term for the machine, *gyrocopter*, is never recorded as *\*girocopter*.

 - Current ratio in print (*autogiro* vs. \**autogyro*): 1.2:1
