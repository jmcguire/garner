# academically

academically. So spelled—not *\*academicly*. E.g.: "The goal of the strategic plan is to keep the university competitive economically and *academicly* \[read *academically*\] through the year 2005, the release states." Frank Mastin Jr., "84 Employees Lose Their Jobs at Tuskegee University," *Montgomery Advertiser*, 2 Oct. 1997, at C2. See -ic/-ical.
