# alterative

alterative; **alterant**. Although neither word is frequent, each may act as both noun and adjective. As adjectives, they both mean "causing alteration." As nouns, however, the meanings diverge. An *alterant* is anything that alters or modifies. *Alterative* appears in medical contexts—though rarely now by physicians—in reference to a medicine that gradually changes unhealthy bodily conditions into healthy ones.
