# arsenious

arsenious; \***arsenous**; **arsonous**. *Arsenious* /ahr-**sen**-ee-әs/ \(= of, relating to, or involving arsenic\) should not be confused as being an adjectival form of *arson* \(*arsonous*\). As for the arsenic-related adjective, the spelling with the *i* is standard; the other form is a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*arsenious* vs. *\*arsenous*\): 4:1
