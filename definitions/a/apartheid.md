# apartheid

apartheid. The traditional and preferred pronunciation of this Afrikaans term is /ә-**pahrt**-hayt/, the last syllable appropriately mimicking the word *hate*. But it is so commonly pronounced /ә-**pahr**-tīt/ or /-tīd/ \(like *tight* or *tide*\) that those variants are widely accepted. Not accepted are pronunciations that meld the *t* and *h* into a digraph. The term dates only from the late 1940s.
