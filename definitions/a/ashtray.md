# ashtray

ashtray (= a small dish for the remains of used cigarettes and cigars), a compound dating from the late 1800s, was commonly written as two words for the first half of the 1900s (\**ash tray*). But after 1958 the solidified *ashtray* became predominant, as it has been ever since.

 - Current ratio in print (*ashtray* vs. \**ash tray*): 16:1
