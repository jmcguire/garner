# auntie

auntie, an informal term of endearment used especially by children, has been predominantly so spelled since the early 1860s. From the early 1600s until 1864, the spelling \**aunty* predominated. In some World Englishes—especially in Africa, Asia, and the Caribbean—*auntie* is used as a term of respect for an older woman. See diminutives (g).

 - Current ratio in print (*auntie* vs. \**aunty*): 1.9:1
