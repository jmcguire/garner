# affair

affair \(= \[1\] a matter that concerns or involves someone; \[2\] a social event or activity; or \[3\] a secret, usu. short-term sexual relationship between two people, esp. when one of them is married\) is predominantly so spelled in all varieties of English. \**Affaire* is a variant only in sense 3.

Current ratio in print \(*had an affair with* vs. *had an \*affaire with*\): 657:1
