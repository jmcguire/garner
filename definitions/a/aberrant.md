# aberrant

aberrant, adj.; **aberrational; aberrative**. These terms appear in order of descending frequency. *Aberrant* /**ab**-әr-әnt/ or /ә-**ber**-int/ = deviating from behavioral or social norms. *Aberrational* /a-bә-**ray**-shә-nәl/ = of, relating to, or involving an aberration. *Aberrative* /ә-**ber**-ә-tiv/ = tending toward aberration. For *aberrant* as a noun, see **aberration**.
