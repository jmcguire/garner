# augmenter

augmenter (= someone or something that increases the value, amount, or effectiveness of something), an agent noun dating from the 1500s, has always been the predominant spelling. \**Augmentor* is a variant that was predominant from 1885 to 1947 and also from 1968 to 1998. See -er (a).

 - Current ratio in print (*augmenter* vs. \**augmentor*): 7:1
