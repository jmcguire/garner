# avocation

avocation; **vocation**. Although these words are quite different, many writers misuse *avocation* for *vocation*. The first means "hobby," whereas the second means "calling or profession." Here is the common mistake, worsened by redundancy: "My one life—my *professional avocation* \[read *vocation*\]—is to help prevent animal suffering." Michael Fox, "Animal Doctor," *St. Louis Post-Dispatch*, 28 Feb. 1996, Everyday Mag. §, at E2.
