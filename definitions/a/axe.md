# axe

axe; **ax**. According to Sir James A.H. Murray, editor of the first edition of the *OED*, the spelling *ax* is "better on every ground of etymology, phonology, and analogy." But *axe* is standard in AmE and BrE. Despite what American desktop dictionaries have long said, the form ending in *e* has predominated in print.

Compound words follow the same course—hence *pickaxe* and *poleaxe*.

 - Current ratio in print (*axe* vs. *ax*): 2.1:1
