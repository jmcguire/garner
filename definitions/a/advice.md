# advice

advice; advise. *Advice* /ad-**vīs**/ \(= counsel that one person gives another\) is a noun. *Advise* /ad-**vīz**/ \(= to counsel; try to help by guiding\) is a verb. The spellings are sometimes confounded—e.g.: "All the programs take pains to inform you with large disclaimers that they are no substitute for real medical professionals \(good *advise* \[read *advice*\]\) and as such cannot be responsible if you use them improperly." Bob Bielk, "Dr. Disc," *Asbury Park Press* \(Neptune, N.J.\), 10 June 1997, at D1. See **advise**.
