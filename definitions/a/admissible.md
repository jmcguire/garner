# admissible

admissible; \***admissable; \*admittable**. *Admissible* \(the standard word\) = \(1\) allowable; or \(2\) worthy of admittance \(i.e., gaining entry\). The other two are needless variants. See -able \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*admissible* vs. \**admissable* vs. \**admittable*\): 2,711:6:1
