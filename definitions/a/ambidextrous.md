# ambidextrous

ambidextrous. While *dexterous* is preferably spelled with two *e*'s, *ambidextrous* has only one. In modern print sources, *ambidextrous* predominates. This inconsistency between *dexterous* and *ambidextrous* is something of a mystery. Cf. **dexterous**.

Current ratio in print \(*ambidextrous* vs. *\*ambidexterous*\): 136:1
