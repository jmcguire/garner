# anemia

anemia; **anaemia**. Denoting a medical condition in which one's blood has too few red blood cells, *anemia* has been the standard spelling in AmE only since about 1900. From the time when the word was first used in English in the 1820s, the BrE spelling has predominantly been *anaemia*. But the two spellings have been locked in close competition in BrE since about 1980.
