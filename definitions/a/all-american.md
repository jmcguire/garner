# All-American

All-American, n.; **All-America**, adj. As the headwords suggest, *All-American* is predominantly a noun <Jones is an All-American>. In the athletic sense, the preferred adjective has long been *All-America* <three Nebraska linemen were selected for the All-America team>, though usage is almost equally divided. In the more general sense, however, *all-American boy* or *all-American girl* is the only idiomatic phrasing.
