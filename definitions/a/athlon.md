# -athlon, Essay

-athlon. This suffix, from the Greek word for "contest," has two syllables. Avoid adding an *a* when spelling it and slipping in an epenthetic schwa when pronouncing it. For example, *triathlon* \(an athletic contest featuring swimming, running, and bicycle races\) is pronounced /trī-**ath**-lon/, not /trī-**ath**-ә-lon/. Likewise with *pentathlon* \(swimming, running, steeplechase, fencing, and shooting\) and *decathlon* \(three races of varying distances plus high hurdles and six field events\). See pronunciation \(b\). Cf. **athlete**.
