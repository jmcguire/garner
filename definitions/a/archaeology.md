# archaeology

archaeology \(= the study of the remains of ancient peoples and their cultures\) is the standard spelling in both AmE and BrE. *\*Archeology* is a variant spelling. \(See ae.) The word is pronounced /ahr-kee-**ol**-ә-jee/, preferably not /ahr-kay-/.

Current ratio in print \(*archaeology* vs. \**archeology*\): 13:1
