# amorous

amorous; **amatory; \*amative**. *Amorous* = (1) strongly moved by love and sex <amorous passions>; or (2) indicative of love <amorous glances>. *Amatory* = of, relating to, or involving sexual love <amatory poetry> <amatory songs>. *\*Amative* is a needless variant, not of *amatory* but of *amorous*.
