# again

again, like *against*, is preferably pronounced in AmE with an /e/ in the second syllable—hence /ә-**gen**/ and /ә-**genst**/. The pronunciations /ә-**gayn**/ and /ә-**gaynst**/ are chiefly BrE variants—though *DARE* records that these pronunciations may be found in AmE in Atlantic States, adding that they "may be considered affected" (1 *DARE* at 19). The pronunciations /ә-**gin**/ and /ә-**ginst**/ are frequent among less educated speakers (*ibid.*).
