# agilely

agilely, adv., is occasionally misspelled *\*agiley*—e.g.: "But it's pointless to bemoan the status quo; what we need to do is work as *agiley* \[read *agilely*\] and cannily as we can with the situation as given to get across the many exciting and provocative and challenging works that continue to be written—and widely read." "Will Publishing Survive?," *L.A. Times*, 25 Feb. 2001, Book Rev. §, at 6. Cf. **futilely** & **solely**. See adverbs (c).

## Language-Change Index

 - *agilely* misspelled *\*agiley*: Stage 1
