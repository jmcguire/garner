# adjacency

adjacency \(= \[1\] the quality, state, or condition of being next to something; or \[2\] something that is next to something else\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the 1600s, has always been the standard noun. **Adjacence* is a variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*adjacency* vs. \**adjacence*\): 459:1
