# adjuster

adjuster; \***adjustor**. *Adjuster* \(= one whose job is to determine the amount of loss suffered when an insurance claim is submitted and to try to settle the claim\) is the preferred spelling—the one that has vastly predominated since the words came into common use in the 1700s. See -er \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*adjuster* vs. \**adjustor*\): 8:1
