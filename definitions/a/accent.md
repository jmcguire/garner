# accent

accent, v.t.; **accentuate**. These synonyms have a good latent distinction. H.W. Fowler noted that *accent* is more common in literal senses, *accentuate* in figurative senses \(*FMEU1* at 7\). Hence one properly *accents* the second syllable of the word *insurance*, but *accentuates* the advantages of buying life insurance from a reputable company. *Accentuate the positive* has been a set phrase since the 1940s.
