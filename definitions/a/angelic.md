# angelic

angelic \(= \[1\] of, relating to, or involving angels; or \[2\] being or appearing to be kind, gentle, and altogether good\), a French/Latin loanword dating in English from the 1400s, has been the standard adjective since the mid-1700s. **Angelical* is a variant. See -ic/-ical.

 - Current ratio in print \(*angelic* vs. \**angelical*\): 46:1
