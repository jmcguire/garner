# Archean

Archean \(= of, relating to, or involving the very earliest period of geological history\), a proper adjective dating from the 1870s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since the 1890s. *Archaean*, long predominant in BrE, has recently given way to *Archean*—in a kind of geological progression.

 - Current ratio in print \(*Archean* vs. *Archaean* in AmE\): 3:1

 - Current ratio in print \(*Archean* vs. *Archaean* in BrE\): 1.03:1
