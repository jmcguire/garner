# angioma

angioma /an-jee-**oh**-mә/ (= a tumor consisting of blood vessels or lymph vessels), a German/Greek loanword dating in English from the mid-1800s, has two plurals: the homegrown *angiomas* (standard since the 1930s) and the Latinate *angiomata* (standard until the 1930s). See plurals (b); see also germanicisms.
