# air freight

air freight. Two words for the noun. But the verb is hyphenated <the seller air-freighted the wine>. The solid *airfreight* is a variant of both noun and verb.

Current ratio in print \(*by air freight* vs. *by airfreight* vs. *by air-freight*\): 21:8:1

Current ratio in print \(*air-freighted* vs. *airfreighted* vs. *air freighted*\): 1.9:1.4:1
