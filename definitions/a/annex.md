# annex

annex, n.; **annexation**; \***annexment**; **\*annexion**. *Annex* = something attached, as an appendix or a wing of a building. (*\*Annexe* is a chiefly BrE variant.) *Annexation* = (1) the act of attaching or incorporating (as territory within a municipality or nation); or (2) the state of having been attached or incorporated. *\*Annexment* and *\*annexion* are needless variants of *annexation*.
