# apnea

apnea \(= a temporary stoppage in breathing, usu. during sleep\), a Greek/Latin loanword dating in English from the early 1700s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since about 1900. In BrE, the two forms *apnea* and *apnoea* have vied closely since about 1975.

 - Current ratio in print \(*apnea* vs. *apnoea* in World Englishes\): 5:1
