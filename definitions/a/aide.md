# aide

aide; aid, n. Both terms are used in reference to a person whose responsibility is to provide help to another <a physician's aide> <a teacher's aide>. The spelling *aide* is preferable—and it's much more common, probably because *aid* also has the sense of "practical help or support, such as money or food."

 - Current ratio in print \(*teacher's aide* vs. *\*teacher's aid*\): 15:1
