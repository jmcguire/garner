# Albuquerque

Albuquerque; **Alburquerque**. The accepted spelling of the city in New Mexico is *Albuquerque*. But the original spelling—used in the title of Rudolfo Anaya's novel by that name (published in 1992)—was *Alburquerque*. The place name is sometimes misspelled *\*Alberquerque* or *\*Albequerque*.

 - Current ratio in print (*Albuquerque* vs. *Alburquerque* vs. *\*Albequerque* vs. *\*Alberquerque*): 1,625:24:1.5:1
