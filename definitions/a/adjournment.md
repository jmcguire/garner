# adjournment

adjournment = (1) the act of suspending proceedings to another time or place; or (2) an adjourned meeting, i.e., a meeting "scheduled for a particular time (and place, if it is not otherwise established) by the assembly's ‘adjourning to' or ‘adjourning until' that time and place." *Robert's Rules of Order Newly Revised* § 9, at 90 (10th ed. 2000). As *Robert's* points out, because sense 2 is susceptible to confusion with sense 1, the phrase *adjourned meeting* is preferable to *adjournment* in sense 2. Reserve *adjournment* for its ordinary meaning: sense 1.
