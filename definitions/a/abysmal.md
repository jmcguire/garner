# abysm\(al\)

abysm\(al\); **abyss\(al\)**. Both nouns signify "a bottomless gulf." *Abyss* is in more widespread use and is therefore to be preferred. Though *abysm* is obsolescent, *abysmal* thrives \(indeed, in some phrases it has become trite\) as a figurative term for "immeasurably bad" <abysmal ignorance> <abysmal weather>. *Abyssal* is a technical oceanographic term <the geology of the abyssal deep>.
