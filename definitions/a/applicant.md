# applicant

applicant; **applicator**; **\*applier**. An *applicant* is "someone who applies for something \(as a position in a firm\)." *Applicator* = \(1\) a device for applying a substance; or \(2\) someone who applies a substance. *\*Applier* is a needless variant of *applicator*.

Current ratio in print \(*applicator* vs. *\*applier*\): 14:1
