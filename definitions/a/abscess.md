# abscess

abscess \(= a small mass of pus collected in a hollow where tissue has decayed\) is sometimes misspelled *\*absess* or *\*abcess*—e.g.: "Though the jokes start out low \(tooth *absesses* \[read *abscesses*\], fake body casts\), the sassin' siblings eventually show their true, warm, brotherly colors." "Fall Previews," *Newsday* \(N.Y.\), 8 Sept. 1996, at 4.

## Language-Change Index
