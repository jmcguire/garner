# anyhow

anyhow \(= in any way; in any manner\) is a folksy casualism for *anyway* or *nevertheless*. E.g.: "I'm not sure they would *anyhow*." John Ed Pearce, "Old Age: Not for the Unwary," *Courier-J.* \(Louisville\), 8 Jan. 1995, at D3.

Language-Change Index

*anyhow* for *anyway*: Stage 4   
Current ratio in print \(*anyway* vs. *anyhow*\): 10:1
