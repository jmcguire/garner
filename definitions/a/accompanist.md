# accompanist

accompanist /ә-**kәm**-pә-nist/ is the standard form, not *\*accompanyist*—e.g.: "Paxton was in wonderful form, and *accompanyist* \[read *accompanist*\] Eric Weissberg added just enough instrumental firepower on guitar and dobro to lend the songs some spark." Greg Haymes, "Tom Paxton Shows He's Still at Top of His Songwriting Game," *Times Union* (Albany), 28 Mar. 1994, at C4.
