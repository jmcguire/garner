# artifice

artifice is sometimes misspelled *\*artiface*—e.g.: "There are subtle lines between art and *artiface* \[read *artifice*\], between performance and reality." Jerry O'Brien, "No More Cocktail Dresses," *Providence J.-Bull.*, 10 Nov. 1995, at C1.
