# aftercare

aftercare \(= the treatment, help, or supervision given to someone who has been released from a hospital, prison, etc.\), a compound dating from the mid-1800s, has been predominantly so written, as a solid, since the late 1960s. Before that, the standard form had been \**after-care*.

 - Current ratio in print \(*aftercare* vs. \**after-care*\): 8:1
