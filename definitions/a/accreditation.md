# accreditation

accreditation \(= official approval given to someone or something by reason of having reached an acceptable standard\) is pronounced /ә-kred-i-**tay**-shәn/—not /ә-kred-i-**day**-shәn/. The mispronunciation has led to the occasional misspelling *\*accredidation*, common especially after 1960.

Current ratio in print \(*accreditation* vs. \**accredidation*\): 20,890:1
