# acclimate

acclimate; **acclimatize**. Although the *-ize* form is preferred by H.W. Fowler and other BrE authorities, and indeed by BrE speakers, the shorter form—which actually predates the longer—is now standard AmE. Some American dictionaries put the primary definition under *acclimatize* /ә-**klī**-mә-tīz/, but few Americans use this term; the main term is *acclimate* /**ak**-lә-mayt/. The corresponding nouns are *acclimation* /ak-lә-**may**-shәn/ in AmE and *acclimatization* /ә-klī-mә-ti-**zay**-shәn/ in BrE. See -ize.
