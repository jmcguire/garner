# antepenultimate

antepenultimate \(= the next to the next-to-last\) is sometimes misspelled \**antipenultimate*—e.g.: "The two versions of the *antipenultimate* \[read *antepenultimate*\] poem on the Houghton manuscript offer an even stronger indication." Ashby Bland Crowder, "Attribution or Misattribution: New Poems by Robert Browning?," 43 *Philological Q.* 443 \(2012\). The corresponding noun *antepenult* is pronounced either /an-tee-**pee**-nәlt/ or /an-tee-pi-**nәlt**/.

 - Current ratio in print \(*antepenultimate* vs. *\*antipenultimate*\): 110:1
