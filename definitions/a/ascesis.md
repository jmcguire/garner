# ascesis

ascesis /ә-**see**-sis/ \(= self-discipline\), a Greek loanword dating in English from the mid-1800s, has been predominantly so spelled since the late 1880s. *Askesis* /ә-**skee**-sis/ is a variant. The plural forms are *asceses* and *askeses*.

Current ratio in print \(*ascesis* vs. *askesis*\): 1.3:1
