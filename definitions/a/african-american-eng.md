# African-American English

African-American English is a variety of English traditionally spoken by many North Americans of African descent. It is variously known as *African-American Vernacular English* \(AAVE\), *African-American Language* \(AAL\), *African-American Vernacular Language* \(AAVL\), *Black English* \(BE\), and *Ebonics*. The definitive treatment of this big subject is *The Oxford Handbook of African American Language* \(Sonja Lanehart ed., 2015\).
