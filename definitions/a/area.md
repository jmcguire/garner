# area

area, an abstract word, is sometimes used almost as a space-filler: *a problem in the area of domestic policy* should be *a problem in domestic policy*. E.g.: "Madeline Andrews, a third-grade teacher from the North School, in Londonderry, . . . has developed activities using computers for each theme and *subject matter area* \[read *subject*\]." "Celebrating All Aspects of Education in NH," *Union Leader* (Manchester, N.H.), 10 June 1996, at A8. Cf. **field**.
