# antenna

antenna. When the reference is to insects, *antennae* /an-**ten**-ee/ is the usual plural. But when the reference is to televisions and electronic transmitters, *antennas* is better. See plurals \(b\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*insect antennae* vs. *insect antennas*\): 15:1

 - Current ratio in print \(*TV antennas* vs. *TV antennae*\): 3:1
