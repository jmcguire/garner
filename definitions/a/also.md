# also

also. This word is a close synonym of *too* (= as well), but its syntactic flexibility is greater \<she was also there\> \<she also was there\> \<she was there, also\>. Avoid treating the word as if it were a conjunction—e.g.: "The dishes were dirty, *also* \[read *and*\] several of them were broken." This nonstandard use of *also* creates a run-on sentence.

For more on *also*, see **too (a)**.
