# acoustic

acoustic (= \[1\] of, relating to, or involving sound and how people hear sounds; or \[2\] of, relating to, or involving a musical instrument, esp. a stringed one, that can be played without electronic amplification), a Latin loanword dating in English from the early 1600s, has always predominated over **acoustical*, a needless variant. See -ic/-ical.

 - Current ratio in print (*acoustic* vs. \**acoustical*): 15:1
