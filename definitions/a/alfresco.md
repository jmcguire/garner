# alfresco

alfresco \(= in the open air; outdoor or outdoors\) can be either adverbial <dined alfresco> or adjectival <alfresco dining>. From the mid-1700s until the 1980s, the two-word spelling *al fresco* predominated in print. Although the term had become predominantly solidified by about 1980 in AmE and by 1995 in BrE, the ratio in print between the two forms remains close.

Current ratio in print \(*alfresco* vs. *al fresco*\): 1.2:1
