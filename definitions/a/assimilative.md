# assimilative

assimilative; **\*assimilatory**. Meaning "of, relating to, or involving assimilation," *assimilative* has been the standard form since introduced in the 1300s. \**Assimilatory*, an innovation from the mid-1800s, is a variant.

Current ratio in print \(*assimilative* vs. \**assimilatory*\): 2.4:1
