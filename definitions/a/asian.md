# Asian

Asian; **Asiatic**. *Asian* is the predominant adjective for people or things from or related to Asia. Until the 1950s, *Asiatic* was 8 to 12 times as common in print as *Asian*. But after WWII, *Asian* rapidly became preferred and now predominates by a 17:1 ratio. Today, *Asiatic* is limited to academic uses such as *Asiatic style* \(florid rhetoric\), *Asiatic mode of production* \(Marxist economic theory\), and *Asiatic lion* \(zoology\). See race-related terms \(g\), \(h\).
