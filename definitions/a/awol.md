# AWOL

AWOL /**ay**-wol/, the acronym for *absent without leave* \(= absent without permission\), dating from World War I \(1914–1918\), has almost always predominantly been so written. Variant forms are \**A.W.O.L.* \(predominant only from 1925 to 1931\), \**awol*, and \**a.w.o.l.* See abbreviations \(a\).
