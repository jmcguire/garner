# abridgment

abridgment; **abridgement**. The first spelling predominates in AmE, the second in BrE. But until the 1930s, *abridgment* was the standard spelling in all varieties of English. Cf. **acknowledgment** & **judgment (a)**. See spelling (d).

 - Current ratio in print (*abridgment* vs. *abridgement* in AmE): 1.4:1

 - Current ratio in print (*abridgement* vs. *abridgment* in BrE): 3:1
