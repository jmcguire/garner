# aesthete

aesthete /**es**-theet/ or, in BrE, /**ees**-theet/ (= someone who prizes or purports to prize beauty in art, design, literature, gastronomy, etc.), a Greek loanword dating in English from the 1860s, has always been the predominant spelling. **Esthete* is a variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*aesthete* vs. \**esthete*): 20:1
