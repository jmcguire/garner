# aborigine

aborigine, as a singular noun, is a back-formation from the plural *aborigines* \(L. *ab origine* "from the beginning"\). Traditionally, the word *aboriginal* was considered the proper singular, but today *aborigine* is standard english as a singular noun. It predominates in print by a 2:1 ratio. \(*Aboriginal* is still current in adjectival uses.\)

The spelling *Aborigine*, with the initial capital, is traditional when referring to the indigenous peoples of Australia. See race-related terms \(l\).
