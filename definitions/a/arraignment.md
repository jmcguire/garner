# arraignment

arraignment; **indictment**. The meanings of these terms vary, depending on the jurisdiction. An *indictment* is almost universally the instrument charging a person with a felony; it also loosely refers to the act of charging someone with a crime. An *arraignment* is the reading of the indictment to the defendant or informing the defendant of the substance of the charge and calling for a plea to the charge. For more on *indictment*, see **indictment**.
