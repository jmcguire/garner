# voluptuous

voluptuous \(= sexy; sensually gratifying\) has occasionally been misspelled *\*voluptious* since the late 1700s. E.g.: "It is a big wine, yet soft, *voluptious* \[read *voluptuous*\], and very fruity with fat, berrylike fruity flavors." Robert M. Parker Jr., "The Harvest of 1983," *Wash. Post*, 2 Nov. 1983, at E1.

Current ratio in print \(*voluptuous* vs. \**voluptious*\): 2,577:1
