# veracity

veracity = \(1\) truthfulness; observance of the truth; or \(2\) truth; accuracy. Sense 1, denoting a quality that people have, is the traditionally correct usage. Sense 2 began as a slipshod extension in the 1700s, and still might be so considered. But it's now common in law <the veracity of the affidavit>.

*Veracity* is not to be confused with *voracity* \(= greediness in eating\). See malapropisms.
