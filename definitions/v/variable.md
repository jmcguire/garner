# variable

variable, adj.; **variant**, adj.; **variational**; \***variative**. *Variable* = subject to variation; characterized by variations. *Variant* = (1) differing from one to the next; having diversity in one or more details <variant shapes>; or (2) not agreeing with or conforming to some standard <variant text>. *Variational* = of, relating to, or marked or characterized by variation. *\*Variative* shares the senses of *variational*; because it's the rarer word, it might be considered a needless variant.

*Variable* is pronounced /**va**-ree-ә-bәl/ (in four syllables)—not, as American weather forecasters frequently mouth it, /**ver**-ә-bәl/.
