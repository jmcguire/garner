# victuals

victuals, pronounced /**vit**-әlz/, is spelled phonetically \(*vittles*\) only in colloquial usage. Related forms are *victualer* \(= someone who provides food and drink for payment\), *victualed*, and *victualing* in AmE; these three forms double the *l* in BrE. Cf. **chitterlings**. See spelling \(c\).
