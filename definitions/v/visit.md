# visit

visit, vb.; **to visit with**. To *visit* someone is to call on that person, especially as an act of friendship or courtesy. To *visit* a place is to go there to see \(as a sightseer might\) or to oversee \(as an official might\). To *visit with* someone, in AmE, is merely to talk with that person for more than a moment, usually in a personal way \(at a watercooler, in the front yard, on a city street, in a living room, etc.\).
