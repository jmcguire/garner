# vita

vita \[L. "life"\] is nearly synonymous with *résumé*. The difference is that *vita* usually refers to an academic's accomplishments and often denotes something longer than the typical one-page résumé. Despite the widespread notion to the contrary, the word *vita* /**vee**-tә/ is not a slangy, informal shortening of *curriculum vitae* \[L. "course of one's life"\], in which *vitae* is in the genitive case. Rather, *vita* is a perfectly good term in itself. The British tend to prefer *CV* /see-**vee**/. See **résumé**.
