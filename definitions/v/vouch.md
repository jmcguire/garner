# vouch

vouch; \***avouch**. The first is the word in current use. *\*Avouch* is obsolete, having been replaced by *vouch for* in the sense "to provide proof, to give a guarantee" <I'll vouch for her honesty>. *Vouch* itself is now almost always intransitive in this way (followed by *for*). As a transitive verb meaning "to call upon, rely on, or cite as authority," *vouch* is archaic.
