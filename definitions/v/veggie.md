# veggie

veggie; \***vegie**. *Veggie* (= a vegetable) is the standard spelling of the colloquialism for *vegetable*. It is a mid-20th-century clipped form that adds the diminutive suffix *-ie* (see diminutives (g)).*\*Vegie* is an alternative spelling found primarily in Australia and New Zealand. The *SOED* notes that *veggie* or *\*vegie* can also, in BrE, denote a vegetarian. See casualisms (c).

 - Current ratio in print (*veggie* vs. \**vegie*): 36:1
