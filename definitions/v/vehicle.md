# vehicle

vehicle. The *h* is not pronounced. Hence: /**vee**-i-kәl/.The word itself is often a prime example of officialese, as when a police officer refers to *exiting the vehicle and engaging in foot pursuit* \(= getting out of the car and running after a suspect\). Some auto manufacturers have made their warranties easier to decipher by taking the simple step of substituting *car*, *truck*, *minivan*, or the like for the hypernym *vehicle*. See abstractitis & plain language. See also **hypernym** \(p. 1215\).
