# vertebra

vertebra \(= a single bone that, together with similar bones, forms the spinal column\) has two plurals: the standard *vertebrae* \(/**vәr**-tә-bree/ or /**vәr**-tә-bray/\) and the homegrown variant *vertebras* /**vәr**-tә-brәz/. \(See plurals \(b\).) The Latinate plural (*vertebrae*\) is so common that some writers mistake it for a singular—e.g.: "There were fears that he could be crippled after the fall, but an operation successfully treated a fractured *vertebrae* \[read *vertebra*\]." Charles Laurence, "Death Fall: British Skydiver Flies Home," *Daily Telegraph*, 5 July 1997, at 3.
