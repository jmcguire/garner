# votary

votary \(= \[1\] a monk, nun, or other person whose life is devoted to religion; \[2\] a zealous religious worshipper; or \[3\] a staunch advocate or devoted admirer\), a Latin loanword dating from the 1500s, has always been the standard term. **Votarist* is a needless variant.

Current ratio in print \(*votary* vs. \**votarist*\): 33:1
