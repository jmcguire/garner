# vicegerent

vicegerent; **viceregent**. Both the spellings and the meanings of these two words are confusingly similar. A *vicegerent* (/vīs-**jer**-әnt/ or /vīs-**jeer**-әnt/) is a person appointed to administer the office of another (usually a sovereign or ruler). The word often refers to the Pope as God's representative on Earth.

A *viceregent* /vīs-**ree**-jәnt/ (the rarer word) is the deputy of a regent.
