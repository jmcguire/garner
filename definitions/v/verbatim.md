# verbatim

verbatim; **literatim**; ***ipsissima verba***. These apparent synonyms carry slight nuances. *Verbatim* = word for word. *Literatim* = letter for letter. Sometimes the phrase *verbatim et literatim* is seen. *Ipsissima verba* \(lit., "the selfsame words"\) = the exact language used by someone quoted \(*W11*\). Verb sap.
