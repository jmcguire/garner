# Nazi

Nazi (= \[1\] an adherent to the tenets of Adolf Hitler's National Socialist Party in Germany (1933–1945); or \[2\] someone who exercises authority with excessive strictness and cruelty) is pronounced /**naht**-see/ or (in old-fashioned films, etc.) /**nat**-see/—but never /**nah**-zee/ or /**naz**-ee/. The plural is *Nazis*, not \**Nazies*.

 - Current ratio in print (*Nazis* vs. *\*Nazies*): 19,708:1
