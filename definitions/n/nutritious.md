# nutritious

nutritious; **nutritive**; **nutritional**. Although these terms are sometimes used synonymously, they have undergone differentiation and should be considered distinct. Foods that are *nutritious* are nourishing: they are full of the natural substances that people need to stay healthy or to grow <nutritious foods such as nuts>. What is *nutritive* traditionally relates in some way to the process of giving or getting the right type of food for good health and growth, especially for life-forms other than humans <nutritive substances>. *Nutritional* is now the usual neutral adjective relating to human nutrition—and has been since about 1980 <children's nutritional requirements> <nutritional deficiencies>.
