# neuroma

neuroma; **neurofibroma**. These synonyms denote a usually benign tumor formed on a nerve-cell sheath. The first has consistently been used more frequently in English-language print sources than the second. Although the standard plurals before 1930 were *\*neuromata* and *\*neurofibromata*, usage shifted in the mid-20th century toward the anglicized plurals *neuromas* and *neurofibromas*, first in AmE and then in BrE.

 - Current ratio in print \(*neuromas* vs. \**neuromata*\): 42:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*neurofibromas* vs. \**neurofibromata*\): 35:1
