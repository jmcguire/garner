# Nazism

Nazism, pronounced /**naht**-siz-әm/, preferably not /**naht**-see-i-zem/, denotes the body of political and economic doctrines adopted by Adolf Hitler's National Socialist Party, which controlled Germany from 1933 to 1945. The word has been predominantly so spelled in English from its first appearances in print in the early 1930s. \**Naziism* is a variant form.

 - Current ratio in print \(*Nazism* vs. \**Naziism*\): 206:1
