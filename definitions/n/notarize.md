# notarize

notarize (= to have \[a document\] attested by a notary), originally an Americanism dating from the 1920s, is now commonplace in AmE and BrE alike—e.g.: "Patrick Henry Talbert, a minister who *notarizes* some Greater Ministries documents, has been sued twice in the past three years by people claiming he bilked them of their investments." Michael Fechter, "IRS Probes Ministry's Gift Program," *Tampa Trib.*, 25 Aug. 1997, at 1.
