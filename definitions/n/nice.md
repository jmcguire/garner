# nice

nice = (1) subtle, precise \<a nice question\> \<a nice distinction\>; or (2) good, attractive, agreeable, pleasant \<they're nice people\> \<it's a nice vacation package\>. Although purists formerly objected to sense 2, it's now universally accepted among reputable critics. Still, the word is so vague in that sense—as a generalized expression of approval—that stylists work to find a more concrete term to express their meaning.
