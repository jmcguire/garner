# Nevada

Nevada is pronounced /nә-**vad**-ә/ inside the state but /nә-**vah**-dә/ throughout much of the rest of the country—perhaps on the analogy of *enchilada* and names such as *Estrada* and *Prada*. Given the sovereign right of citizens to choose how their place names are pronounced, the short *a* in /nә-**vad**-ә/ should be preferred.

One is tempted to say that /nә-**vah**-dә/ is incorrect, since the native people's preference ought to be determinative—but things are not so simple. Nationwide, though, many highly refined people believe that /-**vah**-/ is the better pronunciation. Given this circumstance, one can only conclude that either pronunciation is acceptable. But if you're in the state, you'd be wise to say /-**vad**-/. Cf. **Missouri**.
