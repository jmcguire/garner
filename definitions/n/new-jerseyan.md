# New Jerseyan

New Jerseyan; **New Jerseyite**. The first is standard; the second is a variant used by the U.S. Government Printing Office. Although the *-ite* form predominated until the 1980s, the *-an* form is now significantly more common. See demonyms.

 - Current ratio in print \(*New Jerseyan* vs. *New Jerseyite*\): 3:1
