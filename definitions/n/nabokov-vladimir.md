# Nabokov, Vladimir

Nabokov, Vladimir (1899–1977). How do you pronounce the writer's name? It's /vla-**deem**-әr/ (rhyming with *redeemer*) /**nab**-ә-kawf/ (rhyming with *have a cough*). According to the late Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg, who studied closely with Nabokov as her teacher at Cornell, that's how the man pronounced his own name. See Bryan A. Garner, Interview with Justice Ginsburg, 13 *Scribes J. Legal Writing* 133, 135 (2010).
