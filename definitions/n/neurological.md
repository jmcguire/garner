# neurological

neurological; **\*neurologic**. Although some doctors use the shorter form, it's a needless variant that occurs in print much less often than the longer form—e.g.: "Treatment of RSDS begins with a prompt and serious attempt to find the nerve lesion, using available *neurologic* \[read *neurological*\] examinations." Allen Douma, "Pain Syndrome Can Be Difficult to Beat and to Understand," *Chicago Trib.*, 16 Dec. 2001, Health & Fam. §, at 8.

 - Current ratio in print \(*neurological* vs. \**neurologic*\): 1.9:1
