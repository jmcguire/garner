# nomenclatural

nomenclatural; \***nomenclatorial**. What is the adjective corresponding to the noun *nomenclature*? From the late 1800s, when the terms first arose, the two headwords vied for predominance. But in the 1930s and 1940s, *nomenclatural* \(much closer in pronunciation to the noun\) established itself as standard and has remained so.

 - Current ratio in print \(*nomenclatural* vs. \**nomenclatorial*\): 12:1
