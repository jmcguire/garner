# numismatist

numismatist \(= a coin collector\) is pronounced /n\[y\]oo-**mis**-mә-tist/ or /n\[y\]oo-**miz**-mә-tist/—not /n\[y\]oo-miz-**mat**-ist/. The most common cognates are the adjective *numismatic* /n\[y\]oo-miz-**mat**-ik/ and the noun *numismatics* /n\[y\]oo-miz-**mat**-iks/ \(avoid the erroneous /n\[y\]oo-**miz**-mә-tiks/\). *Numismatics* is plural in form but singular in construction <numismatics is a terrific hobby for those with a historical bent>.
