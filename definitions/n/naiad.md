# naiad

naiad (/**nay**-ad/ or /**nī**-ad/) = a river nymph. This permissibly forms the Greek plural *naiades* /**nay**-ә-deez/—even in English-language contexts—but *naiads* (/**nay**-adz/ or /**nī**-adz/) has always predominated. For the principles governing foreign plurals, see plurals (b).

 - Current ratio in print (*naiads* vs. *naiades*): 41:1
