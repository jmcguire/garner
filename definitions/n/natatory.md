# natatory

natatory \(= \[1\] of, relating to, or involving swimming; or \[2\] adapted for swimming\), a Latin loanword dating in English from the early 1800s, has always been the predominant form of the adjective <natatory appendages>. \**Natatorial*, though listed by several dictionaries as the primary term, is but a needless variant.

Current ratio in print \(*natatory* vs. \**natatorial*\): 5:1
