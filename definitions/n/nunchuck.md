# nunchuck

nunchuck /**n”ôn**-ch”ôk/ or /**noon**-ch”ôk/ or /n”ôn-**chah**-koo/ \(= a martial-arts weapon consisting of two pieces of hardwood sticks joined by a chain or cord, so that while one end is held the other can be twirled at great velocity and force\), a Japanese loanword dating in English from the late 1960s, is predominantly so written. **Nunchaku* and \**nunchuk* are variants.

Current ratio in print \(*nunchuck* vs. \**nunchaku* vs. \**nunchuk*\): 13:10:1   
Current ratio in print \(*nunchucks* vs. \**nunchakus* vs. \**nunchuks*\): 7:2.3:1
