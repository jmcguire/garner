# nonessential

nonessential; **inessential**; **unessential**. Although most American dictionaries list *unessential* as the usual term, it is the least common of these words (and has been since about 1940). *Inessential* is somewhat more common, but *nonessential* is by far the most common \<nonessential computer storage\>. *Nonessential* has predominated in print since about 1940.

 - Current ratio in print (*nonessential* vs. *inessential* vs. *unessential*): 5:2.2:1
