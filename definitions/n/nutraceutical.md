# nutraceutical

nutraceutical \(= a food designed to improve health and lower the risk of disease\), a portmanteau word dating from 1990, is predominantly so spelled. The term combines *nutrition* and *pharmaceutical*. \**Nutriceutical* is a variant. Apparently there was some debate at first about whether the second vowel would come from the first or the second source word.

Current ratio in print \(*nutraceutical* vs. \**nutriceutical*\): 106:1
