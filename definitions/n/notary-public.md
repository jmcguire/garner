# notary public

notary public. Pl. *notaries public*—not *\*notary publics*. E.g.: "County Clerk Mary Jo Brogoto said *notary publics* \[read *notaries public*\] should call the office at 881-1626 before picking up their commissions in Independence." "Metro Digest: Independence," *Kansas City Star*, 28 July 1997, at B2. See plurals (g) & postpositive adjectives.

## Language-Change Index

 - *\*notary publics* for *notaries public*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*notaries public* vs. *\*notary publics*): 5:1
