# novelty

novelty does not mean "an extreme rarity." Rather, it denotes something new (and possibly rare). In the sentence "Mother–son incest is so rare as to be regarded as a *novelty*," the writer could have better written, "Mother–son incest is an extreme *rarity*." *Oedipus Rex* belies any claim that incest might have to novelty. Besides, the connotations of *novelty* are positive, so linking it to something as negative as incest makes this use even stranger (but not more novel).
