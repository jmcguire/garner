# nautilus

nautilus \(the mollusk with a spiral shell\) has traditionally formed the plural *nautili* /**nawt**-i-lī/, but since about 1970 *nautiluses* has been strongly predominant in AmE. In both BrE and World Englishes, the two plurals have been locked in close competition since about 1920.

 - Current ratio in print \(*nautiluses* vs. *nautili* in World Englishes\): 3:1
