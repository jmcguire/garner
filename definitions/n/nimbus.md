# nimbus

nimbus \(= \[1\] a dark cloud that may bring rain; or \[2\] a halo\) has two plurals, *nimbuses* and *nimbi*, which are about equally uncommon. *Nimbuses* might be considered preferable as the anglicized plural—e.g.: "Happy faces and halos \(she calls them *nimbuses*\) are parts of her visual vocabulary." Donald Miller, "Welcome the Mystery," *Pitt. Post-Gaz.*, 31 Mar. 1995, Weekend Mag. §, at 2. See plurals \(b\).
