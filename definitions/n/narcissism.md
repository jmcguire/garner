# narcissism

narcissism \(= excessive interest in oneself; self-love\) is the standard term. *\*Narcism* is a needless variant that appeared sporadically from about 1920 to 1960.

Although *narcissism* bears a clinical meaning for psychologists, in the popular mind it has become a generalized term for selfishness or self-centeredness. The technical sense has been blunted by its recently acquired status as a vogue term. "The label is everywhere," a *Psychology Today* article informs us, "but it's widely misused to describe anyone who offends us." Rebecca Webber, "The Real Narcissists," *Psychology Today*, Oct. 2016, at 53. If that's so, it represents slipshod extension whereby the word is nothing but a generalized term of disparagement.

 - Current ratio in print \(*narcissism* vs. \**narcism*\): 258:1
