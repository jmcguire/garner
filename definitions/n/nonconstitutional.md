# nonconstitutional

nonconstitutional; **unconstitutional**. These terms have distinct meanings. *Nonconstitutional* = of, relating to, or involving some legal basis or principle other than those of the U.S. Constitution \<a nonconstitutional doctrine\>. *Unconstitutional* (the more familiar word) = in violation of, or not in accordance with, principles and procedures found in the U.S. Constitution \<an unconstitutional and therefore void statute\>. See **constitutional**.
