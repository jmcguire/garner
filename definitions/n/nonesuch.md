# nonesuch

nonesuch; **nonsuch**. The first spelling is etymologically superior, the word being a combination of *none* and *such*. *Nonsuch* is a chiefly BrE variant. Regardless of spelling, the first syllable is pronounced /nәn/.

*Nonesuch* functions both as a noun \(= a person or thing without equal\) and as an adjective \(= unparalleled, incomparable\).

Current ratio in print \(*nonesuch* vs. *nonsuch*\): 4:1
