# 'nother

'nother. This aphaeretic shortening of *another*—typically used in the phrase *whole 'nother* \(see tmesis\)—has predominantly taken the apostrophe at the fore since the early 1970s. For the 20 years before that, the term was most commonly written without the apostrophe.

Current ratio in print \(*a whole 'nother* vs. *a whole nother*\): 4:1
