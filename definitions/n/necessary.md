# necessary

necessary; **necessitous**. *Necessary*, the more common word, means "essential." *Necessitous* = placed or living in a condition of necessity or poverty; hard up; needy. One of the most famous statements using the word was Franklin Delano Roosevelt's: "As President Roosevelt said in justifying his bill of rights, ‘*necessitous* men are not free men.' " "Constitutional Amendments," *L.A. Times*, 4 July 1995, at B8.
