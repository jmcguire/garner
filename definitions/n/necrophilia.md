# necrophilia

necrophilia \(= an obsession, esp. of an erotic nature, with corpses\) has been the standard term since just after it first appeared in the 1890s. During the decades before, \**necrophilism* was standard, but today it is merely a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*necrophilia* vs. \**necrophilism*\): 125:1
