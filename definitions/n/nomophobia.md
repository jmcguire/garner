# nomophobia

nomophobia = \(1\) fear of or aversion to law and its rules; or \(2\) fear of being without one's mobile phone \(fr. "no" \+ "mobile \(phone\)" \+ phobia\). Sense 1, of course, is traditional, dating from the early 1800s. Sense 2 dates from the early 2000s—e.g.: "Researchers have been looking into the reasons for our ‘smartphone separation anxiety'—known as *nomophobia*—and found that it has little to do with being unable to make or receive a call." Tom Bawden, " ‘Smartphone Separation Anxiety' Afflicts Rising Numbers," *The I Paper*, 16 Aug. 2017, at 7.
