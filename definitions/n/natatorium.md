# natatorium

natatorium \(= an indoor swimming pool\), an Americanism dating from the early 1800s, originally made the plural *natatoria*. But for more than a century after 1895, *natatoriums* tended to predominate. Since about 2015, the plural *natatoria* has floated once again to the surface. See plurals \(b\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*natatoriums* vs. *natatoria* in World Englishes\): 1:2.2
