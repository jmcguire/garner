# melee

melee \(= \[1\] group hand-to-hand fighting; or \[2\] mass confusion and struggle\), a gallicism borrowed into English in the 1600s, has lost its diacritical marks: originally *mêlée*, it is now usually written just *melee*. In BrE it is pronounced /**mel**-ay/, but in AmE it is usually either /**may**-lay/ or /may-**lay**/.

 - Current ratio in print \(*melee* vs. *mêlée* vs. *melée* vs. \**mélée*\): 166:37:5:1
