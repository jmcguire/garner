# mandrel

mandrel; **\*mandril**; **mandrill**. *Mandrel*, a term dating from the 1500s, denotes a metal spindle or bar used in machining or metalwork <lathe mandrel>. \**Mandril* is a variant. A *mandrill* is a large baboon of West Africa; the male has a red and blue face. So if you're needing the tool, be sure to spell it correctly.

2016 ratio in print \(*lathe mandrel* vs. *lathe \*mandril*\): 30:1
