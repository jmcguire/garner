# medieval

medieval; **\*mediaeval**. *Medieval* \(= of, relating to, or involving the Middle Ages\) has been the predominant spelling in AmE since the 1920s and in BrE since the 1930s. *\*Mediaeval*, the standard until the shift occurred, is now a mere variant. *Medieval* is pronounced /med-**ee**-vәl/ \(three syllables\), /med-i-**ee**-vәl/ \(especially in BrE\), or /meed-i-**ee**-vәl/. The last two \(four-syllable\) pronunciations are arguably pedantic. Avoid /mid-/ in the first syllable.

 - Current ratio in print \(*medieval* vs. \**mediaeval*\): 22:1
