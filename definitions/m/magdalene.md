# Magdalen\(e\)

Magdalen\(e\). The \(more famous\) Oxford college is *Magdalen*; the Cambridge college is *Magdalene*. They're spelled differently but pronounced the same. Although the Magdalen Islands, in Quebec, have an intuitive English pronunciation \(/**mag**-dә-lәn/\), the colleges are both pronounced /**mawd**-lәn/. In fact, that pronunciation gave rise to the English word *maudlin* \(= excessively sentimental\), from Mary Magdalene's traditional depiction as a sobbing penitent. Although her name is spelled with an *e* on the end, the islands and the Oxford college omit it.
