# mike

mike; **mic**. As a shortened form of *microphone*, both *mike* and *mic* are used in AmE and BrE alike. Although most dictionaries in 2020 listed the main headword under *mike*, the spelling *mic*—popular in the vogue term *drop the mic*—surpassed its rival in frequency of use about 2010. That was the year when the Associated Press endorsed *mic* as the standard spelling.

 - Current ratio in print \(*open mic* vs. *open mike*\): 5:1
