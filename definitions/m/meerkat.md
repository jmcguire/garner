# meerkat

meerkat (the African mongoose that lives in underground colonies), an Afrikaans loanword dating in English from about 1800, literally means "sea cat." Although the anglicized spelling \**meercat* arose in the 1800s and bade fair from 1925 to 1965 to become the standard english spelling, it began dwindling in the late 1960s and is now an uncommon variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*meerkat* vs. \**meercat*): 51:1
