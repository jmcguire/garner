# matinee

matinee (= a daytime movie showing or theater performance) is so written today, without the acute accent over the first *e*. (See diacritical marks.) Although the etymology suggests a morning performance, the word has denoted primarily afternoon scheduling since the early 1900s. *Matinee* is predominantly /mat-i-**nay**/ in AmE and /**mat**-i-nay/ in BrE.
