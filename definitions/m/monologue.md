# monologue

monologue \(= a speech or skit delivered by a single performer\) is the standard spelling. *\*Monolog* is a variant. The agent noun is predominantly *monologist* /mә-**nol**-ә-jist/—not *\*monologuist* /**mon**-ә-lawg-ist/—e.g.: "Then in 1986, a friend took him to see a performance by *monologuist* \[read *monologist*\] Spalding Gray, and it turned out to be a revelation." Jan Breslauer, "It All Adds Up Now," *L.A. Times*, 17 Aug. 1997, at 49. See **dialogue**.

Current ratio in print \(*monologue* vs. *\*monolog*\): 82:1
Current ratio in print \(*monologist* vs. *\*monologuist*\): 4:1
