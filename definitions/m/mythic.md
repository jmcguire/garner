# mythic

mythic; **mythical**. Although some dictionaries record these as variant forms, they have undergone differentiation. A *mythic* figure is very great or famous \<she became a mythic figure in film criticism\>. A *mythical* creature exists only in ancient stories or legends \<a mythical animal such as the unicorn\>; *mythical* things are imagined or invented \<all these mythical investment opportunities he keeps talking about\>.
