# medalist

medalist \(= someone who wins a medal in a competition\), a noun dating from the 1600s, has been the predominant spelling in AmE since about 1915. Before that, *medallist*—which has always been the BrE spelling—was standard.

Current ratio in print \(*medalist* vs. *medallist* in AmE\): 13:1   
Current ratio in print \(*medallist* vs. *medalist* in BrE\): 3:1
