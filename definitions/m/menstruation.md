# menstruation

menstruation (= the monthly discharge of blood from the uterus of a fertile but nonpregnant female primate) is pronounced /men-stroo-**ay**-shәn/—not /men-**stray**-shәn/ (a mispronunciation resulting from syncope) or /men-i-**stray**-shәn/ (a mispronunciation caused by epenthesis plus syncope).
