# mujahideen

mujahideen; **\*mujahedeen**; **\*mujahedin**; **\*mujaheddin**; **\*mujahadeen**; **\*mujahadin**. The term denoting, collectively, Islamic guerrilla fighters, especially those found in the Middle East, is predominantly translated *mujahideen*. The others are variant spellings.

 - Current ratio in print \(in order of headwords\): 204:54:27:8:6:1
