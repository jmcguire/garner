# milieu

milieu \(/meel-**yʊ**/ or /mil-**yʊ**/ in AmE but /**meel**-yә/ or /**mil**-yә/ in BrE\) is sometimes misspelled *\*mileau*—e.g.: "Morton was a pianist, composer and band leader who emerged from the New Orleans jazz *mileau* \[read *milieu*\] to become one of the most famous musicians in America during the 1920s." Iris Fanger, "Actor's Right at Home in ‘Jelly' Role," *Boston Herald*, 23 Feb. 1996, at S12. The plural *milieus* is preferable to *milieux*. See plurals \(b\).
