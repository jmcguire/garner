# meringue

meringue; **merengue**. A *meringue* /mә-**rang**/ is a light, sweet food, usually a dessert topping, made by mixing sugar and egg whites and then baking. (See spelling (a).) A *merengue* /mә-**reng**-gay/ is either (1) a fast ballroom dance in 2/4 time in which the dancers drag one foot on every step, or (2) the music that accompanies this dance. The dance and its music are of 19th-century Dominican origin.
