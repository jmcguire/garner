# monarchical

monarchical; **\*monarchic**; **\*monarchial**; **monarchal**. *Monarchical* \(= of, relating to, or characteristic of a monarchy\) is the standard form. It predominates in modern usage by a 5:1 ratio over *\*monarchic*, and by a 19:1 ratio over *\*monarchial*. Both of those terms are needless variants. But *monarchal* \(= of, relating to, or characteristic of a monarch\), the least frequent of these words, is different because it relates to the person and not to the institution.
