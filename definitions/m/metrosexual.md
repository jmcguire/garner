# metrosexual

metrosexual, n. & adj., is a portmanteau word made by combining *metropolitan* with *heterosexual*. The term refers to a probably heterosexual man who takes pains to present an upscale dapper appearance through fastidious clothing selections and impeccable grooming as evidenced, for example, by eyebrow-plucking, waxing of the trunk, and highlighting of the hair. A metrosexual is thought to have many of the habits and sensibilities of a sophisticated urban gay man. Coined in 1991, the term rose steadily in AmE and BrE alike—especially since 2000.
