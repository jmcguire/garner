# mynah

mynah \(= a large dark Asian bird that can mimic human speech\) has long been the standard spelling. *Myna*, now but a variant, predominated from the 1700s until the mid-20th century. The term commonly appears in the phrase *mynah bird*, which doubtless disambiguates the term *mynah* in speech.

 - Current ratio in print \(*mynahs* vs. *mynas* vs. *mynah birds* vs. *myna birds*\): 4:3:2.5:1
