# moratorium

moratorium. Both *moratoriums* and *moratoria* are fairly common plurals. On the reasons for preferring the native plural *moratoriums*, see plurals (b). But the fact is that *moratoria* has always appeared more frequently in post-1900 print sources. Before that, the words weren't used much.

 - Current ratio in print (*moratoria* vs. *moratoriums*): 1.5:1
