# meteoroid

meteoroid; **meteor**; **meteorite**. The headwords are listed in astronomical order. That is, a *meteoroid* \(a piece of rock or metal traveling through space\) becomes a *meteor* if it enters the earth's atmosphere and a *meteorite* when it hits the earth's surface.

The adjective *meteoric* means not only "of, relating to, or involving meteors" but also "resembling a meteor in speed or sudden brilliance" <a meteoric rise to stardom>.
