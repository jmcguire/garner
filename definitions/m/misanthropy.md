# misanthropy

misanthropy; **misandry**; **misogyny**. *Misanthropy* /mis-**an**-thrә-pee/ is a hatred or distrust of humankind—all people. Its etymological antonym is *philanthropy*, which literally denotes a love for humankind. *Misandry* /**mis**-әn-dree/ is a hatred or distrust of men in particular, its counterpart being *misogyny* /mi-**sahj**-ә-nee/ (a hatred or distrust of women). Of all these terms, *misandry* is the rarest. Its corresponding agent noun is *misandrist* /**mis**-әn-drist/, which also serves as the adjective. \**Misandrous* and \**misandric* are needless variants.
