# matte

matte, adj.; **matt**, adj. *Matte* \(= lacking shine, luster, or glossiness; dull\), a French loanword dating in English from the 1600s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE only since about 1950 <matte finish>. Before that, *matt* or even *mat* was more usual. In BrE, *matt* was the predominant spelling until 2016 <matt finish>. *Mat* is a variant adjective in all varieties of English.
