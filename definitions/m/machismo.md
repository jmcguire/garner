# machismo

machismo /mah-**cheez**-moh/ \(= the quality of being macho; exaggerated masculine pride\) is sometimes mispronounced /mah-**keez**-moh/. It's acceptable to say the first syllable with a schwa sound /mә-/. The anglicized /-chiz-/ for the second syllable, typical of BrE, is gaining ground in AmE.
