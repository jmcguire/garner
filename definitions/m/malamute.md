# malamute

malamute /**mal**-ә-myoot/ \(= a sled dog of northern North America, esp. Alaska\), a loanword dating in English from the late 1800s, derives from an Inuit language indigenous to Alaska. The variant spelling **malemute* predominated in print sources from about 1938 to 1965, but it was dropped perhaps from a proclivity to cause mispronunciations. Before and after that period, *malamute* has been strongly predominant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*malamute* vs. \**malemute*\): 14:1
