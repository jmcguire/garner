# \*most especially

\*most especially is redundant for *especially*—e.g.: "Stendhal's imaginative relation to his protagonists, *most especially* \[read *especially*\] to Julien, . . . is inseparable from a consideration of the kind of narrator or dramatized persona he invents to serve as his intermediary." Robert Alter, *A Lion for Love* 191 \(1979\) \(biography of Stendhal\).
