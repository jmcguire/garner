# midwifery

midwifery \(= the skill or work of a specially trained nurse who helps in childbirth\) is predominantly pronounced /mid-**wif**-ә-ree/ or /mid-**wif**-ree/ in BrE, but /mid-**wīf**-ә-ree/ or /mid-**wīf**-ree/ in AmE. Yet as Elster notes, the most traditional 18th- and 19th-century pronunciations had an initial syllable stress: /**mid**-wīf-ree/ or /**mid**-wīf-ә-ree/. *BBBM* at 314.
