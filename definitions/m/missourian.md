# Missourian

Missourian; \***Missouran**. The newspaper in Columbia, Missouri, is called *The Missourian*. And interestingly, even though most people in Columbia pronounce the name of their state /mi-**zʊr**-ә/, they pronounce the name of their newspaper /mi-**zʊr**-ee-әn/. In fact, Missourian informants say that the four-syllable pronunciation is universal within the state. See demonyms.

 - Current ratio in print \(*Missourian* vs. \**Missouran*\): 523:1
