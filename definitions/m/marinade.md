# marinade

marinade /ma-ri-**nayd**/, n. & vb.; **marinate** /**ma**-ri-nayt/, vb. Although *marinade* has been recorded as a verb as well as a noun, it's predominantly a noun <he soaked the steak in a pineapple-sauce marinade>. When a verb is needed, *marinate* is better: it's older and much more common <he marinated the steak overnight>.

 - Current ratio in print (*marinated* vs. \**marinaded*): 105:1
