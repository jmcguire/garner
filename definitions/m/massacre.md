# massacre

massacre, v.t., makes *massacring* \(not *\*massacreing*\)—e.g.: "If I were *massacring* English, he would grimace at my linguistic butchery, wouldn't he?" "Interview with Czech Defector Ivo Moravec," *Ottawa Citizen*, 16 Apr. 1997, at A17.

 - Current ratio in print \(*massacring* vs. \**massacreing*\): 151:1
