# metal

metal for *mettle* \(= determination and courage\) is a blunder—e.g.: "We have yet to test the *metal* \[read *mettle*\] of our response capabilities." "Coordinators Rank Top Challenging Issues," *Emergency Mgmt. Update* \(Va. Dep't of Emergency Servs.\), Jan. 1995, at 1.

Language-Change Index

*metal* misused for *mettle*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*test their mettle* vs. *\*test their metal*\): 23:1
