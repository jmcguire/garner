# microbrewery

microbrewery; **craft brewery**. *Microbrewery* is the predominant term for a small, usually local beermaker, appearing in print about three times as often as the more upscale term *craft brewery*. Oddly, though, in 2015 the Associated Press preferred *craft brewery* in its influential *Stylebook*, explaining that it's more descriptive. Whether this stylistic endorsement will have much impact on actual usage remains to be seen.
