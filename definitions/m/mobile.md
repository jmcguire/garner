# mobile

mobile, adj. & n.; **Mobile**. As an adjective, *mobile* means "movable" and is pronounced /**moh**-bәl/ in AmE—BrE and Canadian speakers preferring /**moh**-bīl/. As a noun referring to a sculpture or decoration consisting of small objects tied to wires or strings and hung up so that the objects can be set into motion by air currents, *mobile* is pronounced /**moh**-beel/ in AmE but /**moh**-bīl/ in BrE (as with the adjective). The proper noun denoting the city in Alabama (*Mobile*) is pronounced /moh-**beel**/ (as locals say it)—not /**moh**-beel/.
