# mizzenmast

mizzenmast \(= the mast just behind or second from behind the mainmast in a ship\), a compound dating from the 1400s, has been predominantly so spelled only since about 1860 in AmE and 1920 in BrE. The earlier spelling was \**mizenmast*, now a variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*mizzenmast* vs. \**mizenmast* in World Englishes\): 14:1
