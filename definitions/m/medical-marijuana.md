# medical marijuana

medical marijuana; \***medicinal marijuana**. The former is the more common phrase for the *Cannabis sativa* plant when used to treat the symptoms of some medical conditions \(such as AIDS\) or treatment \(such as chemotherapy\). It appears much more often than *\*medicinal marijuana* in both World English print sources and legal sources. Since *medical marijuana* is the plainer, simpler, and shorter phrase, *\*medicinal marijuana* should be considered a needless variant. See **marijuana**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*medical marijuana* vs. *\*medicinal marijuana*\): 22:1
