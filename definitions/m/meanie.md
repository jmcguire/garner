# meanie

meanie (= an unkind and spiteful person), a casualism dating from about 1910, has always appeared more frequently in the plural <Blue Meanies> <the meanies at that school> than in the singular. Hence writers have sometimes been unsure about how to spell the singular: *meanie* or \**meany*? Since the late 1920s, *meanie* has become standard.

 - Current ratio in print (*a meanie* vs. \**a meany*): 8:1
