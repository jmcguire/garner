# Muslim

Muslim; **Moslem**. Both terms mean "a follower of Islam" and appear in English-language works from the 1400s on. *Moslem* was the standard spelling until the 1940s, when *Muslim* became predominant across World Englishes. Curiously, BrE and AmE lagged behind the shift. *Muslim* became predominant in BrE about ten years later. *Muslim* overtook *Moslem* in AmE in 1964, possibly influenced by the prominence of the *Black Muslims*, an alternative name for the Nation of Islam, during the civil-rights movement. Worldwide, *Muslim* is now 32 times as common as *Moslem*.
