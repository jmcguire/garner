# muffuletta

muffuletta (= a sandwich made with cold cuts, cheese, and olive salad in an Italian-style bun), an Italian loanword dating in English from the 1960s, is so spelled. **Muffaletta* is a variant. The term first appeared in New Orleans and retains strong associations with that city.

 - Current ratio in print (*muffuletta* vs. \**muffaletta*): 4:1
