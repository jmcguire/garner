# metronomic

metronomic \(= of, relating to, or involving the rigorous regularity in beat of a metronome\), an adjective dating in English from the mid-1800s \(perhaps borrowed from the French *métronomique*\), has always been predominantly so written. \**Metronomical* is a variant. See -ic/-ical.

 - Current ratio in print \(*metronomic* vs. \**metronomical*\): 75:1
