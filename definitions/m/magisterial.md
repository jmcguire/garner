# magisterial

magisterial; **magistral**; \***magistratic**; **\*magistratical**. Although *magisterial* carries connotations of nobility, command, and even dictatorialness, it is also the preferred adjective corresponding to the noun *magistrate*. In this latter sense, *\*magistratic* and *\*magistratical* are needless variants. *Magistral* = \(1\) of a master or masters <an absolutely magistral work>; or \(2\) formulated by a physician <a magistral ointment>.
