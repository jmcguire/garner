# manner in which

manner in which is often unnecessarily verbose for *how* or *way*—e.g.: "Those larger issues—the foreign money contributions, the conduit money, the virtual merging of Clinton campaign and Democratic Party coffers and the generally reckless *manner in which* \[read *way*\] the Democrats raised funds for the 1996 presidential campaign—continue to demand vigorous investigation and prosecution." "An Independent Counsel?," *Wash. Post*, 26 Nov. 1997, at A18.
