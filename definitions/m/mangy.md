# mangy

mangy \(= \[1\] suffering from mange—that is, the parasitic skin disease that makes animals lose their fur; or \[2\] filthy, shabby, and squalid\), an adjective dating from the 1500s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Mangey* is a variant.

Current ratio in print \(*mangy* vs. \**mangey*\): 131:1
