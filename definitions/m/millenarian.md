# millenarian

millenarian \(= someone who believes in a thousand-year age of blessedness beginning with the second coming of Christ\) is so spelled despite the *nn* in *millennium*. The difference in spelling relates to etymology: like the adjective *millenary*, the term *millenarian* derives from the Latin term *milleni* "a thousand each." An arcane synonym for *millenarian* is *chiliast* /**kil**-ee-ast/, from the Greek term *chilioi* "a thousand." Hence a thousand-year period is known not only as a *millennium* \(fr. L. *mille* "a thousand"\) but also as a *chiliad* /**kil**-ee-ad/. These are words for only the heartiest vocabularians.
