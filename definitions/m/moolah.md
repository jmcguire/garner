# moolah

moolah \(= money\), a mid-20th-century Americanism of unknown origin, has been predominantly so spelled since the 1990s, when the now-variant spelling *moola* was overtaken. Before that time, *moola* had predominated for many decades. The spelling *moolah* has the advantage of more instantly conveying the usual pronunciation /**moo**-lah/, although a schwa sound in the final syllable is also common.

 - Current ratio in print \(*enough moolah* vs. *enough moola*\): 2.1:1
