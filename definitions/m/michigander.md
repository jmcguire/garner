# Michigander

Michigander; **Michiganian**; **\*Michiganite**. By popular consensus, *Michigander* is the predominant form, appearing more than seven times as often in print as *Michiganian* \(the form once decreed by state statute\). *\*Michiganite* is a rare variant used by the U.S. Government Printing Office. See demonyms.

Current ratio in print \(*Michigander* vs. *Michiganian* vs. \**Michiganite*\): 21:3:1
