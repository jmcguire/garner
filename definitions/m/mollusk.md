# mollusk

mollusk \(= a type of sea or land animal having a soft body covered by a hard shell\), a term dating from the 1780s, has been the predominant spelling in AmE since about 1860. *Mollusc* has been the predominant BrE spelling since about the same time. Oddly enough, the corresponding adjective is overwhelmingly *molluscan* in all varieties of English—not \**molluskan*.

Current ratio in print \(*mollusk* vs. *mollusc* in AmE\): 2.1:1
Current ratio in print \(*mollusc* vs. *mollusk* in BrE\): 3:1
Current ratio in print \(*molluscan* vs. \**molluskan* in World Englishes\): 22:1
Current ratio in print \(*molluscan* vs. \**molluskan* in AmE\): 23:1
