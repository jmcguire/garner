# milquetoast

milquetoast \(= a weak, timid person who is easily led\) is the standard spelling. Even though the name derives from Harold T. Webster's early-20th-century comic strip *The Timid Soul*, in which the character is named Caspar Milquetoast, the word has become a common noun without a capital *m*-. *\*Milktoast* is a variant spelling, not recommended.

 - Current ratio in print \(*milquetoast* vs. \**milktoast*\): 52:1
