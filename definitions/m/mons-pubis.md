# mons pubis

mons pubis; **mons veneris**. The first denotes the fleshy elevation at the lower part of the human abdomen covered with hair—and it relates to either male or female. The second, commonly translated as "mound of Venus," relates exclusively to the female anatomy. The plurals, rarely used, are *montes pubis* and *montes veneris*. See plurals \(b\).
