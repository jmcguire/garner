# meow

meow; \***miaow**; **\*miaou**; **\*miaul**; **\*meou**. According to a solid majority of dictionaries, the standard word for a cat's sound is *meow*. Some cats, presumably ill trained, use variant pronunciations. \(See **mewl**.) Cats with good lungs *caterwaul*.

 - Current ratio in print \(*meow* vs. *\*miaow* vs. *\*miaou* vs. *\*miaul* vs. *\*meou*\): 1,707:157:10:4:1
