# malevolent

malevolent; **maleficent**. Whereas the former means "desirous of evil to others," the latter means positively "hurtful or criminal to others." Hence *malevolent* has to do with malicious desires and *maleficent* with malicious actions. Cf. **beneficent**.

*Maleficent* is pronounced /mә-**lef**-i-sәnt/—not /mә-**lif**-ә-sәnt/. Cf. **beneficent**.
