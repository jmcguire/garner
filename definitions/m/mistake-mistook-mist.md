# mistake \> mistook \> mistaken

mistake \> mistook \> mistaken. So inflected. Mercifully, the correct forms are seldom mistaken, though they sometimes are—e.g.: "Finally, Fly almost *mistaked* \[read *mistook*\] El Sid Fernandez for a bat when he showed up in Sarasota at 225, down 40 from fightin' weight." "Caught on the Fly," *Phoenix Gaz.*, 15 Apr. 1995, at D2. See irregular verbs.

## Language-Change Index

 - *\*mistaked* for *mistook*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*mistook* vs. *\*mistaked*): 1,828:1
