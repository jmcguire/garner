# mesquite

mesquite \(the American tree or shrub whose wood is often used in grilling food\), a Spanish loanword dating in English from the 1700s, has been predominantly so spelled since 1872. Before that, \**mesquit* was a frequent variant and sometimes even predominant. Today \**mesquit* seldom appears.

 - Current ratio in print \(*mesquite* vs. \**mesquit*\): 137:1
