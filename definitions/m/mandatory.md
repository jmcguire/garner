# mandatory

mandatory; **mandatary**. *Mandatory* is the usual word, meaning "obligatory, compulsory." *Mandatary* is much rarer; it's either a noun meaning "a person or entity holding a mandate; an agent" or an adjective meaning "of, relating to, or involving an agency relationship." For purposes of differentiation, *mandatory* should not be used as a noun where *mandatary* can work in its place.
