# macerate

macerate \(= \[1\] to cause to waste or wither away from lack of nourishment; or \[2\] to steep or soak \[something\] in fluid so as to soften and separate into component parts\) is pronounced /**mas**-ә-rayt/—not /**may**-sә-rayt/.

The corresponding agent noun is *macerator*, dating from the mid-1800s. \**Macerater* is a variant. See -er \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*macerator* vs. \**macerater*\): 15:1
