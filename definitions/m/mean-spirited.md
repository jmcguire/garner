# mean-spirited

mean-spirited; **meanspirited**. The hyphenated form is standard. Some make it a solid word—e.g.: "Ms. Phillips is accustomed to being called *meanspirited*." Anita Gates, "Return to L.A.," *N.Y. Times*, 18 Feb. 1996, § 7, at 21. Yet the hyphenated form remains more than seven times as common in print as the solid form. See **mean**, adj.
