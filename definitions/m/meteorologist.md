# meteorologist

meteorologist \(= a professional student of weather\), a nonsexist equivalent to the old-fashioned term *weatherman*, is pronounced /mee-tee-ә-**rol**-ә-jist/, in six syllables—not /mee-tә-**rol**-ә-jist/. Meteorologists often mispronounce the word themselves—as daily newscasts throughout the U.S. will bear out. The term has occasionally been misspelled *\*meterologist* since the beginning of the 20th century—and the misspellers are almost certainly mispronouncers as well.
