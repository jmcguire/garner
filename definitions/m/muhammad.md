# Muhammad

Muhammad; Mohammed; Mahomet; Muhammed. How do you spell the name of the founder of Islam \(who lived from 570 to 632\)? The spelling *Mahomet* predominated from 1600 till about 1880, when *Mohammed* became predominant throughout the English-speaking world. The retransliterated *Muhammad* overtook all rivals about 1945 and is now standard.

Current ratio in print \(*Muhammad* vs. *Mohammed* vs. *Mahomet* vs. *Muhammed*\): 22:10:1.7:1
