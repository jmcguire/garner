# madrasa

madrasa (= an Islamic school or college, often associated with a mosque)—a loanword dating in English from the 1600s and borrowed probably from Arabic, Persian, or Urdu, or possibly even Turkish—has been predominantly so spelled since 1975. Among the variant spellings are \**madrasah*, \**madrassa*, and \**madrassah*.

 - Current ratio in print (*madrasa* vs. \**madrasah* vs. \**madrassa* vs. \**madrassah*): 17:6:5:1
