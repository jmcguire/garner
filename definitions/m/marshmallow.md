# marshmallow

marshmallow is so spelled—not *\*marshmellow* \(under the influence of the adjective *mellow*\). But the misspelling is fairly common—e.g.: "Everything hinged on the *marshmellow-throwing* \[read *marshmallow-throwing*\] left arm of Brian Anderson, the Diamondbacks starter." Lisa Olson, "D-Backs' Shot Rests with Curt," *Daily News* \(N.Y.\), 31 Oct. 2001, at 70.

Language-Change Index

*marshmallow* misspelled *\*marshmellow*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*marshmallow* vs. \**marshmellow*\): 687:1
