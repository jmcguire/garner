# mazurka

mazurka \(= \[1\] a fast traditional Polish dance; or \[2\] the music that accompanies this dance\), a Polish loanword dating in English from the early 1800s, has been predominantly so spelled since 1840. Although the variant spelling \**mazourka* vied closely with *mazurka* in the early days, it is now an infrequent variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*mazurka* vs. \**mazourka*\): 81:1
