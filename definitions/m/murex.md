# murex

murex (= a flesh-eating snail abounding in tropical seas) traditionally formed the plural *murices*. But since the late 1940s in AmE and the early 1970s in BrE, *murexes* has become predominant in English-language print sources. But note the ratio in World Englishes. All change within this term is assuredly slow-moving.

 - Current ratio in print (*murexes* vs. *murices* in AmE): 1.2:1
 - Current ratio in print (*murexes* vs. *murices* in BrE): 3:1
 - Current ratio in print (*murices* vs. *murexes* in World Englishes): 1.5:1
