# measles

measles is sometimes misspelled *\*measels*—e.g.: "Proximity to children also exposes teachers to the gamut of childhood illnesses, including chicken pox and *measels* \[read *measles*\]. And then there is head lice." Gary White, "A Virus for the Teacher," *Ledger* \(Lakeland, Fla.\), 16 Oct. 2002, at D1.

## Language-Change Index

*measles* misspelled *\*measels*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*measles* vs. \**measels*\): 1,133:1
