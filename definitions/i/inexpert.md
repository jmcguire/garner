# inexpert

inexpert, adj.; **nonexpert**, adj. An important distinction exists. *Inexpert* = unskilled \<the novice's inexpert shooting caused a real danger to observers\>. *Nonexpert* = not of or by an expert, but not necessarily unskilled \<even nonexpert drivers can handle this course\>. Whereas *inexpert* has been in common use since the 1700s, *nonexpert* did not appear with any frequency till about 1900.
