# irreligious

irreligious; **unreligious**; **nonreligious**. All three words essentially mean "not religious." But the *ir-* prefix usually marks a dichotomy (e.g., *rational* vs. *irrational*), so *irreligious* often suggests conscious indifference or even hostility toward religion. It is a judgmental term. *Unreligious* is less negative, but *nonreligious* is the most common and the most reliably neutral. Cf. **sacrilegious**.
