# incomparable

incomparable; **noncomparable**; \***uncomparable**. *Incomparable* \(= so good as to be beyond comparison\) is the usual word; it has been in common use since the 1400s. *Noncomparable* \(= not similar enough to something else to allow comparison\) emerged in the early 1900s to rival its synonym, *\*uncomparable*. By 1940, *noncomparable* had been established as standard. *\*Uncomparable* remains a needless variant.

The words are pronounced /in-**kom**-pәr-ә-bel/ and /non-**kom**-pәr-ә-bel/. Don't stress the third syllable.
