# iterate

iterate. Although *iterate* (= to say or do again) is not an equivalent of *underscore* or *ensure*, some writers apparently think so—e.g.: "The always-busy \[Quincy\] Jones is currently completing an ambitious project that *iterates* \[read *underscores*\] his place in American music annals, an album of new songs and updated classics called *Q's Jook Joint*." John H. Ostdick, "The Joint Is Jumping," *Am. Way*, 15 Oct. 1995, at 16. See **reiterate**.

## Language-Change Index

 - *iterate* misused for *underscore* or *ensure*: Stage 1
