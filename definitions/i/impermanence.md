# impermanence

impermanence \(= the quality or state of not enduring forever\), a noun dating from the 1700s, has been predominantly so spelled since the mid-1800s in all varieties of English. The older form \**impermanency*, dating from the 1600s, is now but a needless variant. Cf. **permanence**.

Current ratio in print \(*impermanence* vs. \**impermanency*\): 50:1
