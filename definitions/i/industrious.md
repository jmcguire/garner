# industrious

industrious; **industrial**. *Industrious* corresponds to *industry* in the general sense of a person's work ethic. *Industrial* corresponds to *industry* in the narrow sense of manufacturing goods. *Industrious* typically refers to people <an industrious worker>; *industrial* typically refers to manufacturing activities or productive enterprises <an industrial town>. *Industrial* is pronounced in four distinct syllables: /in-**dәs**-tree-әl/—not /in-**dәs**-trәl/.
