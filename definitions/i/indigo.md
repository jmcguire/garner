# indigo

indigo /**in**-di-goh/ (= a dark purple-blue color), an Italian loanword dating in English from the 1500s, has predominantly formed the plural *indigos* since the early 1900s. \**Indigoes*, quite common and sometimes predominant before that, is a variant. See plurals (d).

 - Current ratio in print (*indigos* vs. \**indigoes*): 4:1
