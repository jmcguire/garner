# Iraq

Iraq. In educated speech, the country's name is pronounced either /i-**rahk**/ (preferred) or /i-**rak**/ (more anglicized). Avoid /ī-**rak**/ and /**ī**-rak/, which smack of ignorance and perhaps enmity.

The corresponding adjective and demonym is *Iraqi*, pronounced /i-**rahk**-ee/ (preferred) or /i-**rak**-ee/. Avoid /ī-**rak**-ee/ and /**ī**-rak-ee/. See names (c).
