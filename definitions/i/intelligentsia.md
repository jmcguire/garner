# intelligentsia

intelligentsia is the standard spelling. *\*Intelligentzia* is a variant.

In the early 20th century, some purists pronounced the Russian loanword with a hard /g/, as in *get*. But the pronunciation quickly conformed to a soft *g* sound \(/j/\) on the analogy of the cognates *intelligent* and *intelligence*. Only /in-tel-i-**jent**-see-ә/ is now considered the standard pronunciation.

Current ratio in print \(*intelligentsia* vs. *\*intelligentzia*\): 501:1
