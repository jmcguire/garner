# inhibitor

inhibitor \(= someone or something that checks, represses, or restrains\), an agent noun dating from the early 1600s but not in widespread use until the 1900s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Inhibiter* is an infrequent variant. See -er \(a\). Cf. **exhibitor**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*inhibitor* vs. \**inhibiter*\): 1,649:1
