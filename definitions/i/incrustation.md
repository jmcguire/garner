# incrustation

incrustation; \***encrustation**. Although *encrust* (not *\*incrust*) is the verb, the spelling *incrustation* predominated for most of the period since the mid-1700s. Since the 1970s, however, the two forms have been about equally common. The most prestigious dictionaries correctly put the main headword under *in-*. For more, see en-.

 - Current ratio in print (*incrustation* vs. *\*encrustation*): 1:1
