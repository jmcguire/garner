# inartistic

inartistic; **unartistic**. *Inartistic*, which preferably refers to people, means "lacking in artistic taste; not appreciative of art" <a workaday, inartistic writer of pulp fiction>. *Unartistic*, which refers to things, means "not relating or conforming to art" <an unartistic photograph>.

 - Current ratio in print \(*inartistic people* vs. *\*unartistic people*\): 15:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*unartistic eye* vs. *inartistic eye*\): 7:1
