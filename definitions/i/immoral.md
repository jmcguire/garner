# immoral

immoral; **unmoral**; **amoral**. These three words have distinct meanings. *Immoral*, the opposite of *moral*, means "evil, depraved." The word is highly judgmental. *Unmoral* \(also *nonmoral*\) means merely "without moral sense, not moral," and is used, for example, of animals and inanimate objects. *Amoral*, perhaps the most commonly misused of these terms, means "not moral, outside the sphere of morality; being neither moral nor immoral." It is loosely applied to people in the sense "not having morals or scruples."
