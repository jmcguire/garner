# intimidatable

intimidatable. So formed—not \**intimidable*. E.g.: "Sloan was perhaps the least *intimidatable* player in league history." Ray Ratto, "Nobody Sings the Blues Louder than the Jazz," *News & Observer* \(Raleigh\), 6 June 1997, at C1. See -atable.

 - Current ratio in print \(*intimidatable* vs. \**intimidable*\): 4:1
