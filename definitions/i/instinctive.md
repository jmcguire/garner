# instinctive

instinctive; **\*instinctual**. The latter is a needless variant. More than most such variants, it appears in respectable publications—e.g.: "Men notice women's vulnerabilities with a falcon's eye—it's surely *instinctual* \[read *instinctive*\]—yet not just for predatory purposes: also protectively." Edward Hoagland, "Sex and the River Styx," *Harper's Mag.*, Jan. 2003, at 49, 54.

 - Current ratio in print \(*instinctive* vs. \**instinctual*\): 3:1
