# *inter alia* 

*inter alia* ; ***inter alios***. The best course, undoubtedly, is to use *among others*, a phrase that can refer to people or things. The Latin is not so simple. Whereas *inter alia* \(= among other things\) refers to anything that is not human, *inter alios* \(= among other people\) refers to human beings. \(The Latin form *inter alias* means "among other female persons."\)
