# inasmuch as

inasmuch as; **in as much as**; **insofar as**; **in so far as**. In AmE and BrE, the standard spellings are now *inasmuch as* and *insofar as*. Before 2008 in BrE, *in so far as* was preferred as four separate words.

However the phrase is spelled, though, *inasmuch as* is almost always inferior to *because* or *since*. In fact, as H.W. Fowler noted, "its only recommendation as compared with *since* is its pomposity" (*FMEU1* at 263). See **insofar as**. Cf. **insomuch**.
