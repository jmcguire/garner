# infectious

infectious is sometimes erroneously rendered *\*infectuous*—e.g.: "A note of caution: *West End Christmas* possesses a humor that's *infectuous* \[read *infectious*\]." Lynne Cline, "West End Christmas," *Santa Fe New Mexican*, 6 Dec. 1996, Pasatiempo §, at 6. See **contagious**.

## Language-Change Index

 - *infectious* misspelled *\*infectuous*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*infectious* vs. \**infectuous*): 10,977:1
