# Iran

Iran. In educated speech, the country's name is pronounced either /i-**rahn**/ (preferred) or /i-**ran**/ (more anglicized). Avoid the xenophobic yokel's pronunciation /ī-**ran**/ or /**ī**-ran/.

The corresponding adjective and demonym is *Iranian*, pronounced /i-**rahn**-ee-әn/ (preferred) or /i-**ray**-nee-әn/. Avoid /i-**ran**-ee-әn/ and especially /ī-**ray**-nee-әn/ or /**ī**-ray-nee-әn/. See names (c).
