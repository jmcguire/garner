# indicative

indicative; **\*indicant**; **\*indicatory**; **indicial**. *Indicative* is the usual adjective corresponding to the noun *indication*. *\*Indicant* and *\*indicatory* are needless variants except in archaic medical contexts. *Indicial* is the adjective corresponding to both *indicia* and *index*; it means \(1\) "of the nature of an indicia; indicative"; or \(2\) "of the nature or form of an index." See **indicia**.
