# inapposite

inapposite \(= inappropriate; irrelevant\) is sometimes misspelled *\*inopposite*—e.g.: "Federal laws governing probate and land use can lead to results *inopposite* \[read *inapposite*\] to traditional laws for communal property." David S. Case & David A. Voluck, *Alaskan Natives and American Laws* 117 \(3d ed. 2012\).

Current ratio in print \(*inapposite* vs. \**inopposite*\): 209:1
