# inferable

inferable; **\*inferrable**; **\*inferrible**. The preferred form *inferable*, pronounced /in-**fәr**-ә-bәl/, was standard by the mid-1800s. *\*Inferrable* is a variant spelling. Through the mid-1950s, some commentators considered *\*inferrible* the best spelling because of the rule that a consonant should be doubled after a stressed syllable. *Inferable*, which has now ousted the other forms, is anomalous in its spelling. Cf. **deferrable** & **transferable**.
