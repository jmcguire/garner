# impeller

impeller \(= someone or something that imparts motion or drives forward, such as a rotor in a water pump\), an agent noun dating from the 1600s, has always been predominantly so spelled. One feels impelled to note that \**impellor*, an infrequent variant, reached the height of its popularity in the 1960s. See -er \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*impeller* vs. \**impellor*\): 99:1
