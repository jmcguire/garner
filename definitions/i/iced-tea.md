# iced tea

iced tea; **ice tea**. The first is more logical and far more frequent in print. In speech, however, the /d/ sound in *iced* is usually slurred into the following /t/; hardly anyone asks a server for a glass of /īsd **tee**/. But despite this indistinct pronunciation, *iced tea* shows little evidence of following the progression of *\*iced cream* to *ice cream*. See adjectives (f). Cf. **fine-toothed comb**, **skim milk** & **stained glass**.

 - Current ratio in print (*iced tea* vs. *ice tea*): 11:1
