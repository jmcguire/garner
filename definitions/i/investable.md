# investable

investable (= available for investment), an adjective dating from the mid-1800s, has vied with \**investible* for most of its life. In BrE, \**investible* predominated from about 1925 to 2003; in AmE, it predominated only from about 1959 to 1967. Since 2001, *investable* has predominated across World Englishes.

 - Current ratio in print (*investable* vs. \**investible*): 2.4:1
