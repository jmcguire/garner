# insubstantial

insubstantial; \***unsubstantial**. Although H.W. Fowler preferred *\*unsubstantial* and it predominated till about 1950, it is rarely seen now and should be classed a needless variant. *Insubstantial*, the standard term, is much more common than *\*unsubstantial* in modern print sources.

 - Current ratio in print (*insubstantial* vs. \**unsubstantial*): 3:1
