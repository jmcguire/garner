# inconsistency

inconsistency; **\*inconsistence**. Writers on usage formerly tried to distinguish between the forms, reserving *inconsistency* for the sense "the general quality of being inconsistent" and making *\*inconsistence* mean "an inconsistent act; an instance of being inconsistent." Today, however, *inconsistency* has ousted *-ce* in all senses. Avoid *\*inconsistence* as a needless variant. Cf. **consistency**.

Current ratio in print \(*inconsistency* vs. \**inconsistence*\): 133:1
