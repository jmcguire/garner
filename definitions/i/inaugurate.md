# inaugurate

inaugurate is a formal word (some might say pompous) for *begin* or *start*, being more formal even than *commence*. In the 1800s, Richard Grant White wrote that *inaugurate* "is a word which might better be eschewed by all those who do not wish to talk high-flying nonsense." *Words and Their Uses, Past and Present* 128 (2d ed. 1872). The word is no longer so distasteful. Cf. **initiate**.
