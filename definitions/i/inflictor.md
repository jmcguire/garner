# inflictor

inflictor; **inflicter**. Meaning "someone who makes another suffer something unpleasant," this word has been spelled both ways with almost equal frequency since the mid-1700s. Today *inflictor* predominates in AmE and BrE alike. But the competition persists. See -er (a).

 - Current ratio in print (*inflictor* vs. *inflicter* in AmE): 3:1
 - Current ratio in print (*inflictor* vs. *inflicter* in BrE): 2.2:1
