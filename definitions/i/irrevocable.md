# irrevocable

irrevocable; **\*unrevokable**; **\*irrevokable**. The first—the preferred form—is pronounced /i-**rev**-ә-kә-bәl/, not /ir-ә-**voh**-kә-bәl/. \(See pronunciation \(b\).\) *\*Unrevokable* is a needless variant. *\*Irrevokable* is simply a misspelling.

On *irrevocable* as a nongradable adjective, see adjectives \(b\).

 - *irrevocable* mispronounced /ir-ә-**voh**-kә-bәl/: Stage 4
 - Current ratio in print \(*irrevocable* vs. *\*irrevokable* vs. *\*unrevokable*\): 5,631:1.6:1
