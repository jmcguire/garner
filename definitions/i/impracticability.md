# impracticability

impracticability \(= practical impossibility\) is sometimes misspelled *\*impractibility*—e.g.: "The *impractibility* \[read *impracticability*\] of backing reservoir water back over Hanford reactor sites helped prevent construction of Ben Franklin Dam." Bill Dietrich, "Ecology—An Opportunity or Embarrassment?," *Seattle Times*, 9 Nov. 1992, at D1.

 - Current ratio in print \(*impracticability* vs. *\*impractibility*\): 156:1
