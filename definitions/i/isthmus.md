# isthmus

isthmus is pronounced /**is**-mәs/ or /**isth**-mәs/. The latter is almost unpronounceable, and there's no good reason to try. Pl. *isthmuses*. The word derives from the Greek *isthmos*, and it is an error to make the plural—as some do—*\*isthmi*. See hypercorrection \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*isthmuses* vs. *\*isthmi*\): 4:1
