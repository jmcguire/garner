# inflator

inflator \(= someone or something that \[a\] fills with air or gas, \[b\] is seen as more impressive or important than it really is, or \[c\] makes something increase in price or value\), an agent noun dating from the mid-1800s, has always been predominantly so spelled. \**Inflater* is a variant. See -er \(a\).

Current ratio in print \(*inflator* vs. \**inflater*\): 2.4:1
