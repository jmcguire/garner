# intendancy

intendancy (= \[1\] superintendence or supervision; or \[2\] the office, position, or function of a superintendent) is a little-needed word unless one is discussing a foreign functionary, especially one from France, Spain, or Latin America. (The word is established in that type of context.) Even less needed are its variants \**intendance*, \**intendency*, and \**intendence*.

 - Current ratio in print (*intendancy* vs. \**intendance* vs. \**intendency* vs. \**intendence*): 19:10:2.2:1
