# imbecile

imbecile; **imbecilic**. The preferred adjectival form of *imbecile*, n., was once thought to be *imbecile*. But *imbecilic*, which emerged in the late 1800s, is now standard as well.

## Language-Change Index

*imbecilic*: Stage 5
Current ratio in print \(*imbecile grin* vs. *imbecilic grin*\): 1.3:1
