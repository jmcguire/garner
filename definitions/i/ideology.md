# ideology

ideology. So spelled. But many writers misunderstand its etymology, believing that the word is somehow derived from our modern word *idea*, and so misspell it *\*idealogy*. In fact, like several other words beginning with *ideo-* (e.g., *ideograph*), *ideology* passed into English through French (*idéologie*) and has been spelled *ideo-* in English since the 1700s. Although the bungled spelling has become common enough that it's listed in some dictionaries, that isn't a persuasive defense of its use. Cf. **minuscule**.
