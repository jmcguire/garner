# improviser

improviser; \***improvisator**; **\*improvisor**; **improvisatore**. The usual term for "someone who improvises" is *improviser*. \(The *-or* spelling is not preferred.\) *\*Improvisator* is an archaic formalism. *Improvisatore* is an Italianate literary word meaning "someone who composes verse or drama extemporaneously."

 - Current ratio in print \(*improviser* vs. *\*improvisor* vs. *\*improvisator*\): 35:1.6:1
