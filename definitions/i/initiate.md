# initiate

initiate —a formal word for *start*, *begin*, *open*, or *introduce*—is appropriate when referring to taking the first step in an important matter. Although you may *start* an interview, *start* a conversation, or *begin* a lecture, you *initiate* a series of high-level negotiations. But it is ludicrous to say that you *initiate* an ordinary telephone call. Perhaps you *place* it. Cf. **inaugurate**.
