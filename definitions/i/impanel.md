# impanel

impanel; **empanel**. Meaning "to enroll in or on a panel, as of jurors," *impanel* is the usual spelling in AmE, *empanel* in BrE. Dating from the 1400s, the term did not have its settled preferences in spelling until the late 1800s. AmE inflects the verb *impaneled*, *impaneling*; BrE makes it *empanelled*, *empanelling*. See spelling \(c\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*impaneled* vs. *empaneled* in AmE\): 1.4:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*empanelled* vs. *impanelled* in BrE\): 6:1
