# instill

instill; **instil**. The predominant spelling is *instill* in AmE, *instil* in BrE. This word takes the preposition *in(to)*, not *with* \<he instilled character as well as knowledge into his students\>. *With* results from confusion of *inspire* with *instill* or *infuse*. See object-shuffling & **inculcate (b)**. Cf. **imbue**.

 - Current ratio in print (*instill* vs. *instil* in AmE): 12:1
 - Current ratio in print (*instil* vs. *instill* in BrE): 1.5:1
