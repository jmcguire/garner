# inhospitable

inhospitable (= \[1\] \[of a place\] difficult to live or stay in because of adverse conditions; or \[2\] \[of a person\] not welcoming visitors in a friendly way) is traditionally pronounced /in-**hos**-pi-tә-bәl/—though today /in-hah-**spit**-ә-bәl/ is probably more frequent throughout the English-speaking world. Cf. **hospitable**.
