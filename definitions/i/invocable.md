# invocable

invocable \(= \[1\] that can be appealed to; or \[2\] that can be put into effect or operation\) has been the standard term since the early 1800s. *\*Invokable* is a variant spelling. Both are pronounced /in-**voh**-kә-bәl/.

 - Current ratio in print \(*invocable* vs. \**invokable*\): 1.8:1
