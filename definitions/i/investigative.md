# investigative

investigative; **investigatory**. *W3* calls *investigatory* "chiefly British," but it occurs almost as commonly as *investigative* in American law-enforcement contexts. There is no need for the two variants to coexist. We might be well advised to throw out *investigatory* and stick with *investigative*, or to develop some differentiation.

 - Current ratio in print (*investigative* vs. *investigatory*): 10:1
