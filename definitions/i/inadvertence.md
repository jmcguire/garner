# inadvertence

inadvertence; **inadvertency**. The differentiation between these terms should be carefully observed. *Inadvertence* = an inadvertent act; a fault of inattention. *Inadvertency* = the quality or state of being inadvertent \<the inadvertency of the act is not disputed\>. *\*Inadvertancy* and *\*inadvertance* are common misspellings.
