# discover

discover; **invent**. To *discover* is \(1\) to recognize for the first time something that exists, such as electricity or a law of nature, or has come into existence, such as a new species, or \(2\) to devise a new use for or application of something already known, such as synthetic silk. To *invent* is usually to consciously create something that did not previously exist and that has not been thought of by anyone else. For example, in creating a new chemical compound, a person may *invent* it by planning its creation \(studying the properties of various chemicals and their known interactions and deducing how they will work together\). On the other hand, one may accidentally combine chemicals in an unintended amount or manner and then notice an interesting product, thus *discovering* a new compound.
