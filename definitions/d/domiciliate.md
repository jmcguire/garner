# domiciliate

domiciliate; **domesticate**; **\*domesticize**. *Domiciliate* = to establish a domicile or home. *Domesticate* = \(1\) to make domestic; \(2\) to make a member of the household; or \(3\) to tame \(wild animals\) or cultivate \(wild plants\) for human use. *\*Domesticize* is a needless variant of *domesticate*. See -ize.

 - Current ratio in print \(*domesticated* vs. *\*domesticized*\): 512:1
