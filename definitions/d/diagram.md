# diagram

diagram, vb., makes *diagrammed* and *diagramming* across World Englishes. \**Diagramed* and \**diagraming* are chiefly AmE variants that never became predominant. See spelling \(c\). Cf. **program\(m\)er**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*diagrammed* vs. \**diagramed*\): 7:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*diagramming* vs. \**diagraming*\): 18:1
