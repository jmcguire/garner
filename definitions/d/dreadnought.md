# dreadnought

dreadnought \(= a fearless and powerful person or thing, such as a battleship, or by extension, a heavy wool coat or a large-bodied acoustic guitar\) is the standard spelling. *\*Dreadnaught* is a variant. See **naught**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*dreadnought* vs. \**dreadnaught*\): 6:1
