# draperies

draperies; **drapes**. Usage critics and etiquette mavens have long preferred *draperies* over *drapes*, but both have been stamped as a lowbrow usage. The term *curtains* is perhaps the best choice. Since 1980 or so, *drapes* has appeared more often in print sources than *draperies*. See class distinctions.

 - Current ratio in print (*curtains* vs. *drapes* vs. *draperies*): 15:2.2:1
