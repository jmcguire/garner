# danse macabre

danse macabre; **dance macabre**. Denoting an art form meant to remind people of their mortality and of death as a social equalizer, *danse macabre* is more than ten times as common as the anglicized *dance macabre*. Because *macabre* is a postpositive adjective, the plural is *danses macabres* or *dances macabres*. See plurals \(g\).
