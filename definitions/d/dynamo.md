# dynamo

dynamo \(= \[1\] a power generator; or \[2\] an inexhaustibly energetic and forceful person\) forms the plural *dynamos*—not *\*dynamoes*. The metaphorical extension from sense 1 to sense 2 occurred in the late 1930s. See plurals \(d\).

Current ratio in print \(*dynamos* vs. *\*dynamoes*\): 223:1
