# deceive

deceive; **defraud**. To *deceive* is to induce someone to believe a falsehood. The deceiver may know the statement to be false or may make it recklessly. To *defraud* is to cause some kind of injury or loss by deceit. *Defrauding* deprives a person of a right or possession, whereas *deceiving* merely leads a person into a state of mind.
