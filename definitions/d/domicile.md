# domicile

domicile; \***domicil**. *Domicile* /**dom**-ә-sil/ is spelled both with and without the final *e*, but the better and more common spelling today is with it. Although *\*domicil* predominated in AmE for most of the 1700s, it looks incomplete today. See **citizenship**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*domicile* vs. \**domicil*\): 30:1
