# diminuendo

diminuendo \(= a musical direction to become gradually quieter\), an Italian loanword dating in English from 1776, has predominantly made the plural *diminuendos* \(not *\*diminuendoes*\) from 1850 on. The Italianate plural *\*diminuendi* is pure pretension today. See plurals \(b\).

Current ratio in print \(*diminuendos* vs. *\*diminuendi* vs. *\*diminuendoes*\): 9:2:1
