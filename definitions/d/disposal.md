# disposal

disposal; **disposition**. Both mean generally "a getting rid of," but *disposal* more often has to do with trash or inconsequential items, whereas *disposition* is used in reference to things that are arranged, settled, or otherwise managed. *Disposition* connotes a preconceived plan and an orderly arrangement. *Disposal*, by contrast, bears negative connotations—more so in AmE than in BrE.

*Disposition* also—and primarily—means "mood; temperament; inclination."
