# defuse

defuse \(= \[1\] to remove the detonating device from \[a bomb, etc.\]; or \[2\] to render less volatile\) is the standard spelling. *\*Defuze* is a variant. For a similar variation in spelling, see **fuse**. For confusion of *defuse* with *diffuse*, see **diffuse**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*defuse* vs. \**defuze*\): 463:1
