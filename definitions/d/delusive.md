# delusive

delusive; **delusional**; \***delusory**. *Delusive* = \(1\) tending to delude, deceptive; or \(2\) of the nature of a delusion. Usually sense 1 applies. *Delusional* is the more usual term for sense 2—e.g.: "Florence acted under a completely *delusional* perception of reality." *\*Delusory* is a needless variant of the other two.
