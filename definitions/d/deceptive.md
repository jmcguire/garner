# deceptive

deceptive; **deceitful**. Although both words connote "misleading," *deceptive* does not necessarily indicate dishonesty. A person or thing may mislead another with bad intent <deceptive business practice> <deceptive answer> or with no bad intent <appearance can be deceptive>. But someone or something *deceitful* always has an underlying intent to lie, cheat, or defraud by misleading <deceitful story> <deceitful game>.
