# Decalogue

Decalogue (= the Ten Commandments), a French/Latin loanword dating in English from the 1300s, has been predominantly so written since the 1840s. The variant forms *decalogue* (in lowercase), \**Decalog*, and \**decalog* occur less frequently.

 - Current ratio in print (*Decalogue* vs. *decalogue* vs. \**Decalog* vs. \**decalog*): 395:54:3:1
