# dido

dido \(= \[1\] a mischievous or foolish caper; or \[2\] a flibbertigibbet\), a word of uncertain origin dating from the early 1800s, has predominantly made the plural *didoes* since about 1895. Before that, the variant \**didos* was a close competitor with *didoes*. See plurals \(d\).

Current ratio in print \(*didoes* vs. \**didos*\): 3:1
