# delirium tremens

delirium tremens. This phrase—often abbreviated as *d.t.'s* or *D.T.'s*—should not be used, as it sometimes is, to describe frenzied drunkenness. In fact, it denotes a mental disease characterized by violent mania, with tremors and hallucinations, usually induced by sudden abstinence from alcohol or another drug after excessive use over a prolonged period. A synonymous phrase is *mania à potu*.
