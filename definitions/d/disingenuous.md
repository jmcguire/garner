# disingenuous

disingenuous \(= lacking candor, sincerity, and intellectual honesty\), an adjective dating from the 1600s, has always been subject to being misrendered \**disingenious*, which the *OED* terms "a frequent error in the 17th century." Less frequent today, it persists.

1700 ratio in print \(*disingenuous* vs. \**disingenious*\): 41:1   
Current ratio in print \(*disingenuous* vs. \**disingenious*\): 1,284:1
