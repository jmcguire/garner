# deplane

deplane. This verb, like *enplane* and *reinplane*, is characteristic of airlinese, a relatively new brand of jargon dating from the latter half of the 20th century. Surprisingly, *deplane* is traceable back to the 1920s. Even so, careful writers and speakers stick to such time-honored expressions as *get off* and *get out*. It's as plain as that.

## Language-Change Index

 - *deplane* for *get off* (an airplane): Stage 4
