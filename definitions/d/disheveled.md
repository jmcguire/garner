# disheveled

disheveled /dis-**shev**-әld/ (= tousled, rumpled, and unkempt), an adjective dating from the late 1500s, has been predominantly so spelled in AmE since the late 1930s. Before that, *dishevelled*—the BrE spelling—was universal. Delighted to have these points tidied up.

 - Current ratio in print (*disheveled* vs. *dishevelled* in AmE): 6:1
 - Current ratio in print (*dishevelled* vs. *disheveled* in BrE): 2.2:1
