# dos and don'ts

dos and don'ts. This is the logical form of this irreversible binomial—not *\*do's and don'ts*—because the plural of *do* needs no apostrophe \(just as the plural of *don't* needs no apostrophe\). See plurals \(k\) & punctuation \(a\).

Current ratio in print \(*dos and don'ts* vs. *\*do's and don'ts*\): 1.7:1
