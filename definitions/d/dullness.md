# dullness

dullness; **\*dulness**. Although *\*dulness* predominated in print \(both AmE and BrE\) from the 1700s through the early 1900s, *dullness* experienced an exciting surge in the first two decades of the 20th century and had established itself as the standard form by the 1920s.

 - Current ratio in print \(*dullness* vs. \**dulness*\): 2.5:1
