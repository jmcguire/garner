# dollhouse; doll's house

dollhouse; doll's house; **\*dolls' house**. The strong BrE preference today is for the oldest form, dating from the 1700s: *doll's house*. The strong AmE preference \(since about 1970\) is for the 19th-century solid form *dollhouse*. The plural possessive *\*dolls' house* is a variant that has predominance nowhere across World Englishes.
