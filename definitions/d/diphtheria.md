# diphtheria

diphtheria is properly pronounced /dif-**thir**-ee-ә/, not /dip-**thir**-ee-ә/. See pronunciation \(d\).

Language-Change Index

1. *diphtheria* mispronounced /dip-**thir**-ee-ә/: Stage 4

2. *diphtheria* misspelled *\*diptheria*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*diphtheria* vs. \**diptheria*\): 75:1
