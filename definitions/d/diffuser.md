# diffuser

diffuser \(= someone or something that spreads out or distributes something widely and usu. evenly\), an agent noun dating from the late 1600s, has almost always been predominantly so spelled. For a brief period from about 1880 to about 1910, \**diffusor* was a frequent variant. See -er \(a\).

Current ratio in print \(*diffuser* vs. \**diffusor*\): 59:1
