# discommend

discommend is the opposite of *recommend*, not of *commend*. E.g.: "I read in Wednesday morning's edition of *The Press-Enterprise* that they still *discommend* eating bacon." Letter of F.D. Richardson, "Riverside Bring on the Eggs," *Press-Enterprise* (Riverside, Cal.), 17 Nov. 1995, at A10. This verb was much more widely used in the 1700s than it is today.
