# demi-

demi-. This prefix indicates that a thing is \(1\) half the size or quality of another \(e.g., *demitasse* = a small cup for coffee\), or \(2\) of inferior or less than full rank, size, or status \(e.g., *demigod* = a less-important deity\). It's occasionally used pejoratively to belittle someone or something—e.g.: "On and on, painting Stalin a little taller, a bit more substantial, without a withered arm and never, ever with another Party leader, those fatuous *demi-tyrants* who sooner or later were erased from pictures and marched to a cell." Martin Cruz Smith, *Tatiana* 20 \(2013\).
