# desegregation

desegregation; **integration**. Although no distinction between a requirement of *integration* and a requirement of *desegregation* is ordinarily observed in legal usage, the distinction may be important in understanding the constitutional law of race and the schools. Certainly it would be useful, in reference to public schools in the U.S., if we distinguished between court-ordered *desegregation* \(= the abrogation of policies that segregate races into different institutions and facilities\) and court-ordered *integration* \(= the incorporation of different races into existing institutions for the purpose of achieving a racial balance\).
