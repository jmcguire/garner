# dildo

dildo \(= an object shaped like a penis, used for sexual stimulation\), a word of unknown origin dating in English from the 1500s, has predominantly made the plural *dildos* since about 1975. Before that, the variant \**dildoes* was sometimes predominant. See plurals \(d\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*dildos* vs. \**dildoes*\): 13:1
