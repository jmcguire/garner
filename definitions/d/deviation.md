# deviation

deviation; **deviance**; \***deviancy**. The general term for "an act or instance of deviating" is *deviation* <a ship's deviation from its voyage route> <deviation from orthodox religion>. *Deviation* is more neutral in connotation than *deviance*, which means "the quality or state of deviating from established norms, esp. in social customs." *\*Deviancy* is a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*deviance* vs. *\*deviancy*): 12:1
