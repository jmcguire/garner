# divulgence

divulgence; **\*divulgation**; **\*divulgement**. Though the latter two date from the early 1500s, *divulgence*, which dates from the mid-1800s, is now the preferred noun corresponding to the verb *divulge*.

Current ratio in print \(*divulgence* vs. \**divulgation* vs. \**divulgement*\): 8:1.1:1
