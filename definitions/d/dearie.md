# dearie

dearie \(the old-fashioned colloquialism for referring to someone in an esp. familiar way\), a diminutive dating in English from the late 1600s, was originally **dearee* or \**deary* but was later predominantly spelled *dearie*—first in BrE \(beginning about 1800\) and later in AmE \(beginning about 1885\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*dearie* vs. \**deary*\): 4:1
