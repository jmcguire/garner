# dinky

dinky; **dinkey**. A *dinkey engine* is one in a small locomotive used in a freight yard for tasks such as shunting railway cars. A *dinky engine* is a pitifully puny one. The word *dinkey* standing alone is normally a noun meaning "a small locomotive." The word *dinky*, normally an adjective, is a casualism, meaning "unduly and unattractively small"; it often appears in the redundant phrase *dinky little* \<that dinky little house\>.

 - Current ratio in print (*dinky little* vs. \**dinkey little*): 92:1
