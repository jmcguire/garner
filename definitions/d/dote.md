# dote

dote, vb.; **dotage**, n. These terms apply both to uncritical (and sometimes foolish) affection (such as "puppy love") and to mental impairment (such as senility). But the modern trend is to use the verb *dote* only in the former sense <dote on one's children>, and the noun *dotage* only in the latter sense <the debilities of dotage>.
