# dismissal

dismissal; \***dismission**. The much older word *\*dismission* \(1547\) has given way almost completely to the upstart *dismissal* \(1778\), considered a mere variant in the early 1800s. Today, *\*dismission* is the needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print \(*dismissal* vs. \**dismission*\): 111:1
