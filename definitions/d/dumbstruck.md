# dumbstruck

dumbstruck \(= so shocked or surprised as to be speechless\), an adjectival compound dating from the mid-1700s, has been the predominant form since the early 1920s. Before that, \**dumb-stricken* was the standard term from about 1860 to 1922. Other variants are \**dumb-struck* and \**dumbstricken*.

Current ratio in print \(*dumbstruck* vs. \**dumb-struck* vs. \**dumb-stricken* vs. \**dumbstricken*\): 1,107:11:4:1
