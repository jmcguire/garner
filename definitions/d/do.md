# do

do; **doh**. In the "sol-fa syllables" made famous in *The Sound of Music* \(1965\)—do, re, mi, fa, sol, la, ti, do—the AmE spelling of the first and last syllable is *do* \(pronounced /doh/, as in a deer, a female deer\). The BrE spelling is said to be *doh* \(*FMEU3* at 224\)—but the linguistic evidence in BrE print sources points to *do*.
