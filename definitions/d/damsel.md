# damsel

damsel. Are you in distress over the spelling of this word? No need. *Damsel* has been vastly predominant since 1700. \**Damosel*, \**damoiselle*, \**damozel*, and \**damoisel* \(as well as others too rare to be recorded\) are variant forms. Not much of a boost to the penultimate variant in that list was Dante Gabriel Rosetti's poem "The Blessed Damozel" \(1850\)—and his splendid paintings by the same name didn't do much, either.

Current ratio in print \(*damsel* vs. \**damosel* vs. \**damoiselle* vs. \**damozel* vs. \**damoisel*\): 1,794:119:12:7:1
