# deforest

deforest; **disafforest**; \***disforest**. For the act of cutting down trees, especially clear-cutting, *deforest* has predominated in AmE since the 1860s and worldwide since the 1920s. *Disafforest* dominated in BrE until the mid 1980s, when *deforest* inexplicably overtook it to become much more common. The more practical *\*disforest* was strong in the 1800s but declined as *deforest* rose. It is now a needless variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*deforest* vs. \**disforest* vs. *disafforest*): 26:1.3:1
