# dexterous

dexterous; \***dextrous**; **dextral**. *Dexterous*, the preferred spelling in both AmE and BrE, means "clever, adept, skillful, artful." The spelling *\*dextrous* predominated in BrE through the late 1700s but has waned in print since about 1800. *Dextral* means "on the right; right-handed." See **ambidextrous**.

 - Current ratio in print (*dexterous* vs. *\*dextrous*): 7:1
