# derringer

derringer \(= a small pistol\) is so spelled even though the person for whom it was named was Henry Deringer \(1786–1868\), with only one *r*. Today *\*deringer* \(sorry, Henry\) is only a variant spelling, and neither form is capitalized.

Current ratio in print \(*derringer* vs. \**deringer*\): 103:1
