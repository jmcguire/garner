# digestion

digestion \(= \[1\] the body's transformation of food into useful substances; or \[2\] the ability of one's body to make food undergo this transformation\) is preferably pronounced /di-**jes**-chәn/—not /**dī**-jes-chәn/. And the pain known as *indigestion* is preferably /in-di-**jes**-chәn/—not /in-dī-**jes**-chәn/.

The corresponding verb *digest* is preferably /di-**jest**/ and the adjective *digestive* /di-**jes**-tiv/.

Yet the noun *digest* \(= an abstract or synopsis of a book, report, etc.\) is pronounced /**dī**-jest/.
