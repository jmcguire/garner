# daredevilry

daredevilry; **daredeviltry**. The latter has become a needless variant—though it was predominant in AmE from 1900 through the 1980s. Perhaps AmE was influenced by BrE, in which *daredevilry* has always been standard. See **deviltry**.

 - Current ratio in print (*daredevilry* vs. *daredeviltry*): 3:1
