# disembark

disembark; \***debark**; **\*disbark**. *Disembark*, the preferred term since the late 1700s, is over ten times as common in modern print sources as the other two forms combined. *\*Debark* and *\*disbark* are needless variants.

 - Current ratio in print (*disembark* vs. \**debark* vs. \**disbark*): 3,132:165:1
