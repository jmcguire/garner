# disqualified

disqualified; **unqualified**. These words have quite different senses. *Disqualified* = disabled; debarred. *Unqualified* = not meeting the requirements. An *unqualified* applicant doesn't have the skills or training that the job requires. A *disqualified* applicant, even one with the skills and training, can't be considered because of some rule, such as a policy against nepotism.
