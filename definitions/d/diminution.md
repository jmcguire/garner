# diminution

diminution; **\*diminishment**. The latter is a needless variant. *Diminution* \(/dim-ә-**nyoo**-shәn/ or /-**noo**-shәn/\) is often mispronounced /dim-yoo-**nish**-әn/, by metathesis. And it is sometimes erroneously spelled *\*dimunition*—e.g.: "Dilution of talent rarely translates into *dimunition* \[read *diminution*\] of interest." Bob Verdi, "World Cup Is Great, but the Earth Didn't Move or Angels Sing," *Chicago Trib.*, 6 July 1994, Sports §, at 1.
