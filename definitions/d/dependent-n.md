# dependent, n

dependent, n.; **dependant**. For the noun, the older spelling is *dependant*. The *OED* notes: "from the 18th c. \(like the adj.\) spelt *dependent*, after L.; but the spelling *-ant* still predominates in the \[noun\]." *W11* countenances *-ent* over *-ant.* The *COD* continues the Oxonian preference for *-ant*, noting that *-ent* is chiefly American. Certainly the British differentiation in spelling between the adjective and the noun is a useful one, but it remains tenuous in BrE. The AmE preference for -*ent* for both noun and adjective has been strong since 1950.
