# diuretic

diuretic (= a substance that increases the flow of urine), a Latin loanword dating in English from about 1400, is occasionally misspelled \**diarrhetic* (a rare adjective corresponding to *diarrhea*—for which purpose *diarrheal* is standard). See **diarrheal**.

 - Current ratio in print (*diuretics* vs. \**diarrhetics*): 12,667:1
