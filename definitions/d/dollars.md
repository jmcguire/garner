# dollars

dollars. When the dollar sign appears with a numeral, using the word *dollars* afterward is redundant—e.g.: "By October this year, Mr. Bradley had raised nearly *$20 million dollars* \[read *$20 million*\]." James Dao, "Seeking Upset, Bradley Enlists Unlikely Model," *N.Y. Times*, 27 Dec. 1999, at A1, A12. See redundancy.
