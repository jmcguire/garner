# desist

desist, a formal word for *stop* or *leave off*, is usually followed by the preposition *from* or \(less commonly\) *in*—e.g.: "Clinton told congressional leaders that he has sent a written warning to Saddam through the Iraqi mission at the United Nations in New York to *desist from* all threatening moves against U.S. pilots—or face devastating consequences." Leo Rennert, "U.S. Force Ordered to Kuwait," *Fresno Bee*, 18 Sept. 1996, at A1.
