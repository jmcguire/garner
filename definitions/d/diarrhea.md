# diarrhea

diarrhea; **diarrhoea**. The term denoting the physical symptom of abnormally frequent bowel movements consisting of watery stools is predominantly spelled *diarrhea* in AmE and *diarrhoea* in BrE. The British spelling was usual from the 1700s through the early 1900s, but in AmE *diarrhea* became the predominant spelling about 1915, and that spelling has become predominant today across World Englishes.

 - Current ratio in print \(*diarrhea* vs. *diarrhoea* in AmE\): 18:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*diarrhoea* vs. *diarrhea* in BrE\): 1.8:1
 - Current ratio in print \(*diarrhea* vs. *diarrhoea* in World Englishes\): 5:1
