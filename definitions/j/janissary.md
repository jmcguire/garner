# janissary

janissary (= a loyal, subservient follower) has been the standard spelling since the 1700s. It is capitalized only when used to mean "a Turkish infantry soldier in the sultan's guard." *\*Janisary* and *\*janizary* are variants.

 - Current ratio in print (*janissary* vs. *\*janizary* vs. *\*janisary*): 331:21:1
