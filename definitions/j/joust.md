# joust

joust. The traditional view is that this word should be pronounced either /jәst/ or /joost/. See *NBC Handbook of Pronunciation* 189 \(3d ed. 1964\) \(listing only /joost/\); William H.P. Phyfe, *20,000 Words Often Mispronounced* 421 \(1937\) \(listing only /jәst/ and /joost/\). But almost all Americans and Britons say /jowst/; this pronunciation must be considered not just acceptable, but—because of its overwhelming prevalence, coupled with no good reason for opposing it—preferable. Let the orthoepic jousting cease.
