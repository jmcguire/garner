# jargonistic

jargonistic (= \[1\] characterized by jargon; or \[2\] phrased in jargon), an adjective dating from the 1880s, has been the standard term since the 1940s. Among its needless variants are (in descending order of frequency) *\*jargony*, *\*jargonish*, *\*jargonic*, and *\*jargonal*.

 - Current ratio in print (*jargonistic* vs. *\*jargony* vs. *\*jargonish* vs. *\*jargonic* vs. *\*jargonal*): 107:75:13:4:1
