# jujitsu

jujitsu; **\*jujutsu**; **\*jiujitsu**; **\*jiujutsu**. Although the Japanese term is *jū-jūtsu*, the phonetic spelling *jujitsu* became the established form in both AmE and BrE during the mid-20th century. The others are variant spellings in English.

Current ratio in print \(*jujitsu* vs. \**jujutsu* vs. \**jiujitsu* vs. \**jiujutsu*\): 32:6:5:1
