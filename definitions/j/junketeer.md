# junketeer

junketeer \(= \[1\] someone who participates in a feast or picnic; or \[2\] someone who goes on an excursion or pleasure trip\), a modern noun dating from the early 1900s in AmE, soon displaced the older term \**junketer*, which dates from the early 1800s. *Junketeer* /jәn-kә-**teer**/ became predominant in AmE in the 1920s. Neither term now appears, however, in any variety of English. The variant form \**junketter* is rare.

 - Current ratio in print \(*junketeer* vs. \**junketer*\): 2:1
