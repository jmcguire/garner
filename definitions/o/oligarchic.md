# oligarchic

oligarchic; \***oligarchical**. For the adjective corresponding to *oligarchy* \(= \[1\] a small, exclusive group of people who run a country or organization; or \[2\] the country or organization so run\), the standard adjective was \**oligarchical* from the late 1500s through the end of World War I \(1914–1918\). But since about 1918, *oligarchic* has had the support of more than just a small cadre and has reigned supreme as the standard adjective. See -ic/-ical.

 - Current ratio in print \(*oligarchic* vs. \**oligarchical*\): 3:1
