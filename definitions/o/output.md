# output

output, vb., meaning "to produce \(goods, information, etc.\) as the result of a process, work, etc.," forms the past tense and past participle on the model of *put*: *output* > *output* > *output*. The variant form \**outputted* is a solecism. Cf. **input**.

 - Current ratio in print \(*been output* vs. \**been outputted*\): 8:1
