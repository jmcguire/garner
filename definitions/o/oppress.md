# oppress

oppress; **repress**. *Oppress* = to subject \(a person or a people\) to inhumane or other unfair treatment; to persecute. *Repress* = \(1\) to keep under control; \(2\) to reduce \(people\) to subjection; \(3\) to inhibit or restrain; or \(4\) to suppress. The negative connotations of *oppress* are greater than those of *repress*.
