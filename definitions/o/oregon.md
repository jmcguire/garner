# Oregon

Oregon. Some natives of the state insist on a long medial *e*: /**or**-ee-gәn/. Others, though, are perfectly happy putting a short vowel or schwa in the middle syllable: /**or**-i-gәn/ or /**or**-ә-gәn/. No full-blooded Oregonian /or-i-**goh**-nee-әn/, though, countenances rhyming the last syllable with *gone*. It's *gun*, not *gone*.
