# objectify

objectify; **objectivize**. *Objectify*, dating from the mid-1800s, means either \(1\) "to make into an object," or \(2\) "to render objective." *Objectivize*, dating from the early 1800s, means "to render objective." It would be convenient for the words to undergo differentiation, so that *objectify* would be confined to its sense 1, while *objectivize* would preempt *objectify* in the latter's sense 2. See **reify**.
