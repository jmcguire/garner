# otolaryngology

otolaryngology; **otorhinolaryngology**. These are essentially synonymous words denoting the medical field dealing with the ears, nose, and throat. The *-rhino-* version manages to work in explicit reference to the nose, but the resulting word is downright proctological. So the shorter form, which is more widespread in medicine, is preferable. But most people don't say *otolaryngologist*; they say *ear, nose, and throat specialist*.

The words are pronounced as follows: *otolaryngology* /**oh**-toh-ler-in-**gol**-ә-jee/; *otorhinolaryngology* /**oh**-toh-**rī**-noh-ler-in-**gol**-ә-jee/.

Current ratio in print \(*otolaryngology* vs. *otorhinolaryngology*\): 5:1
