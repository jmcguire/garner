# osetra

osetra /oh-**se**-trә/ (= \[1\] a type of sturgeon common in the Caspian Sea; or \[2\] the golden or brownish caviar from this fish), a term introduced into English in the 1950s, is predominantly so spelled. \**Ossetra* is a variant.

 - Current ratio in print (*osetra* vs. \**ossetra*): 7:1
