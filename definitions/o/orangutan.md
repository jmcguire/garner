# orangutan

orangutan; \***orang-utan**; **\*orangoutang**; **\*orangutang**; **\*orangoutan**. The spelling of this word—deriving from the Malay term *oran utan* ("forest man")—has been settled since about 1950: *orangutan*. The others are variant spellings. The standard AmE pronunciation is /ә-**rang**-\[g\]ә-tan/. But the other pronunciation (/ә-**rang**-\[g\]ә-tang/) remains quite common, possibly because the reduplicative rhyme is a bit easier to remember and easier to say.
