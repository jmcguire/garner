# obsequies

obsequies; **obsequious**. Despite their resemblance and common etymology, these words are unrelated in meaning. *Obsequies* /**ob**-si-kweez/, the plural noun (the singular *obsequy* is rare), is a formal word for *funeral.* *Obsequious*, the adjective, means "overeager to please or agree with powerful people." It is pronounced /әb-**see**-kwee-әs/ or /ob-**see**-kwee-әs/.
