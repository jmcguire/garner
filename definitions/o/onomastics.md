# onomastics

onomastics \(= the study of names, esp. proper names\), recorded from 1904, has been the standard term since the early 1950s. Before that, the standard term was \**onomatology*, which had been in use since the mid-1800s but is now a needless variant. The agent noun *onomastician* superseded \**onomatologist* in the early 1980s.

Current ratio in print \(*onomastics* vs. \**onomatology*\): 13:1
Current ratio in print \(*onomastician* vs. \**onomatologist*\): 8:1
