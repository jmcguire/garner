# olla podrida

olla podrida \(= \[1\] a Spanish-style dish consisting of meat and vegetables stewed together; or \[2\] a hodgepodge\), a Spanish loan dating in English from Shakespeare's time in the late 1500s, has two plurals: *olla podridas* \(standard in BrE\) and *ollas podridas* \(predominant in AmE\). See plurals \(b\); see also loanwords \(a\).

 - Current ratio in print \(*ollas podridas* vs. *olla podridas* in World Englishes\): 6:1
