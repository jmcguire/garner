# optimize

optimize (= to make the best use of) is the word, not *\*optimalize*—e.g.: "Insulin dosage was *optimalized* \[read *optimized*\] by the study physician." Julio Wainstein et al., "The Use of Continuous Insulin Delivery Systems in Severely Insulin-Resistant Patients," 24 *Diabetes Care* 1297 (1 July 2001). See nonwords.

## Language-Change Index

 - *\*optimalize* for *optimize*: Stage 1
 - Current ratio in print (*optimized* vs. \**optimalized*): 6,511:1
