# obstinacy

obstinacy, not *\*obstinance*, is the noun corresponding to *obstinate* \(= stubborn\)—e.g.: "Gundolf informed George that he had chided Rausch for his *obstinance* \[read *obstinacy*\]." Robert E. Norton, *Secret Germany* 419 \(2002\).

## Language-Change Index

*\*obstinance* for *obstinacy*: Stage 1
Current ratio in print \(*obstinacy* vs. \**obstinance*\): 77:1
