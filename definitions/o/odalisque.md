# odalisque

odalisque \(= \[1\] a female slave or concubine in a harem; or \[2\] an exotic, sexy woman, esp. a reclining nude as a theme in a painting\) is the standard spelling. *\*Odalisk* is a variant. The word is pronounced /**oh**-dә-lisk/ or /**od**-ә-lisk/. See spelling \(b\).

Current ratio in print \(*odalisque* vs. \**odalisk*\): 10:1
