# orthodontics

orthodontics; **orthodontia**. The original term for the branch of dentistry dealing with the correction of crooked teeth and similar irregularities was *orthodontia*, a compound made from Greek elements in the mid-1800s. The competing term *orthodontics*, introduced in the first decade of the 1900s, had become the predominant term by 1950.

 - Current ratio in print \(*orthodontics* vs. *orthodontia*\): 7:1
