# ozonation

ozonation (= the act or process of treating or combining a substance with ozone, or of producing ozone) has been the standard noun since the early 1970s. Until that time, the usual noun was \**ozonization*, now a needless variant. Yet the corresponding verb is *ozonize*, not *ozone*.

 - Current ratio in print (*ozonation* vs. \**ozonization*): 31:1
 - Current ratio in print (*ozonized* vs. *ozoned*): 32:1
