# out-of-body experience

out-of-body experience (= the sensation that one is somehow outside one's body, usu. looking down on it from above), a phrase dating from the early 1960s, is so hyphenated. The mistaken wording \**outer body experience* qualifies as a mondegreen.

 - Current ratio in print (*out-of-body experience* vs. \**outer body experience*): 11:1
