# zinc

zinc, vb. \(= to coat with zinc\), vacillates in its inflected forms. The predominant spellings in AmE and BrE alike are the anomalous *zinced* and *zincing* \(pronounced with a /k/ sound\), variants being *\*zincked* and *\*zincking*, as well as the willful misspellings *\*zinked* and *\*zinking*. See -c-.

Current ratio in print \(*zinced* vs. *\*zincked*\): 1.6:1
